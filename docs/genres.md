## Composite Genre Rules

- Supports `Genre A + Genre B` (maximum 2 genres)
- Recommended primary-to-secondary ratio of 7:3
- Main plot follows primary genre logic; secondary genre contributes hooks, rules, and cool-points

Examples:

- `Urban Mind-bender + Rules Horror`
- `Xianxia + System-driven`
