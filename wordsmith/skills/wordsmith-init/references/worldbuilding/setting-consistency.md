# Setting Consistency Check Guide

> **Core Principle**: Settings can be wild, but logic must be self-consistent. Readers can accept "magic exists," but cannot accept "magic in this world works intermittently."

---

## Vietnamese Writing Patterns

Các pattern tiếng Việt áp dụng khi kiểm tra tính nhất quán của setting:

### Giọng xưng hô và tính nhất quán
- Trong cùng một cảnh: KHÔNG chuyển đổi đại từ xưng hô bất thường
- Nếu nhân vật dùng "ta" với vua: phải dùng XUYÊN SUỐT trong cảnh đó
- Ngoại lệ: khi nhân vật cố tình thay đổi — phải có lý do rõ ràng trong narrative

### Show-Don't-Tell cho logic holes
- Thay vì nói "có vấn đề logic": *"Nhưng đợi đã — nếu hắn đi đường này, sao không ai thấy?"*
- Khi phát hiện logic breach: nhân vật phản ứng — suy nghĩ kéo dài với "..."
- Để người đọc nhận ra: mô tả chi tiết bất thường, không giải thích

### Pacing khi sửa consistency
- Nếu phải reveal rule mới: xen vào đoạn descriptive dài, không trong action scene
- Sau khi reveal: cho người đọc thời gian hiểu — câu dài, nhiều giải thích
- Tránh: reveal ngay trước climax, gây confusion

### Trigger words cho inconsistency
- "Nhưng đợi đã..." — dấu hiệu sắp có inconsistency được phát hiện
- "Sao lại..." — confusion của nhân vật
- "Thế nhưng..." — mâu thuẫn giữa expectation và reality

---

Tính nhất quán là yếu tố sống còn trong webnovel tiếng Việt. Pattern từ **Biên niên sử rồng** (BNsr) cho thấy tầm quan trọng của việc giữ logic xuyên suốt, đặc biệt trong các cảnh chiến đấu và hệ thống sức mạnh. **Useless hero from another world** (UH) thể hiện cách status card cần được dùng nhất quán để tránh inflation.

## 1. Three Pillars of Consistency

### Physics Consistency
Once the world's basic rules are established, they cannot be casually broken.

#### Positive Example ✅
```
Setting: Cultivators flying consumes spiritual power
→ Chapter 100: Protagonist's spiritual power exhausted, falls from sky
→ Logic consistent, reader accepts
```

#### Negative Example ❌
```
Setting: Mortals cannot cultivate, need to awaken spirit roots
→ Chapter 50: Protagonist is mortal, successfully cultivates through hard work
→ Contradiction, reader questions: "What's the point of spirit roots then?"

Fix options:
Option A: Protagonist actually has recessive spirit root (awakens later)
Option B: Protagonist obtains cheat item (e.g., Spirit Devouring Pearl, can steal others' spirit roots)
```

### Power System Consistency
Level suppression and combat power comparisons must be consistent throughout.

#### Common Problems
| Problem Type | Case | Fix |
|--------------|------|-----|
| **Power system collapse** | Chapter 10 says "Golden Core strong one destroys city with one palm," Chapter 100 Golden Core can only explode one house | Introduce new dimension (city has protective array) or admit earlier exaggeration (flashback fix) |
| **Unlimited cross-rank** | Protagonist crosses major realm from Qi Condensation to Golden Core, later Qi Condensation layer 10 can't even beat Foundation Building | Set cross-rank conditions (requires divine weapon/formation/sneak attack, cannot be normalized) |
| **Realm invalid** | Setting says higher realm means longer lifespan, but some senior lived for 100,000 years yet still Nascent Soul | Explain as special constitution/possession/time dilation difference |

### Social Ecology Consistency
World's economy, politics, and culture must conform to basic logic.

#### Consistency Checklist
- [ ] **Currency system**: Is spiritual stone/gold coin purchasing power stable? (Don't have the same pill cost 1000 spiritual stones in Chapter 10 and only 10 spiritual stones in Chapter 50)
- [ ] **Faction balance**: Why can righteous and evil factions coexist for a thousand years? (Must have balancing factors: supreme experts' pact, common enemy, resource complementarity)
- [ ] **Population ratio**: Is the ratio of cultivators to mortals reasonable? (If everyone can cultivate, who's going to farm?)

## 2. Common Logic Loopholes and Fixes

### Loophole 1: "Antagonist IQ suddenly drops"
**Manifestation**: Early on, the antagonist is deeply calculating, but when it's time for the protagonist to face-slap, they suddenly become stupid.

**Fix options**:
- **Preset trap**: Antagonist seems foolish, actually fishing (counterplot).
- **Information gap**: Antagonist doesn't know protagonist has broken through/obtained divine weapon, so underestimates.
- **Internal split**: Antagonist faction has traitors, deliberately leaking intelligence.

### Loophole 2: "Setting distorted to serve plot"
**Manifestation**:
- Setting: Using forbidden technique burns lifespan
- Plot: Protagonist used forbidden technique ten times, no side effects
- Reader: "What about the lifespan burning?"

**Fix options**:
- **Plant foreshadowing early**: Protagonist obtained "immortal body" type cheat, can be immune to side effects.
- **Later repayment**: Lifespan secretly burning, later suddenly triggers crisis (triggers new plot).
- **Equivalent exchange**: Each time using forbidden technique, explicitly show the price protagonist pays (e.g., hair turns white, realm drops).

### Loophole 3: "Timeline chaos"
**Manifestation**:
- Chapter 30: "This secret realm only opens during the blood moon night once a century, last opened 50 years ago"
- Chapter 80: "50 years ago on blood moon night, that senior entered the secret realm and disappeared"
- Reader: "So which time is this blood moon? Timeline doesn't match!"

**Fix options**:
- **Build timeline document**: Record time points of all major events (see template below).
- **Unified era system**: Use "Cultivation Calendar Year XXX" or "Year the protagonist turned 15" as anchor points.

## 3. Setting Consistency Check Templates

### World Rules Self-Check Table
```markdown
## Core Laws (Iron rules, cannot be violated)
1. **Death is irreversible**: Dead cannot be resurrected (unless... special conditions)
2. **Realm suppression**: Lower realm cannot harm higher realm (unless... cheat/formation)
3. **Energy conservation**: Using power must have consumption (spiritual power/stamina/lifespan)

## Variable Laws (Can be broken with prerequisites)
1. **Flight restriction**: Cannot fly below Foundation Building
   - Exception: Riding flying boat/being carried by expert
2. **Lifespan cap**: Golden Core lives 500 years
   - Exception: Taking lifespan-extending pills/special constitution

## Current Version
v1.2 (Update version number each time settings are modified, record reason for change)
```

### Power System Self-Check Table
```markdown
## Realm Combat Power Comparison Table
| Realm | Representative Battle Achievements | Lifespan | Spiritual Power Total |
|-------|-----------------------------------|----------|----------------------|
| Qi Condensation | Bare hands crush stone | 100 years | 100 |
| Foundation Building | Sword flying, one person destroys hundred-person army | 200 years | 1,000 |
| Golden Core | Palm destroys small town, can briefly change terrain | 500 years | 10,000 |

## Cross-Realm Rules
- **Maximum span**: Protagonist can cross 1 major realm (Qi Condensation kills Foundation Building)
```

## 4. Dynamic Maintenance: Settings File Structure

Projects generated by `/wordsmith-init` should contain the following files:

```
Settings/
├── Worldbuilding Settings.md        # Physics laws, geography, history
├── Power System Settings.md          # Realms, techniques, combat power comparison table
├── Faction Settings.md              # Major sects/families/faction relationships
├── Character Cards/               # One file per important character
│   ├── Protagonist-Lin Tian.md
│   ├── Antagonist-Ye Liangchen.md
│   └── Master-Yun Xianzi.md
├── Timeline.md                     # Main + sub-timelines
└── Settings Change Log.md         # Record each reason for modifying settings
```

### Settings Change Log Template
```markdown
## v1.0 → v1.1 Change Record
**Date**: 2025-01-01
**Change content**:
- Modified: Golden Core lifespan changed from 300 years to 500 years
- Reason: Conflicts with description in Chapter 50 "that Golden Core senior lived 400 years"
- Scope of impact: Need to review all Golden Core lifespan descriptions in first 30 chapters

## v1.1 → v1.2 Change Record
**Date**: 2025-01-10
**Change content**:
- Added: Introduced "Law Comprehension" dimension
- Reason: Avoid later numerical bloat (can't always rely on spiritual power total for victory)
- Scope of impact: From Nascent Soul stage onward, law comprehension becomes new combat power dimension
```

## 5. Real-Time Check Workflow (During Writing)

### Before writing each new chapter
1. **Review settings**: Confirm the settings involved in this chapter (realm/faction/time)
2. **Search history chapters**: Use keywords to search if similar settings were written before
3. **Mark contradictions**: If inconsistencies found, immediately record in `Settings Change Log.md`

### After completing each major storyline
1. **Update timeline**: Record the time point when the event occurred
2. **Update combat power table**: If new realms/experts appear, add to comparison table
3. **Check side effects**: If there are follow-up cost displays for forbidden techniques/cheat used by protagonist

---

## Consistency Check Checklist

After generating chapters with `/wordsmith-write`, must pass the following checks:
- [ ] **Realm combat power**: Does the battle scene in this chapter conform to power system settings?
- [ ] **Timeline logic**: Does the time of events contradict previous ones?
- [ ] **Character settings**: Do characters' actions match their personality/stance?
- [ ] **Item restrictions**: Do divine weapons/pills/talismans usage exceed setting scope?
- [ ] **Faction relationships**: Do faction interactions in this chapter conform to established relationship network?

## Appendix: Common Reader Question Case Library

### Case 1: "Why doesn't protagonist use XXX method?"
- **Problem**: Protagonist clearly has a simpler solution, yet insists on taking the complex route.
- **Reason**: Author forgot abilities/items protagonist obtained earlier.
- **Prevention**: Maintain a "protagonist current abilities list," browse before each writing session.

### Case 2: "This antagonist is alive again?"
- **Problem**: Antagonist who was clearly written dead appears again, without reasonable explanation.
- **Reason**: Wanted antagonist to continue driving plot, but didn't plant foreshadowing in advance.
- **Fix**: Add explanation (clone/fake death/rescued), mark in settings as "to be filled."

### Case 3: "The world's economy collapsed"
- **Problem**: Spiritual stone purchasing power differs a hundredfold before and after.
- **Reason**: No currency anchor established (e.g., 1 jin spirit rice = 10 spiritual stones).
- **Prevention**: Set "price reference table," fix core product prices.

## 6. Vietnamese Consistency Patterns (Pattern từ webnovel Việt)

Trong webnovel tiếng Việt, sự nhất quán phải được xây dựng theo pattern từ **Biên niên sử rồng** (BNsr) và **Useless hero from another world** (UH).

### 6.1 Vietnamese Character Behavior Consistency

**Đại từ xưng hô nhất quán:**
| Tình huống | Nhân vật chính | Nhân vật phụ |
|------------|----------------|--------------|
| Thân thiết | mày/tao | mày/tao |
| Lạ | tôi/ngài | ngài/tôi |
| Căng thẳng | thằng khốn | thằng nào |

**Pattern từ BNsr:**
- Jack và Rosa dùng "tao-mày" với nhau
- Nhưng với người lạ: "tôi-ngài"
- Khi giận: chuyển sang "thằng chó", "con nhóc"

**Pattern từ UH:**
- Tanaka nội tâm: 'Ta thật vô dụng' (tự xưng)
- Khi đối đầu: "Thằng chó đó mà dám..."

### 6.2 Vietnamese Power Consistency Check

**Status Card System (từ UH):**
```
Mana: X/Y
Sức khỏe: X
Phòng thủ: X
Nhanh nhẹn: X
Dẫn suất ma lực: X
```

**Cross-realm rules cho Vietnamese context:**
- 5% chance hồi phục khi level up
- Skill Độc nhất (Độc nhất) có hiệu ứng đặc biệt
- "Cơ Địa Của Kẻ Vô Dụng" giảm 80% exp cần thiết

### 6.3 Vietnamese Timeline Consistency

**Pattern từ BNsr:**
- "Sáu năm trước..." cho flashback
- "Buổi sớm... chiều nào cũng buồn tẻ thế thôi" cho daily routine
- "Bão. Tuyết. Cái lạnh cắt da cắt thịt." cho atmosphere setup

**Pattern từ UH:**
- "—0o0—" cho scene transitions
- Chapter opening với thẻ trạng thái
- Cliffhanger: "Bảy ngày…"

### 6.4 Vietnamese Sentence Rhythm Consistency

**Action scenes (BNsr):**
- Câu cực ngắn: "Ầm!" "Bắn!" "Chết tiệt!"
- Không dấu phẩy, động từ mạnh

**Emotional scenes (BNsr):**
- Câu dài, descriptive: "Lâu đài Chyrse một buổi chiều lặng tuyết buồn tẻ lãng mạn."
- "Có một nụ cười làm tuyết tan."

**System description (UH):**
- Thẻ trạng thái hiển thị rõ ràng
- Inner monologue với nhận xét mỉa mai

### 6.5 Vietnamese Consistency Checklist

- [ ] **Xưng hô**: Nhân vật dùng đúng ngôi xưng theo mối quan hệ
- [ ] **Chỉ số**: Status card và power system nhất quán xuyên suốt
- [ ] **Timeline**: Không contradict với "Sáu năm trước..." hoặc event cũ
- [ ] **Nhịp điệu**: Action ngắn, emotional dài, system descriptive
- [ ] **Cảm xúc**: Biểu hiện cụ thể (run bần bật, nghiến răng) thay vì nói trực tiếp
