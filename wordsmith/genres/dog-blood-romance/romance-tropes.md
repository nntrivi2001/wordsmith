# Romance Tropes

> **Core Principle**: Tropes are tools—it's how you wield them that matters. Readers don't crave "new," they crave "satisfying." Classic tropes persist because they precisely hit readers' emotional pain points.

---

## Vietnamese Romance Writing Patterns

### Pronoun Usage in Romance Scenes (Vietnamese)

| Person | Pronoun | Context |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Third male | hắn | Action, provoking opponent |
| Peer/friend | mày | Close dialogue |
| Group | tụi mày | Calling multiple people |
| Speaker's group | bọn tao | Main character's group |
| Archaic/ceremonial | ta | Noble characters |
| Respectful | ngài | With authority figures |
| Formal | tôi | Serious context |

**Romance-specific patterns:**
- Main characters (Rosa, Jack) use "tao-mày" with each other but "tôi-ngài" with strangers
- Rivals/love interests often use "hắn" when referring to romantic competitor
- Emotional tension increases when pronouns shift (mày → thằng khốn)

### Colloquial vs Literary in Romance (Vietnamese)

| Colloquial | Literary | Note |
|------------|----------|------|
| đi đường | đường đi | Same meaning, different order |
| làm gì | làm chi | Asking purpose |
| sao | như thế nào | Asking manner |

**Pattern:**
- Dialogue uses colloquial language
- Inner monologue and scene description use literary language
- Romance scenes blend both for emotional depth

### Sentence Structure in Romance Scenes (Vietnamese)

**Dialogue - Short and direct:**
```
"JACKKKK!!!!"
"Thôi không! Khok!"
```

**Narration - Longer, descriptive:**
```
"Jack nhìn cô với ánh mắt hấp háy cười.

-Em có nghĩ gì đâu.

-Chỉ ngủ gật thôi hả? - giờ thì anh chàng đang phá lên nắc nẻ trước điệu bộ gãi đầu gãi tai của cô."
```

### Show-Don't-Tell in Romance (Vietnamese)

**Instead of telling:**
"Cô ấy rất giận."

**Write (Vietnamese):**
"Rosa nghiến răng, nắm chặt tay lại đến trắng bóc. Mắt cô nổ đom đóm."

**Trigger words for emotion in romance:**
| Emotion | Vietnamese Expression |
|---------|----------------------|
| Anger | nghiến răng, nổ gân, mặt đỏ |
| Fear | run bần bật, mặt tái mét |
| Sadness | nước mắt lã chã, cười méo, thở dài |
| Surprise | tròn mắt, há hốc mồm |
| Touched | nghẹn bứ, run lên, khóc nức nở |

## 1. Mười Trope Tình Yêu Cổ Điển Thường Gặp

### Trope 1: Giám Đốc Chiếm Hữu (Dominant CEO)

**Core Formula**:
```
Dominant CEO + Cinderella heroine = Power gap + status gap → Collision → Conquest → Sweetness
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Male Lead Setting**: Young success (28-35), billionaire, cold outside/warm inside, obsessive cleaner/compulsive
- **Heroine Setting**: Ordinary girl, kind and strong, financial difficulties, self-respect
- **Key Plot Points**:
  1. Accidental meeting (car crash/rescue/misunderstanding)
  2. Contract/agreement (pretend girlfriend/surrogate/deal)
  3. Domineering pursuit (blocking door/wall slam/forced kiss)
  4. Pain and heartbreak (misunderstanding/separation/disease)
  5. Sweet ending (confession/wedding/children)

**Example Scene**:
```markdown
【Domineering Declaration】
" Woman, you've successfully caught my attention."
CEO Lu stared at her with dangerous eyes.

【Wall Slam Scene】
He braced one hand against the wall, trapping her in his arms.
"Think of running? Too late."
```

**Classic Variations**:
- **Cold-face CEO** - Innocent sweet secretary
- **Dark CEO** × Cold career woman
- **Yandere CEO** × Healing heroine

---

### Trope 2: Thay Thế (Substitute)

**Core Formula**:
```
Heroine is white moonlight substitute → Male lead abuses heroine → Heroine leaves → Male lead chases wife to cremation
```

**Required Elements**:
- **White Moonlight**: Perfect goddess in male lead's heart (usually dead/missing)
- **Heroine**: Looks like white moonlight, used as substitute
- **Pain Point Design**:
  1. Male lead uses heroine to vent longing for white moonlight
  2. Male lead mentions white moonlight in front of heroine
  3. Male lead makes heroine wear white moonlight's clothes
  4. White moonlight returns, heroine abandoned

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Heartbreak Dialogue】
"You think I married you because I love you? No, you're just her shadow."
He said coldly, photo of white moonlight clutched in his hand.

【Chasing Wife to Cremation】
"I was wrong, please come back..."
He knelt in the rain, but she walked away without looking back.
"The person you love isn't me, and I don't love you either."
```

**Reversal Techniques**:
- **Heroine darkens**: From enduring to revenge
- **Truth revealed**: White moonlight is actually a green tea/bad woman
- **Identity reversal**: Heroine is the real white moonlight

---

### Trope 3: Kịch Gia Đình Phú Quý (Wealthy Family Drama)

**Core Formula**:
```
Wealthy family power struggles + romance = Power games + emotional entanglement
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Family Background**: Century-old wealthy empire/business empire
- **Power Struggles**: Inheritance rights/stock rights
- **Emotional Barriers**: Class mismatch/family opposition/ancient rivalry
- **Antagonist Roles**: Evil elders/white lotus rival/dark-hearted brothers

**Classic Plots**:
```markdown
【Family Opposition】
"She is not worthy of our Lu family! Unless you step over my corpse!"
Lu grandmother slammed the table and roared.

【Rivalry Resolved】
"For you, I would abandon the entire Lu Group."
He announced in front of everyone, gasps rippled through the crowd.
```

**Sub-tropes**:
- **Arranged Marriage**: Family marriage → fake becomes real → true love
- **Revenge**: Heroine approaches male lead for revenge → falls in love
- **Power Struggle**: Male lead claims power + heroine assists → joint rule

---

### Trope 4: Kết Hôn Trước Yêu Sau (Marriage First, Love Later)

**Core Formula**:
```
Flash marriage (contract/agreement) - Daily time together - gradual feelings - pain point - true love
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Flash Marriage Reasons**:
  - Family pressure
  - Mutual benefit (inheritance/project)
  - Revenge on ex
  - Accidental pregnancy
- **Cohabitation Mode**: Separate rooms → gradual intimacy → breakthrough
- **Emotional Turning Point**: Pretending not to care → jealous → possessiveness erupts

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Contract Terms】
"Clause 1: Maintain distance, sleep separately."
"Clause 2: Divorce after one year, mutual non-interference."
"Clause 3..."
Half a year later—
"Wife, shouldn't clause 1 be considered void?"
He held her and wouldn't let go.

【Jealousy Scene】
Seeing her talk to another man, he walked over with a dark face.
"Mr. Chen, we should go home."
His tone was dangerous.
```

---

### Trope 5: Tình Yêu Đau Đớn (Tortured Love)

**Core Formula**:
```
Love → misunderstanding/betrayal → heartbreak separation → truth revealed → HE/BE
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Pain Point Sources**:
  - Misunderstanding (third party stirring/trickery)
  - Betrayal (surface betrayal actually protection)
  - Injury (abortion/divorce/imprisonment)
  - Illness (cancer/amnesia/disability)
- **Pain Intensity**:
  - Light pain: Misunderstanding → explanation → makeup
  - Medium pain: Breakup → mutual pain → reunion
  - Heavy pain: Life-and-death separation → endless regret

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Misunderstanding Heartbreak】
"You personally killed our child..."
Tears streamed down her face, eyes dead.
He wanted to explain, but all evidence pointed to him.

【Truth Revealed】
"I'm sorry, it was all my fault..."
He knelt by her hospital bed, tears like rain.
But she was already in a coma.
```

**BE vs HE**:
- **BE (Bitter Ending)**: Heroine dies/amnesia/leaves, male lead mourns forever
- **HE (Happy Ending)**: Misunderstanding cleared, makeup, marriage and children

---

### Trope 6: Tái Sinh Trả Thù (Reborn Revenge)

**Core Formula**:
```
Die cruelly in past life - Reborn return to the past - Revenge on scumbag/green tea - Meets true love - Counterattack
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Past Life Experience**: Betrayed by scumbag/frames by girlfriend/rich family abuse/die tragically
- **Rebirth Advantage**: Knows future/remembers past life/premonition
- **Revenge Targets**: Scumbag ex-husband/white lotus sister/vicious mother-in-law
- **True Mr. Right**: Good man ignored in past life/new domineering CEO appears

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Rebirth Awakening】
Opening her eyes, she discovered she returned ten years ago.
"This life, I absolutely won't repeat the same mistakes!"

【Revenge Face-Slapping】
"Think you can trick me? I've seen through all your tricks."
She coldly smiled and produced evidence, scumbag's face turned pale.

【Meeting True Love】
He silently protected her in past life, but she ignored it.
This life, she proactively walked to him: "Shall we start over, okay?"
```

---

### Trope 7: Giới Giải Trí (Entertainment Industry)

**Core Formula**:
```
Small transparent heroine + top actor/financial backer male lead = Casting couch rumors - Official announcement - Face-slap haters
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Heroine Setting**: Eighteenth-tier actress/newcomer/substitute
- **Male Lead Setting**: Top actor/director/entertainment company president
- **Plot Design**:
  1. Heroine faces casting couch/cyberbullying
  2. Male lead protects majestically
  3. Heroine relies on talent to counterattack
  4. Public relationship, whole internet blesses

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Casting Couch Rumors】
#Certain actress trades sleep for advancement#
Trending exploded.

【Majestic Wife Protection】
Top actor personally responds:
"She's my wife. Any objections?"
Photo: marriage certificate.
Whole internet explodes.

【Talent Face-Slapping】
Heroine wins best actress with acting, haters shut up.
"Acting skill is the real weapon."
```

---

### Trope 8: Ngọt Ngào Thuần Túy (Pure Fluff No Pain)

**Core Formula**:
```
Male lead pampers heroine + heroine pampered = Full sweetness + zero pain points
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Male Lead Attribute**: Pampering maniac/wifey slave/daughter slave
- **Heroine Attribute**: Cute/soft and cute/healing type
- **Sweet Point Design**:
  - Different pampering methods daily
  - Male lead does silly things for heroine
  - Public displays of affection
  - Sweet daily life

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Daily Pampering】
"Wife's words are always right."
"What wife wants, I get."
"Wife's happiness matters most."

【Public Displays of Affection】
Reporter: "Mr. Lu, what's the secret to success?"
Lu CEO: "Listen to wife."
All present: "..."
```

**Sweet Types**:
- **Pampering Type**: Male lead unconditionally pampers heroine
- **Flirting Type**: Domineering CEO flirts with heroine
- **Reversal Charm**: Cold outside, warm inside, only gentle to heroine

---

### Trope 9: Tình Yêu Nam Nhỏ Hơn (Younger Man Romance)

**Core Formula**:
```
Mature heroine + young male lead = age-gap romance - chase wife - break through worldly conventions
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Age Gap**: 3-10 years
- **Heroine Setting**: Mature and independent/successful career/divorced/widowed
- **Male Lead Setting**: Sunny/persistent/pampering maniac
- **Barriers**: Social prejudice/heroine's insecurity/family opposition

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Pursuit】
"Sister, I like you."
The young man said seriously.
"You're still young..."
"I've already come of age, and age is not a problem."

【Breaking Prejudices】
"What I love is her as a person, not her age."
He announced publicly, defying social eyes.
```

---

### Trope 10: Hôn Nhân Bí Mật (Secret Marriage)

**Core Formula**:
```
Married but hide identity → Various coincidences almost expose → Official announcement → Whole internet shocked
```

**Required Elements**:
- **Secret Marriage Reasons**:
  - Family opposition
  - Career considerations (celebrity)
  - Low-key (rich person)
- **Narrow Escapes**:
  - Photographed together
  - Wedding ring exposed
  - Child calls mom
- **Official Announcement Methods**:
  - Accidental exposure
  - Proactive disclosure
  - Forced hand

**Classic Scenes**:
```markdown
【Narrow Escape】
Reporter: "Miss Lin, that wedding ring on your hand..."
Heroine: "Oh, this is... a prop!"

【Shocking Official Announcement】
Top actor posts on Weibo:
"Let me introduce, my wife @Miss Lin"
Photo: marriage certificate + family photo
Trending explodes.
```

## 2. Công Thức Kết Hợp Trope

### Công Thức 1: CEO + Thay Thế + Đuổi Vợ
```markdown
Domineering CEO uses heroine as substitute → Abuses heroine → Heroine leaves → CEO chases wife
```
**Iconic Work**: 《Substitute Bride》type

---

### Công Thức 2: Tái Sinh + Trả Thù + Tình Yêu Đích Thực
```markdown
Heroine reborn → Stays away from scumbag → Meets true love CEO → Revenge succeeds → HE
```
**Iconic Work**: 《Reborn: Luxurious Family Favor》type

---

### Công Thức 3: Kết Hôn Trước + Hôn Nhân Bí Mật + Ngọt
```markdown
Flash marriage → Secret marriage → Daily sweetness → Official announcement → Whole internet envies
```
**Iconic Work**: 《Secret Marriage Sweet Wife》type

---

### Công Thức 4: Giải Trí + Gia Đình Phú Quý + Tát Mặt
```markdown
Small actress × Rich family heir → Trapped by blackmail → Truth revealed → Face-slap haters
```
**Iconic Work**: 《The Star's Secret Husband》type

### Công Thức 5: Melodrama + Hiểu Lầm + Hối Hận
```markdown
Love → Misunderstanding breakup → Heroine leaves/fakes death → Male lead regrets → HE/BE
```
**Iconic Work**: 《Silent Eternal Melody》type

## 4. Danh Sách Tự Kiểm Tra Trope

Sau khi viết trope, kiểm tra từng mục:
- [ ] **Công thức cốt lõi rõ ràng**: Độc giả có thể ngay lập tức nhận ra trope nào không?
- [ ] **Yếu tố bắt buộc đầy đủ**: Tất cả yếu tố chính của trope này có mặt không?
- [ ] **Nhịp cảm xúc hợp lý**: Tỷ lệ ngọt-đau có phù hợp không? (Ngọt 7:3, melodrama 3:7)
- [ ] **Có điểm đổi mới**: Có sáng tạo mới trên nền tảng cổ điển không?
- [ ] **Điểm nhấn đủ**: Ít nhất 1 điểm ngọt/tát mặt/đấu tranh mỗi 5000 ký tự?
- [ ] **Tránh cliche**: Đã tránh những trope độc giả ghét nhất chưa?

## Phụ Lục: Ví Dụ Tiêu Cực

### Lỗi 1: Chồng Trope, Logic Rối
```markdown
Nhân vật nữ đồng thời là CEO, thay thế, tái sinh, VÀ công chúa thật...
```
**Vấn Đề**: Quá nhiều trope, độc giả bị lú.

**Cải thiện**: Tập trung 1-2 trope chính, các trope khác là phụ.

---

### Lỗi 2: Trope Clichéd, Không Ý Tưởng Mới
```markdown
Một CEO tát tường khác, một " Woman, you've successfully caught my attention" khác...
```
**Vấn Đề**: Năm 2025, những clichéd này đã quá cũ.

**Cải thiện**: Thêm yếu tố mới (hài hước, anti-trope) trên nền tảng cổ điển.

---

### Lỗi 3: Trope Vì Trope
```markdown
Hiểu lầm có thể giải thích rõ ràng, cố tình kéo dài mười chương.
Tỏ tình có thể xảy ra trực tiếp, tạo tình huống hợp đồng không cần thiết.
```
**Vấn Đề**: Gượng ép, padding chỉ để dùng trope.

**Cải thiện**: Trope phục vụ cốt truyện, không ngược lại.

## Phân Tích Trường Hợp Cổ Điển

### Trường Hợp 1: 《Silent Eternal Melody》
**Trope**: Tình yêu đau đớn + Chờ đợi kiên nhẫn
**Điểm Thành Công**:
- Cảm giác hối hận "nếu lúc đó..."
- Nhân vật nam chờ bảy năm
- Hiểu lầm có chiều sâu, không drama máu

---

### Trường Hợp 2: 《Shanshan Is Coming to Eat》
**Trope**: CEO chiếm hữu + Ngọt ngào thuần túy
**Điểm Thành Công**:
- Nhân vật nam dễ thương (tsundere + chiều chuộng)
- Nhân vật nữ không ngây thơ ngọt, có ý kiến riêng
- Chi tiết đời thường ngọt ngào

---

### Trường Hợp 3: 《A Slight Smile is Quite Captivating》
**Trope**: Campus + Game + Ngọt ngào chiều chuộng
**Điểm Thành Công**:
- Thiết kế nhân vật thiên tài kép
- Yếu tố game mới lạ
- Ngọt hoàn toàn, không drama máu

## Tóm Tắt

Trope không phải từ xấu — đó là "pattern cốt truyện mà độc giả mong đợi." Chìa khóa để sử dụng trope tốt:
1. **Nắm vững cổ điển**: Biết độc giả thích gì
2. **Đổi mới vừa phải**: Thêm mới lạ trên nền cổ điển
3. **Phục vụ cốt truyện**: Trope phục vụ cốt truyện, không ngược lại
4. **Kiểm soát nhịp**: Tỷ lệ ngọt-đau phải hợp lý
5. **Tránh gượng ép**: Trope phải tự nhiên, không gượng ép
