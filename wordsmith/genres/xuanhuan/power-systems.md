# Power Systems Design

> **Core Principle**: The power system is the skeleton of xuanhuan fiction. A great power system = clear tier divisions + logical growth paths + ample room for imagination.

---

## Vietnamese Writing Patterns

### From Ta Dung Nhin Vo Lam Toang & Sources:
- Dialogue format: "Content" + action tag
- Inner thoughts: third-person narrative without quotes
- Scene breaks: *** major, —0o0— minor
- Pronouns: mày/tao (close), tôi/ngài (formal), hắn (antagonist)
- First-person internal: cậu/mình

### 8 Error Types to Avoid:
1. Units: mét/cm/km/kg (NOT trượng/dặm/tấc/thốn)
2. Vocabulary: context-appropriate
3. Punctuation: — single (NOT ——)
4. Sentence: complete (subject + predicate)
5. Spelling: natural Vietnamese
6. Connectors: và/nhưng/nên/vì/rồi/thì
7. Subject: explicit in descriptions
8. Natural rhythm

### Genre-Specific Patterns:

#### Xuanhuan/Cultivation:
- Use mét/cm/km for distances (NOT trượng)
- Power levels: Trúc Cơ, Kim Đan, Nguyên Anh
- Dragon descriptions from Bien Nien Su Rong style

#### Isekai/Gaming:
- Status card format with "Keng!" notifications
- Combat: short → explanation → short punch
- Level-up pacing with 5% recovery chance

#### Romance/Fire Strand:
- Show-don't-tell emotion markers
- Building slow, conflict before resolution
- Body language over telling emotions

#### Rules-Mystery:
- Clue placement patterns
- Revelation timing
- Suspense building

## 1. The Four Foundation Elements of a Power System

### Element 1: Energy Essence (What)
**Definition**: What is the source of power in this world?

**Common Types**:
| Type | Energy Name | Representative Works | Characteristics |
|------|-------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| **Spiritual Qi Cultivation** | Spiritual qi / True qi / Primal force | *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality* | Heavenly and earthly spiritual qi, absorbable and refinable |
| **Elemental Magic** | Mana / Elemental force | *Soul Land* | Fire / Water / Wind / Earth elements |
| **Martial Will** | Qi-blood / Martial intent | *Wu Dong Qian Kun* | Physical strength + willpower |
| **Ability Gene** | Gene energy / Superpower | *Swallowed Star* | Gene evolution, sci-fi flavored |
| **Divine Faith** | Faith force / Divine power | *Coiling Dragon* | Concentrated from believers' faith |
| **Hybrid System** | Multiple energies coexist | *Perfect World* | Spiritual qi + Laws + Bloodlines |

**Design Notes**:
```
✅ The energy essence should be unique (avoid "yet another spiritual qi cultivation system")
✅ How to acquire energy must be clear (absorb / convert / cultivate)
✅ Energy characteristics should be differentiated (offensive / defensive / support)
```

---

### Element 2: Realm Grades (How High)
**Definition**: The ranking standard from weak to strong.

**Classic Nine-Tier System**:
```
Tier 1 (Mortal Stage):    Qi Condensation / Apprentice / Awakening
Tier 2 (Entry Stage):     Foundation Building / Mage / Warrior
Tier 3 (Advancement):     Core Formation / Grand Mage / Master
Tier 4 (Elite Stage):     Nascent Soul / Archmage / Grand Master
Tier 5 (Peak Stage):      Deity Transformation / Holy Mage / Martial Saint
Tier 6 (Legend Stage):    Void Refinement / Legendary Mage / Martial Emperor
Tier 7 (Mythic Stage):    Integration / Demigod / Martial God
Tier 8 (Near-Divine):     Grand Ascension / Quasi-God / God King
Tier 9 (Divine):          Tribulation Crossing / True God / Supreme
```

**Naming Conventions**:
- **Cultivation style**: Qi Condensation → Foundation Building → Core Formation → Nascent Soul → Deity Transformation → Void Refinement → Integration → Grand Ascension → Tribulation Crossing
- **Magic style**: Apprentice → Mage → Grand Mage → Archmage → Holy Mage → Legendary → Demigod → True God
- **Martial style**: Martial Pupil → Martial Practitioner → Martial Artist → Master → Grand Master → Martial Saint → Martial Emperor → Martial God → Supreme
- **Ability style**: Awakening → Evolution → Mutation → Transcendence → Legend → Myth → Eternal

**Recommended Realm Count**:
- **Short novels (under 300k characters)**: 5–7 major realms
- **Medium novels (300k–1M characters)**: 7–9 major realms
- **Long novels (1M+ characters)**: 9–12 major realms, can span multiple worlds

---

### Element 3: Ability Expression (What Can Do)
**Definition**: What can a cultivator do at each realm? What abilities do they have?

**Ability Progression Example**:
```markdown
## Realm Ability Reference Table

| Realm | Movement | Attack Range | Destructive Power | Lifespan | Special Ability |
|-------|----------|-------------|-------------------|----------|-----------------|
| **Qi Condensation** | Run on ground | 10 meters | Shatter rocks | 100 years | None |
| **Foundation Building** | Short-range flight | 50 meters | Break walls | 200 years | Simple spells |
| **Core Formation** | Long-range flight | 100 meters | Destroy buildings | 500 years | Spiritual sense detection |
| **Nascent Soul** | Soar freely | 1 km | Level a small town | 1,000 years | Nascent Soul clone |
| **Deity Transformation** | Instant teleport | 10 km | Destroy a city | 2,000 years | Initial law comprehension |
| **Void Refinement** | Space traversal | 100 km | Destroy a nation | 5,000 years | Law control |
| **Integration** | Cross-realm transport | 1,000 km | Shatter a continent | 10,000 years | Law fusion |
| **Grand Ascension** | Unrestricted travel | Unlimited | Shatter stars | 100,000 years | Create laws |
| **Tribulation Crossing** | Space-time mastery | Unlimited | Destroy star domains | Immortal | Create heaven and earth |
```

**Design Notes**:
```
✅ Each realm must have a "qualitative leap" (not just "stronger")
✅ Abilities must be visual (readers can picture the scene)
✅ Avoid "power creep" too early (realm 3 destroys heaven and earth)
```

---

### Element 4: Breakthrough Conditions (How to Advance)
**Definition**: How do you break through from a lower realm to a higher one?

**Common Breakthrough Methods**:
1. **Resource Accumulation**: Requires pills / spirit stones / rare treasures
2. **Epiphany**: Requires comprehending laws / the essence of the Dao
3. **Trial Type**: Must survive heavenly tribulation / trial grounds / life-or-death battle
4. **Inheritance Type**: Must obtain cultivation technique / bloodline / legacy
5. **Hybrid Type**: Combination of multiple methods above

**Example (Core Formation → Nascent Soul)**:
```markdown
## Breakthrough Checklist (All Required)
1. **Cultivation Requirement**: Core Formation peak (spiritual power saturated)
2. **Resource Requirement**: Soul-Breaking Pill × 1, Spirit Stones × 100,000
3. **Comprehension Requirement**: Comprehend one type of law (Fire/Water/Wind/Earth/Lightning, etc.)
4. **Tribulation Trial**: Survive the Three-Nine Lightning Tribulation (27 lightning bolts)
5. **Success Rate**: 10–30% (higher talent = higher success rate)

## Breakthrough Process
```
Step 1: Take Soul-Breaking Pill, spiritual power surges
Step 2: The core begins to fracture, nurturing the Nascent Soul
Step 3: Heavenly tribulation descends, lightning baptism
Step 4: Nascent Soul takes shape, bursting from the core
Step 5: Breakthrough succeeds, realm stabilizes
```

## Consequences of Failed Breakthrough
- Minor: Realm regression (Core Formation late stage → Core Formation early stage)
- Severe: Core shatters, becoming a cripple
- Extreme: Struck dead by the tribulation lightning
```

## 2. Three Advanced Power System Designs

### Advanced Design 1: Sub-Realm Divisions
**Purpose**: Let readers feel "continuous progress."

**Division Methods**:
```
Each major realm divided into: Early Stage → Middle Stage → Late Stage → Peak
Or: Layer 1 → Layer 2 → ... → Layer 9
```

**Example**:
```
Core Formation = Core Formation Early + Core Formation Middle + Core Formation Late + Core Formation Peak
There is also a power gap between sub-realms (approximately 1.5–2x)
```

**Word Count Allocation**:
- Major realm breakthrough: every 50,000–100,000 characters
- Sub-realm breakthrough: every 10,000–20,000 characters

---

### Advanced Design 2: Combat Power Multiplier System
**Purpose**: Justify the protagonist's ability to "challenge above their level."

**Common Multiplier Designs**:
| Multiplier Factor | Multiplier | Example |
|------------------|------------|---------|
| **Technique Grade** | 1–10x | Heaven-grade technique vs. Common technique |
| **Combat Skill Grade** | 1–5x | Ultimate art vs. Ordinary move |
| **Artifact Grade** | 1–3x | Divine weapon vs. Common weapon |
| **Bloodline Talent** | 1–5x | Divine beast bloodline vs. Mortal bloodline |
| **Secret Technique Burst** | 2–10x | Burning vital blood / Forbidden technique |
| **Formation Bonus** | 1–10x | Killing formation |

**Calculation Formula**:
```
Actual Combat Power = Base Combat Power × Technique Multiplier × Skill Multiplier × Artifact Multiplier × Bloodline Multiplier

Example:
- Ye Liangchen (Core Formation Early): 100 CP (base) × 1.5 (Mystic-grade technique) = 150 CP
- Lin Tian (Foundation Building Peak): 50 CP (base) × 3 (Heaven-grade technique) × 2 (Ultimate art) × 2 (Divine weapon) = 600 CP
→ Lin Tian at Foundation Building can challenge someone at Core Formation!
```

---

### Advanced Design 3: Hidden Realms / Special Routes
**Purpose**: Add depth and mystery to the system.

**Common Designs**:
1. **Hidden major realm**: Above the known highest realm, there is a "true highest realm"
   - Example: Everyone thought "Tribulation Crossing" was the peak → but there is also "Immortal / Divine level"
2. **Special cultivation path**: The protagonist walks a "different road"
   - Example: Everyone cultivates spiritual qi; the protagonist cultivates "Devouring"
3. **False realm trap**: Some realms are "dead ends"
   - Example: The wrong path leads to the "Demonic Way," with no further progress possible

## 3. Integration of Power System with World-Building

### Integration Point 1: Map and Realm Correspondence
**Design Logic**: Different realms correspond to different maps.

**Example**:
```markdown
## World Map Layering
**1st Layer World (Low-level Continent)**: Qi Condensation – Core Formation
- Thin spiritual qi, maximum cultivation capped at Core Formation
- Example: Azure Profound Continent

**2nd Layer World (Mid-level Continent)**: Nascent Soul – Deity Transformation
- Dense spiritual qi, can cultivate to Deity Transformation
- Example: Tianyuan Continent

**3rd Layer World (High-level Continent)**: Void Refinement – Integration
- Abundant spiritual qi, can cultivate to Integration
- Example: Sacred Realm

**4th Layer World (Divine Domain)**: Grand Ascension – Tribulation Crossing
- Immortal spiritual energy, can cultivate to Tribulation Crossing and beyond
- Example: Immortal Realm
```

**Benefits**:
- Protagonist starts in the low-level continent and progressively "ascends" to higher ones
- Creates "frog in a well" reversals: protagonist is unmatched in the low-level continent → arrives at higher continent to find masters everywhere

---

### Integration Point 2: Faction Realm Requirements
**Design Logic**: Joining a faction requires reaching a specific realm.

**Example**:
```markdown
## Faction Grade Table
| Faction Grade | Minimum Realm Required | Representative Faction |
|--------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Mundane Faction** | No requirement | Kingdoms, families |
| **Third-rate Sect** | Foundation Building | Azure Cloud Sect |
| **Second-rate Sect** | Core Formation | Heaven Sword Sect |
| **First-rate Sect** | Nascent Soul | Grand Profound Sect |
| **Holy Land** | Deity Transformation | Five Great Holy Lands |
| **Ancient Hidden Family** | Void Refinement | Ancient Clan |
| **Mysterious Organization** | Integration | Heavenly Mechanism Pavilion |
```

---

### Integration Point 3: Lifespan and Time Scale
**Design Logic**: Higher realms mean longer lifespans and wider story time spans.

**Example**:
```
Qi Condensation: Lifespan 100 years → Story timeline: 10–20 years
Core Formation: Lifespan 500 years → Story timeline: 50–100 years
Deity Transformation: Lifespan 2,000 years → Story timeline: several hundred years
Grand Ascension: Lifespan 100,000 years → Story timeline: thousands to tens of thousands of years
```

**Uses**:
- Creates a "sense of passing ages" (protagonist cultivates for a thousand years, old friends have already died)
- Justifies "closed-door cultivation" (retreat for a hundred years to break through)

## 4. Common Power System Problems and Solutions

### Problem 1: System Collapse (Contradictions)
**Symptom**:
```
Early story: Core Formation can "destroy a city"
Later story: Nascent Soul fights and can only "shatter rocks"
```

**Cause**: The author forgot the earlier setting, or reduced descriptions to keep combat scenes manageable.

**Solutions**:
- **Setting Manual**: Record the ability limits for each realm
- **Environmental Restrictions**: Prohibit large-scale destruction in special settings (e.g., inside a sect)
- **Law Suppression**: The "Heavenly Dao rules" of higher-level continents are stronger, reducing the effective power at the same realm

---

### Problem 2: Realm Inflation (Leveling Too Fast)
**Symptom**:
```
Volume 1: Qi Condensation → Foundation Building (100k characters)
Volume 2: Foundation Building → Core Formation (100k characters)
Volume 10: Already crossed tribulation and ascended, nothing left to write
```

**Solutions**:
- **Slow down breakthroughs**: Each major realm should take at least 200,000–300,000 characters
- **Sub-realm padding**: Core Formation Early → Middle → Late → Peak (5k characters each)
- **Horizontal expansion**: Don't increase realm, but improve combat skills / artifacts / bloodlines

---

### Problem 3: Invincible Protagonist Syndrome
**Symptom**:
```
Protagonist is always stronger than the enemy, no suspense
```

**Solutions**:
- **Hidden masters**: There is always "someone stronger" in existence
- **Special restrictions**: Protagonist is powerful but has certain weaknesses (e.g., can't kill people / can't use certain powers)
- **Team combat**: Protagonist needs teammates to win

---

## 5. Power System Design Self-Checklist

After completing the system design, check the following:

- [ ] **Energy essence is clear**: What is the energy? Where does it come from?
- [ ] **Realm grades are clear**: How many realms? What is each realm called?
- [ ] **Ability differences are reasonable**: Does each realm have a "qualitative leap"?
- [ ] **Breakthrough conditions are defined**: How to break through? What resources / conditions are needed?
- [ ] **Sub-realm divisions**: Are there early / middle / late stages?
- [ ] **Combat power multiplier system**: How does the protagonist justify cross-realm challenges?
- [ ] **World-building integration**: Do realms correspond to maps / factions / lifespans?
- [ ] **Consistency**: Are there any contradictions in the settings?
- [ ] **Room to grow**: Is there enough upgrade space to carry through to the end?

## Appendix: Classic Power System Case Studies

### Case 1: *A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality* — The Benchmark Cultivation System
**System**:
```
Qi Condensation (13 layers) → Foundation Building → Core Formation → Nascent Soul → Deity Transformation → Void Refinement → Integration → Grand Ascension → Tribulation Crossing
```

**Strengths**:
- Clear realm distinctions, each realm has defined abilities
- Reasonable breakthrough conditions (tribulation, pills, comprehension)
- World map layering (Mortal Realm → Spirit Realm → Immortal Realm)

**Weaknesses**:
- Later stage realm inflation (difficult to sustain after Grand Ascension)

---

### Case 2: *Douluo Dalu* — The Streamlined System Exemplar
**System**:
```
Spirit Masters (1–10 levels) → Spirit Elder → Spirit Ancestor → Spirit King → Spirit Emperor → Spirit Sage → Spirit Douluo → Title Douluo → God
```

**Strengths**:
- Clean, memorable naming (all use "Spirit" prefix)
- Clear sub-level divisions (star ratings)
- Well-developed combat power multiplier system (spirit skills, spirit bones, bloodlines)

---

### Case 3: Counter-Example (A Mediocre Novel)
**Problem**:
```
Realm names are chaotic:
Realm 1: Qi Condensation Stage
Realm 2: Spirit Force Stage (??? What's different from Qi Condensation?)
Realm 3: Foundation Building Stage
Realm 4: Nascent Soul Stage (??? What happened to Core Formation?)
Realm 5: Deity Transformation Stage
...
```

**Problem Analysis**:
- Realm naming is non-standard
- Skipped key realms (Core Formation Stage)
- Readers will be confused
