# Zhihu Short Story Genre Templates

> This document provides complete templates for popular Zhihu short story genres, ready to apply directly.

> **Vietnamese Patterns Note**: When writing in Vietnamese, apply the following style conventions:
> - **Pronoun system**: Use `tao-mày` between close characters, `tôi-ngài` with strangers
> - **Show-don't-tell**: Express emotions through physical reactions (nghiến răng for anger, run bần bật for fear)
> - **Sentence rhythm**: Short punchy sentences for action/conflict; longer for emotional scenes
> - **Ellipsis usage**: Use `...` for hesitation, cliffhangers; `—` for dialogue and inner thoughts
> - **Colloquial dialogue**: Keep dialogue short, colloquial; use literary style for narration and inner monologue
> - **Emotion curves**: Build from suppressed pain to pleasure in face-slapping scenes; slow burn for romance

## Vietnamese Writing Patterns

### From Ta Dung Nhin Vo Lam Toang & Sources:
- Dialogue format: "Content" + action tag
- Inner thoughts: third-person narrative without quotes
- Scene breaks: *** major, —0o0— minor
- Pronouns: mày/tao (close), tôi/ngài (formal), hắn (antagonist)
- First-person internal: cậu/mình

### 8 Error Types to Avoid:
1. Units: mét/cm/km/kg (NOT trượng/dặm/tấc/thốn)
2. Vocabulary: context-appropriate
3. Punctuation: — single (NOT ——)
4. Sentence: complete (subject + predicate)
5. Spelling: natural Vietnamese
6. Connectors: và/nhưng/nên/vì/rồi/thì
7. Subject: explicit in descriptions
8. Natural rhythm

### Genre-Specific Patterns:

#### Xuanhuan/Cultivation:
- Use mét/cm/km for distances (NOT trượng)
- Power levels: Trúc Cơ, Kim Đan, Nguyên Anh
- Dragon descriptions from Bien Nien Su Rong style

#### Isekai/Gaming:
- Status card format with "Keng!" notifications
- Combat: short → explanation → short punch
- Level-up pacing with 5% recovery chance

#### Romance/Fire Strand:
- Show-don't-tell emotion markers
- Building slow, conflict before resolution
- Body language over telling emotions

#### Rules-Mystery:
- Clue placement patterns
- Revelation timing
- Suspense building

## 1. Chasing Wife to Cremation (8000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (500 characters): Male lead pursuing wife in progress, female lead cold
Flashback (2500 characters): How he abused her back then
Present (2500 characters): Her change, his humbleness
Turning point (1500 characters): Truth/opportunity
Ending (1000 characters): Broken mirror reunited/letting go completely
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Famous wife-pursuing scene**: Waiting in rain/kneeling for forgiveness/giving up everything
2. **Heartbreaking memory**: Cold violence/favoring white moonlight/lack of trust
3. **Female lead's transformation**: Career success/new love interest/no longer cares
4. **Truth revealed**: Past misunderstanding/his hardships/her sacrifice

### Emotion Curve
```
Opening: Pleasure (watching him be humble)
Flashback: Pain (heartache for female lead)
Present: Pleasure + Pain (she got stronger but still heartache)
Ending: Sweet or relieved
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Build from suppressed pain to pleasure in face-slapping scenes
- Slow burn for romance, fast escalation for revenge
- Use short punchy sentences for confrontations
- Longer descriptive passages for emotional beats and memory scenes
- Ellipsis (`...`) for hesitation and cliffhangers
- Dialogue dash (`—`) for spoken words and inner thoughts

## 2. Rebirth Revenge (5000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (500 characters): Past life tragic death/rebirth moment
Planning (1500 characters): Change key decision points
Confrontation (2000 characters): Face enemies directly
Ending (1000 characters): Revenge success + new life
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Past life's tragedy**: Scene of being killed (shocking opening)
2. **Butterfly effect**: Change one choice, change fate
3. **Face-slapping moment**: Enemy's shocked expression
4. **Final judgment**: Enemy's fate

### Emotion Curve
```
Opening: Pain (past life) - Excitement (rebirth)
Planning: Pleasure (step by step)
Confrontation: Huge pleasure (tearing apart enemies)
Ending: Satisfied
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Past life scenes: Use inverted syntax for dramatic effect
- Confrontation scenes: Short punchy sentences, minimal punctuation
- Show anger through physical reactions (nghiến răng, hét lên)
- Cliffhanger endings with short punchy sentences

## 3. Wealthy Family True/Fake Daughter (10000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (800 characters): Identity revealed/returning to wealthy family
Adaptation (2500 characters): Being excluded/compared
Counterattack (3500 characters): Displaying strength/face-slapping
Truth (2200 characters): Past truth/ mastermind behind
Ending (1000 characters): Taking everything back
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Identity contrast**: Fake daughter's superiority vs true daughter's "earthiness"
2. **Being bullied**: Fake daughter's provocation/family's favoritism
3. **Displaying strength**: Crushing fake daughter in certain area
4. **Truth revealed**: Reason for the switch back then

### Emotion Curve
```
Opening: Anticipation
Adaptation: Suppressed (upset for female lead)
Counterattack: Pleasure (face-slapping)
Truth: Shocked + pleasure
Ending: Satisfied
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Suppressed emotion scenes: Longer descriptive sentences
- Face-slapping moments: Short punchy dialogue, quick reactions
- Use physical reactions to show emotion (nghiến răng when angry)
- Literary style for narration, colloquial for dialogue

## 4. Entertainment Industry Mask (8000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (500 characters): Being mocked/looked down upon
Foreshadowing (2000 characters): Various mask hints
Mask removal (3500 characters): One after another revealed
Conclusion (2000 characters): Whole internet shocked + sweetness
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Being mocked scene**: Antifans mocking/rivals provoking
2. **Mask hints**: Mysterious big shot appears
3. **Mask removal climax**: Identity revealed, everyone shocked
4. **Face-slapping moment**: Previous mockers' face hurts

### Common Masks
```
- Top designer/painter/musician
- Mysterious heir of financial group
- Academic big shot in certain field
- Life-saving benefactor/investor
```

### Emotion Curve
```
Opening: Suppressed (being mocked)
Foreshadowing: Anticipation (knowing face-slap coming)
Mask removal: Huge pleasure (each one more shocking)
Conclusion: Satisfied + sweet
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Mocking scenes: Build tension with short sentences
- Mask removal climax: Escalating revelation with punchy reactions
- Show character emotions through physical manifestations
- Use vernacular (thằng chó, khốn) for emotional intensity in confrontations

## 5. Contract Marriage (6000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (500 characters): Contract reason/rules setting
Getting to know (2000 characters): From stranger to familiar
Heartbeat (2000 characters): Unconsciously caring
Revelation (1500 characters): Contract expires/true confession
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Contract signing**: Transaction of each getting what they need
2. **Ice-breaking moment**: First real exchange
3. **Jealousy scene**: Third party triggers possessiveness
4. **True feelings exposed**: Accidentally says true feelings

### Emotion Curve
```
Opening: Curious
Getting to know: Relaxed + sweet
Heartbeat: Slight pain + sweet
Revelation: Very sweet
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Ice-breaking moments: Use natural dialogue with colloquial language
- Jealousy scenes: Show possessiveness through physical reactions
- confession scenes: Longer descriptive sentences for emotional weight
- Use tao-mày between close characters, tôi-ngài with strangers

## 6. Yandere Paranoid (5000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (500 characters): Yandere behavior display
Memory (1500 characters): Why did they become like this
Present (2000 characters): Possession and struggle
Ending (1000 characters): Redemption/perish
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Famous yandere scene**: Extreme possession behavior
2. **Childhood/past**: Cause of yandere
3. **Female lead's attitude**: Fear → understanding → accepting/escaping
4. **Critical choice**: Stay or leave

### Notes
```
- Yandere needs a cause, not without reason
- Bottom line: Cannot truly harm female lead
- Need redemption possibility or tragedy inevitability
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Yandere behavior: Show through actions, not stated feelings
- Fear scenes: Use run bần bật, mặt tái mét for physical manifestations
- Use hắn for third-person antagonist pronouns
- Cliffhanger endings for suspense

## 7. Marriage Before Love (8000-character Template)

### Structure
```
Opening (600 characters): Marriage reason/wedding
Breaking in (2400 characters): Cohabitation daily/probing each other
Ambiguous (2500 characters): Heartbeat moments/jealousy
Confirmation (2500 characters): Confession/crisis/HE
```

### Key Scenes
1. **Awkward cohabitation**: Separate rooms/pretending not to care
2. **Daily sweetness**: Unconscious care
3. **Jealousy moment**: Seeing the other with opposite sex
4. **Honesty moment**: Admitting to liking the other

### Emotion Curve
```
Entirely sweet, with slight pain as seasoning
Rhythm: Slow burn - Heating up - Boiling
```

### Vietnamese Pacing Notes
- Slow burn pacing: Build tension gradually with longer descriptive sentences
- Heating up phase: Shorten sentences as emotional tension rises
- Boiling phase: Punchy dialogue, quick physical reactions
- Use ngài/tôi for formal relationship development
- Show jealousy through body language (nghiến răng, nổ gân)
