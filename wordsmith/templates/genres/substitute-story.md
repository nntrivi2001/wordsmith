# Substitute Story Template

## Core Settings

### Essence of Substitute Stories
```
Identity crisis + Distinguishing true/false feelings + Self-worth pursuit
```

### Core Triangle Relationship
- **Female lead (substitute)**: Identity struggle, emotional growth
- **Male lead (obsessed person)**: Transition from obsession to true love
- **White moonlight (prototype)**: Contrast, source of conflict

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F16
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

---

## Female Lead Type Selection

### Passive Substitute Type
- Chosen to be a substitute
- Inner struggle, self-doubt
- Growth arc: From acceptance to awakening

### Active Substitute Type
- Becomes a substitute actively (has purpose)
- Sober, planned
- Growth arc: From using to falling in love

### Awakened Substitute Type
- Transition from passive to active
- Key awakening moment
- Growth arc: From lost to finding self

### Reversal Substitute Type
- Identity reversal (substitute is the real white moonlight)
- Shocking truth revelation
- Growth arc: From misunderstanding to truth

---

## Female Lead Psychology Growth Arc

```
Unaware period → Awareness period → Confirmation period → Resolution period → Letting go period
```

### Unaware Period
- Doesn't know she's a substitute/or knows but doesn't care
- Immersed in being doted on

### Awareness Period
- Discovers small clues
- Starts to doubt, inner turmoil

### Confirmation Period
- Confirms substitute identity
- Emotional collapse, self-worth collapse

### Resolution Period
- Decides to leave, find self, no longer dependent

### Letting Go Period
- Truly lets go, completes self-growth

---

## Male Lead Psychology Transition Arc

```
Obsession period → Confusion period → Confirmation period → Regret period → Wooing period
```

### Obsession Period
- Immersed in obsession with white moonlight
- Treats female lead as substitute, can't see the female lead herself

### Confusion Period
- Starts to unable to distinguish, develops real feelings for female lead
- But unwilling to admit

### Confirmation Period
- Confirms in love with female lead, not the shadow of white moonlight
- Inner struggle

### Regret Period
- Realizes own mistake, regrets hurting female lead
- Wants to make amends

### Wooing Period
- "Wooing the wife through the fire pit"
- Proves with actions, waits for female lead's forgiveness

---

## Key Plot Nodes

### Meeting Node (First 10%)
- Substitute enters the story
- Establish substitute relationship
- Bury identity foreshadowing

### Sweet Period
- Being doted on daily
- Heart-fluttering moments
- Reader immersion

### Cracks Appear
- Discovers substitute identity/white moonlight appears
- Relationship shaken

### Truth Revealed
- Identity completely exposed
- Emotional collapse, relationship breaks

### Separation Period
- Female lead leaves
- Male lead regrets
- Each grows separately

### Wooing the Wife Fire Pit
- Male lead tries to win back
- Female lead refuses/hesitates
- Repeated tugging

### Ending Choices
- HE: Reconciliation like before
- BE: Each at peace
- OE: Open ending

---

## Pain Point Design

### Identity Pain
- Moment of discovering being a substitute
- Being treated as someone else
- Self-worth denied

### Emotional Pain
- Putting out genuine feelings but not being seen
- Being loved but not for oneself
- Pain of love unrequited

### Comparison Pain
- Comparison with white moonlight
- Can never become "that person"
- Even better is not as good as the prototype

---

## Sweet Point Design

### Sweet Daily Life
- Small details of being doted on
- Unconscious preference
- Exclusive tenderness

### Awakening Sweetness
- Male lead discovers true heart moment
- Realizing female lead's uniqueness
- Starts truly seeing female lead

### Wooing Sweetness
- Male lead's humility and effort
- Meticulous compensation
- Sincere confession

---

## Recommended Settings

### Era Background
- Modern city/Republic era/ancient palace

### Identity Setting
- Wealthy family/Entertainment circle/Ancient princely manor

### White Moonlight Status
- Dead/Missing/Changed heart/Doesn't actually exist

### Pain-Sweet Ratio
- Early: Sweet 3 Pain 7
- Mid: Sweet 1 Pain 9
- Late: Sweet 7 Pain 3

---

## Precautions

- Female lead cannot be too merciful (unlimited forgiveness)
- Male lead must be sincere in wooing (cannot be too easy)
- White moonlight cannot be too perfect (otherwise hard to replace)
- Substitute identity revelation must be dramatic
- Emotional transitions must have process (cannot be abrupt)
