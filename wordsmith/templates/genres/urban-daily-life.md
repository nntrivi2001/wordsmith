# Urban Slice-of-Life Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Realistic life feel + Small person counterattack + Detail resonance. Writing the extraordinary in the ordinary, letting readers see their own reflection.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M03
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Workplace Struggle Flow
- **Feature**: Workplace fight, promotion and salary increase, career success
- **Core Satisfaction**: Performance crush, promotion face-slapping, financial freedom
- **Representative Works**: 《Du La La Sheng Zhi Ji》《Wo De Qian Ban Sheng》《Huan Le Song》

### Marketplace Life Flow
- **Feature**: Ordinary people's daily life, cozy domestic atmosphere
- **Core Satisfaction**: Life wisdom, warmth and cold of human relations, small happiness
- **Representative Works**: 《Qing Da Hui Da 1988》《Wu Lin Wai Zhuan》

### Entrepreneurship Counterattack Flow
- **Feature**: Start from scratch, business success
- **Core Satisfaction**: Business wisdom, capital operation, wealth accumulation
- **Representative Works**: 《Da Jiang Da He》《Ji Mao Fei Shang Tian》《Zheng Yang Men Xia》

### Reborn Urban Flow
- **Feature**: Reborn back to the past, change life
- **Core Satisfaction**: Foresight, invest and get rich, make up for regrets
- **Representative Works**: 《Chong Sheng Zhi Shen Ji Xue Ba》《Chong Sheng Wan Mei Shi Dai》

## 2. Worldview: Urban Sociology

### Social Classes
| Class | Feature | Resources |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Wealthy | Capitalist | Unlimited |
| Middle class | White-collar elite | Adequate |
| Working class | Ordinary office worker | Limited |
| Bottom | Working people | Scarce |

### Workplace Ecology
| Role | Feature | Relationship with MC |
|------|---------|---------------------|
| Boss | Decision maker | Need to compete for |
| Superior | Direct leader | Can be enemy can be friend |
| Colleague | Competitor | Cooperate and compete |
| Subordinate | Executor | Cultivate and use |
| Client | Resource side | Need to maintain |

### Resource System
- **Money**: Most direct resource
- **Connections**: Relationship network
- **Ability**: Professional skill
- **Information**: Intelligence advantage
- **Time**: Most scarce resource

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Performance Crush
- **Scene**: Work task/project competition
- **Satisfaction**: MC's performance far exceeds opponent, people look at with new eyes
- **Key**: Must show process, not just result

### Promotion Face-Slapping
- **Scene**: People who looked down on MC
- **Satisfaction**: After MC gets promoted, the other party must report to MC
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, can't be too deliberate

### Investment Wealth
- **Scene**: MC invests in some project/stock
- **Satisfaction**: Obtain huge return, financial freedom
- **Key**: Must have reasonable information source

### Connection Cash-in
- **Scene**: At critical moment, connections play role
- **Satisfaction**: Previously accumulated relationships helped a lot
- **Key**: Connections must be foreshadowed early

## 5. Outline Structure (1.5 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Start (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: MC enters workplace/starts business
- **Status**: Bottom
- **Climax**: First prove self, gain recognition

### Volume 2: Footing (Chapters 81-180)
- **Core**: Gain foothold, establish foundation
- **Status**: Grassroots
- **Climax**: First promotion/project success

### Volume 3: Rise (Chapters 181-300)
- **Core**: Rapid rise, emerge from the crowd
- **Status**: Middle level
- **Climax**: Major project success, enter core circle

### Volume 4: Peak (Chapters 301-450)
- **Core**: Become industry elite
- **Status**: High level
- **Climax**: Reach career peak

### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 451-550)
- **Core**: Life fulfilled
- **Status**: Top level
- **Climax**: Career family both fulfilled

## 6. Character Design

### MC Types
| Type | Feature | Growth Path |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Grassroots counterattack | Ordinary background | Rise through effort |
| Reborn with cheat | Has foresight | Avoid traps seize opportunities |
| Genius type | Extraordinary ability | Rapid rise |
| Steady type | Down-to-earth | Accumulate steadily then explode |

### Supporting Character Design
| Type | Function | Relationship with MC |
|------|----------|---------------------|
| Noble person | Guide and help | Mentor/patron |
| Opponent | Create conflict | Competitive relationship |
| Ally | Fight side by side | Cooperative partner |
| Villain | Make things difficult | Need to guard against |

---

## 7. Conflict Design

### Workplace Conflicts
| Conflict | Source | Resolution |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Performance competition | Colleague | Speak with strength |
| Power struggle | Superior | Side-taking/self-preservation |
| Resource competition | Department | Negotiation/cooperation |
| Layoff crisis | Company | Prove value |

### Life Conflicts
| Conflict | Source | Resolution |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Economic pressure | Mortgage/supporting family | Increase income reduce expenses |
| Family conflict | Relatives | Communicate understand |
| Relationship problems | Partner | Manage relationship |
| Health problems | Self | Adjust lifestyle |

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Overly daily, no conflicts
- ❌ Insufficient satisfaction density
- ❌ MC lacks initiative
- ❌ Cheat too obvious

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Daily life has conflicts
- ✅ Maintain satisfaction rhythm
- ✅ MC takes initiative
- ✅ Success has foreshadowing
