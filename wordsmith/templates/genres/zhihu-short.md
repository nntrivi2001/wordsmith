# Zhihu Short Story Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Quick pace + Strong conflicts + Ultimate reversals. Complete the classic four-part narrative structure within 3000-15000 words, first 300 words determine life or death.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M17
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Features

### Length Positioning
| Type | Word Count | Applicable Scene |
|------|------------|------------------|
| Micro short story | 3000-5000 | Single reversal, emotional explosion |
| Standard short story | 5000-10000 | Double reversal, complete arc |
| Long short story | 10000-15000 | Multiple reversals, complex emotions |

### Golden 300 Words Rule
- **First 50 words**: Create suspense or conflict
- **50-150 words**: Establish character's situation
- **150-300 words**: Throw core contradiction

## 2. Ten Opening Hook Templates

### 1. Death Opening
```
Template: I died, then [reversal]
Example: I died, on my wedding anniversary. More ironic, the person who killed me was crying hardest at my funeral.
Applicable: Revenge, mystery, angsty
```

### 2. Time Flashback
```
Template: [Result], everything started from [time point]
Example: He kneeled before me begging to get back together, I only felt funny. Three years ago, he wasn't like this at all.
Applicable: Wife-wooing fire pit, broken mirror reunion
```

### 3. Identity Reversal
```
Template: Everyone thought I was [A], actually I'm [B]
Example: Everyone thought I was a poor relative getting by on charity, including my "kind" cousin who "kindly took me in."
Applicable: Face-slapping, satisfying, rich family
```

### 4. Extreme Situation
```
Template: [Extreme situation], I [abnormal reaction]
Example: Trapped in a freezer at minus thirty degrees, I laughed instead. Finally, I can die with an excuse.
Applicable: Angsty, redemption, mystery
```

### 5. Dialogue Opening
```
Template: "[Impactful line]"
Example: "Sign here, I'll raise the child, you leave with nothing." He pushed the divorce agreement toward me, eyes full of disgust.
Applicable: Marriage, angsty, satisfying
```

### 6. Rebirth/Transmigration
```
Template: Open my eyes, I returned to [key time point]
Example: Open my eyes, I returned to the day before being pushed down the building. This time, the one who dies won't be me.
Applicable: Rebirth revenge, transmigration
```

### 7. Secret Reveal
```
Template: [Someone] doesn't know, [secret]
Example: What my husband doesn't know is that his white moonlight, I hired to act.
Applicable: Reversal, revenge, satisfying
```

### 8. Number Impact
```
Template: [Number] + [abnormal fact]
Example: In seven years of marriage, he sent me to the emergency room thirty-two times, I finally learned one thing—pretend death.
Applicable: Angsty, awakening, revenge
```

### 9. Contrast
```
Template: Once [A state], now [B state]
Example: Once, he was a god I couldn't reach. Now, he kneels before me, begging me to look at him.
Applicable: Counterattack, wife-wooing
```

### 10. Suspense Question
```
Template: Do you know [question]?
Example: Do you know how desperate a person can be? Probably so desperate they don't even have strength to die.
Applicable: Angsty, emotional
```

## 3. Classic Genre Templates

### Wife-Wooing Fire Pit (8000 words)
```
Structure: Result → Memory → Turn → Wife-wooing → Ending

Act 1 (1000 words): Male lead humbly asks to get back together, female lead cold
Act 2 (3000 words): Flashback marriage abuse/neglect
Act 3 (1500 words): Female lead leaves trigger
Act 4 (2000 words): Male lead's wife-wooing process
Act 5 (500 words): HE or BE ending
```

### Rebirth Revenge (6000 words)
```
Structure: Brutal death → Rebirth → Layout → Counterattack → Ending

Act 1 (800 words): Previous life brutal death scene
Act 2 (500 words): Rebirth, make oath
Act 3 (2500 words): Change fate, step by step
Act 4 (1700 words): Key counterattack, truth revealed
Act 5 (500 words): Enemy's ending + new life
```

### Rich Family Face-Slapping (5000 words)
```
Structure: Bullied → Endure → Identity revealed → Face-slap → Wrap up

Act 1 (800 words): Looked down on/bullied
Act 2 (1200 words): Endure, accumulate emotions
Act 3 (1500 words): Identity reveal process
Act 4 (1200 words): Face-slapping climax
Act 5 (300 words): Everyone's reaction + ending
```

## 4. Ending Modes

### HE (Happy Ending)
```
Applicable: Sweet romance, wife-wooing success, revenge fulfilled
Ending formula:
- Reconciliation hug
- Wedding/children
- Years later happy daily life
```

### BE (Bad Ending)
```
Applicable: Angsty, tragic beauty, knife writing
Ending formula:
- Miss each other for life
- One party dies
- Love unrequited
- Open tragic ending
```

### Open Ending
```
Applicable: Profound meaning, spark discussion
Ending formula:
- Leave suspense
- Reader imagine freely
- Endless aftertaste
```

## 5. Rhythm Control

### Word Allocation Golden Ratio
```
Opening hook: 5% (Grab reader)
Background setup: 15% (Quick immersion)
Conflict escalation: 40% (Core content)
Climax reversal: 25% (Emotional explosion)
Ending wrap-up: 15% (Aftertaste)
```

### Rhythm Acceleration Techniques
- **Short sentences**: Create tension
- **Dialogue advancement**: Speed up plot
- **Omit transitions**: Jump directly to key scenes
- **Emotional vocabulary**: Strengthen impact

### Taboos
- ❌ Long backstory introductions
- ❌ Excessive psychological description
- ❌ Dragging transitions
- ❌ Setup too long without explosion

---

## 6. Emotional Curve Design

### Angsty Curve
```
Opening (low) → Memory sweetness (high) → Angst unfolds (sustained low) → Most anguished point (bottom) → Ending (HE rises/BE lower)
```

### Satisfying Curve
```
Opening (suppressed) → Endure (accumulate) → Explode (high) → Face-slap (higher) → Wrap up (satisfied)
```

### Reversal Curve
```
Opening (false A) → Reinforce A → Reverse (B revealed) → Shock (impact) → New understanding (sublimate)
```

## 7. Quick Character Building

### Three-Second Character Method
```
One-sentence character definition:
- Profession/identity + Core trait + Key contradiction

Example:
- "Surface-enduring rich daughter-in-law, actually holds whole family's handle"
- "Ex-wife abandoned by scum man, actually hidden boss"
- "Humbly wife-wooing CEO, three years ago drove her away himself"
```

### Contrast Portrayal Method
```
Early vs Late:
- Early humble → Late queen
- Early cold → Late humble
- Early glamorous → Late down-and-out
```
