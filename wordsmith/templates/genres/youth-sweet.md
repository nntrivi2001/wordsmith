# Youth Sweet Romance Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Light sweet atmosphere + High-density interaction + Growth resonance. Meeting the right person at the most beautiful age.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F06
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Campus Romance Flow
- **Feature**: High school/college campus, pure love stories
- **Core Satisfaction**: Flirty interaction, confession and heart flutter, sweet daily life
- **Representative Works**: 《Zhi Women Dan Chun De Xiao Mei Hao》《Ni Hao Jiu Shi Guang》《Zui Hao De Wo Men》

### Childhood Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Grew up together since childhood, long companionship leads to love
- **Core Satisfaction**: Tacit interaction, identity transition, protective growth
- **Representative Works**: 《He Yi Sheng Xiao Mo》《Wei Xiao Xiao Hen Qing Shen》

### Happy Enemies Flow
- **Feature**: First meeting not compatible, banter leads to true love
- **Core Satisfaction**: Daily sparring, reverse and surprise, genuine feelings revealed
- **Representative Works**: 《Shan Shan Lai Le》《Qin Ai De Re Ai De》

### Healing Sweet Flow
- **Feature**: Warm and healing, long journey together
- **Core Satisfaction**: Warm daily life, mutual healing, plain happiness
- **Representative Works**: 《Ni Shi Wo De Rong Yao》《Yu Shen Qing Duo Zhi Jiao》

## 3. Sweet Interaction Design

### Interaction Types
| Type | Feature | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Daily sugar | Small sweetness | Feeding, hand holding |
| Jealousy interaction | Small jealousy | See opposite sex approaching |
| Protective interaction | Safety sense | Shield from rain, defend |
| Tacit interaction | Kindred spirits | Speak at same time |
| Reverse interaction | Unexpected surprise | Cold face warm heart |

### Sweetness Rhythm
```
First stage (1-30%): Getting to know
- First meeting, leave impression
- Forced interaction, generate interaction
- Sweetness: ★☆☆☆☆

Second stage (30-50%): Flirting
- Interaction increases, heart flutters
- Flirting warms, somewhat distant
- Sweetness: ★★★☆☆

Third stage (50-70%): Confirmation
- Confession succeeds, relationship established
- Sweet romance, high sugar interaction
- Sweetness: ★★★★★

Fourth stage (70-90%): Test
- Encounter obstacles, relationship tested
- Face together, feelings deepen
- Sweetness: ★★★☆☆

Fifth stage (90-100%): Fulfillment
- Overcome difficulties, bear fruit
- Sweet ending, sugar wrap-up
- Sweetness: ★★★★★
```

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Hero Saves Beauty
- **Scene**: Female lead encounters trouble/danger
- **Satisfaction**: Male lead appears in time, protectively scolds
- **Key**: Can't be too deliberate, must be natural

### Jealousy Declaration
- **Scene**: Someone approaches the other
- **Satisfaction**: Jealousy performance, declare sovereignty
- **Key**: Must be cute, can't be too much

### Public Display
- **Scene**: Show affection publicly
- **Satisfaction**: Let onlookers envy and be jealous
- **Key**: Must be natural, can't be too tacky

### Accidental Cohabitation
- **Scene**: Live together for some reason
- **Satisfaction**: Daily interaction, flirting warms up
- **Key**: Must have reasonable reason

## 5. Outline Structure (600K Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Meeting (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: Male and female leads meet, establish contact
- **Relationship**: Strangers → Acquaintances
- **Climax**: First heart flutter moment

### Volume 2: Flirting (Chapters 51-120)
- **Core**: Interaction increases, flirting warms up
- **Relationship**: Acquaintances → Flirting
- **Climax**: Almost confessed/was interrupted

### Volume 3: Confirmation (Chapters 121-180)
- **Core**: Confession succeeds, relationship established
- **Relationship**: Flirting → Lovers
- **Climax**: Official confession, together

### Volume 4: Test (Chapters 181-240)
- **Core**: Relationship encounters test
- **Relationship**: Lovers (with twists)
- **Climax**: Overcome difficulties together

### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 241-300)
- **Core**: Bear fruit
- **Relationship**: Lovers → Future
- **Climax**: Sweet ending

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Sweetness single-note, no variation
- ❌ Conflicts too fake, forced pain
- ❌ Male lead too perfect, no flaws
- ❌ Female lead too naive

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Sweetness has rhythm, has rises and falls
- ✅ Conflicts reasonable, can empathize
- ✅ Male lead has flaws, will grow
- ✅ Female lead has own views, has growth
