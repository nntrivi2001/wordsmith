# Historical Speculative Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Historical framework + Setting reversal + Rule gaming. When supernatural rules intersect with history, what is the cost of changing history?

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M08
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Historical Rule-ification Flow
- **Feature**: Historical laws personified as operable rules
- **Core Satisfaction**: Rule utilization, history rewriting, causal gaming
- **Representative Works**: 《Qing Yu Nian》《Zhui Xu》《Lin Gao Qi Ming》

### Historical Supernatural Flow
- **Feature**: Hidden supernatural truths behind history
- **Core Satisfaction**: Truth reveal, conspiracy cracking, history reconstruction
- **Representative Works**: 《Chang An Shi Er Shi Chen》《Da Tang Xuan Yi Lu》《Xian Wei Xia Kan Da Ming》

### Transmigration Fate-Changing Flow
- **Feature**: Transmigrators trying to change historical trajectory
- **Core Satisfaction**: Butterfly effect, historical turning points, fate struggle
- **Representative Works**: 《Xun Qin Ji》《Hui Dao Ming Chao Dang Wang Ye》《Xin Song》

### Historical Game Flow
- **Feature**: History gamified/systematized
- **Core Satisfaction**: System utilization, task completion, historical intervention
- **Representative Works**: 《Wo Zai Da Kang De Xian Yu Sheng Huo》《Da Ming Guo Shi》

## 3. Rule System Design

### Historical Intervention Rules
```
Can change:
- Individual fates
- Local events
- Technological development

Hard to change:
- Historical general trend
- Key nodes
- Core character fates

Cannot change:
- Time-locked events
- Causality-protected events
```

### Cost System
| Cost Type | Feature | Example |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Equivalent exchange | What you get is what you lose | Save requires sacrifice |
| Causality backlash | Bigger change bigger backlash | Body/mental damage |
| Time debt | Borrow from future to repay | Reduced lifespan |
| Existence dilution | Reduced sense of existence | Forgotten by history |

### Historical Knowledge Utilization
| Knowledge Type | Utilization Method | Risk |
|----------------|-------------------|------|
| Historical events | Early layout | Butterfly effect |
| Technical knowledge | Tech crush | Era maladjustment |
| Character information | Recruit talents | Change fate |
| Economic laws | Wealth accumulation | Draw attention |

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Historical Foresight
- **Scene**: Using historical knowledge to predict events
- **Satisfaction**: Foresight, successful layout
- **Key**: Must have reasonable information source

### Rule Utilization
- **Scene**: Discovering and utilizing historical rules
- **Satisfaction**: Exchange rules for advantage
- **Key**: Rules must be self-consistent

### Historical Reversal
- **Scene**: Changing historical direction
- **Satisfaction**: Butterfly effect, history reconstruction
- **Key**: Must have cost

### Causality Gaming
- **Scene**: Gaming with historical rules/guardians
- **Satisfaction**: Intellectual duel, winning in danger
- **Key**: Both sides must have IQ

## 5. Outline Structure (1.5 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Entering the Game (Chapters 1-60)

- **Status**: Has some influence
- **Climax**: Change local history

### Volume 3: Gaming (Chapters 151-280)

- **Status**: Important figure

- **Status**: Core figure
- **Climax**: Key historical node

### Volume 5: Final (Chapters 401-500)
- **Core**: Final ending

- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 7. Balance of Historical Feel and Speculation

### Historical Foundation
```
Must be accurate:
- Major historical events
- Key historical figures
- Basic era background

Can fictionalize:
- Detail supplements
- Marginal figures
- Secret events

Speculation space:
- Rule settings
- Supernatural elements
- Parallel history
```

### Reasonableness Maintenance
| Aspect | Key Points | Pitfalls |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Technology | Gradual progression | Too big a leap |
| System | Match era | Too advanced |
| Thought | Have foreshadowing | Modern thinking |
| Language | Era characteristics | Internet slang |

### Integration of Speculation and History
- Rules must fit historical background
- Supernatural elements need historical explanation
- Changing history must have reasonable cost

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Settings too fantastical
- ❌ Causality logic confused
- ❌ Historical common sense errors
- ❌ Cost not fulfilled

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Settings integrated with history
- ✅ Causality logic clear
- ✅ Historical foundation accurate
- ✅ Cost clearly fulfilled
