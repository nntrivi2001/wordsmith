# Dog-Blood Romance Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Emotional conflict + Love-hate deep romance + Wife-wooing fire pit. Dog blood is not a derogatory term, it's a tool to hit readers' emotional pain points.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F15
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### CEO Sweet Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Strong male weak female, domineering doting, more sweet less pain
- **Core Satisfaction**: Pamper wife without limit, slap face romantic rivals, wealthy life
- **Representative Mode**: Domineering CEO × Cinderella

### Wife-Wooing Fire Pit Flow
- **Feature**: Early stage tortures female lead, later stage male lead crawls
- **Core Satisfaction**: Female lead counterattack, male lead regrets deeply, humble seeking reconciliation
- **Key**: Torture fiercely, wooing miserably

### Rebirth Revenge Flow
- **Feature**: Female lead reborn with memory, step by step
- **Core Satisfaction**: Tear apart scum man and cheap woman, reverse fate, gain true love
- **Key**: Previous life miserable enough, this life satisfying enough

### Substitute Literature Flow
- **Feature**: Female lead is white moonlight substitute, heart-torturing
- **Core Satisfaction**: Substitute counterattack, white moonlight collapses, male lead regrets
- **Key**: Heart-torture scenes must be poignant

## 2. Classic Formula Templates

### Domineering CEO Formula
```
Domineering CEO + Cinderella = Power differential → Collision → Conquer → Sweet
```

**Essential Elements**:
- **Male lead**: Young and successful (28-35), worth over a hundred million, cold outside warm inside
- **Female lead**: Ordinary girl, kind and strong, has self-respect
- **Classic scenes**: Contract marriage, wall slam kiss, domineering declaration

### Substitute Literature Formula
```
Female lead is substitute → Abused → Heart dies and leaves → Male lead woos wife fire pit
```

**Pain Point Design**:
1. Male lead uses female lead to vent longing for white moonlight
2. Makes female lead wear white moonlight's clothes
3. White moonlight returns, female lead abandoned
4. Regret and remorse after knowing truth

### Wife-Wooing Fire Pit Formula
```
Early torture female lead → Misunderstanding separation → Female lead counterattack → Male lead humbly chases wife → HE
```

**Five-Stage Wife-Wooing**:
1. Discover truth, shocked and regretful
2. Search painstakingly, humbly beg to see
3. Send gifts to apologize, rejected at door
4. Risk life to protect, moves female lead
5. Long atonement, finally forgiven

## 3. Outline Structure (800K Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Sweet Illusion (Chapters 1-50, 15%)
- **Opening hook**: Contract/agreement marriage, display unequal status
- **Characters appear**: Male/female leads, white moonlight, romantic rivals
- **Core conflict**: Male lead cold, female lead endures
- **Small climax**: First heart flutter/misunderstanding

### Volume 2: Heart-Torture Abyss (Chapters 51-130, 25%)
- **Crisis escalation**: White moonlight appears, misunderstanding deepens
- **Pain points concentrate**: Misunderstanding, betrayal, harm
- **Turning point**: Female lead's heart dies, decides to leave
- **Climax**: Miscarriage/serious illness/frame-up

### Volume 3: Break and Leave (Chapters 131-180, 15%)
- **Truth partially revealed**: White moonlight's true face
- **Female lead leaves**: Takes child and runs/divorce with nothing/fake death
- **Male lead shocked**: Discovers what he lost

### Volume 4: Wife-Wooing Path (Chapters 181-280, 30%)
- **Male lead regrets**: Search, wait, humble
- **Female lead grows**: Successful career, confident and independent
- **Repeated tugging**: Old love hard to forget but hard to forgive
- **Climax**: Female lead has new love, male lead collapses

### Volume 5: Broken Mirror Reunion (Chapters 281-350, 15%)
- **Truth fully revealed**: All misunderstandings resolved
- **Male lead atones**: Risk life for her/give up everything
- **Reconciliation process**: Slowly rebuild trust
- **Ending**: HE, wedding/children

## 4. Emotional Tension Design

### Four Pain Elements
```
1. Misunderstanding: Clearly in love but hurt each other
2. Missing: Miss each other again and again
3. Sacrifice: Silent giving not known by the other
4. Regret: Discovered it's too late
```

### Sweet Triggers
```
1. Possessiveness: "You're mine"
2. Jealousy: Goes crazy seeing her talk to others
3. Doting: "I'm gentle with you only"
4. Protection: Stand in front of her
5. Confession: "I love you, have for a long time"
```

## 5. Classic Scene Quick Reference

### Heart-Torture Scenes
```markdown
【Substitute dialogue】
"You think I married you because I love you? No, you're just her shadow."
He said coldly, hand holding white moonlight's photo.

【Heart dies and leaves】
"Sorry, the person you love isn't me, and I don't love you anymore."
She turns and walks into the rain, never looking back.

【Wife-wooing kneels and begs】
"I was wrong, please come back..."
He kneels in the rain, but she doesn't look back.
```

### Sweet Scenes
```markdown
【Domineering declaration】
" Woman, you've successfully caught my attention."
CEO Lu's dangerous gaze stares at her.

【Wall slam confession】
He props his hand against the wall, trapping her in his arms.
"Want to run? Too late. From now on, you can only be mine."

【Daily doting】
"Cold?" He takes off his suit jacket and drapes it over her.
"Not cold..."
"Then wear it anyway, I think you're cold."
```
