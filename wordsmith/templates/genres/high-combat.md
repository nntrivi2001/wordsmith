# High Martial Arts Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Martial peak + Hot-blooded battles + Strength is justice. The fist is reason, the strong make the rules.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M21
- Universal Overlay: Pack U03

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Global High Martial Flow
- **Feature**: Spiritual energy awakens, nationwide martial arts, martial arts become mainstream
- **Core Satisfaction**: Martial breakthrough, ranking rise, national war glory
- **Representative Works**: 《Quan Qiu Gao Wu》《Wo You Yi Jian》《Wan Zu Zhi Jie》

### Martial Arts Master Flow
- **Feature**: Traditional martial arts, master realm, martial arts inheritance
- **Core Satisfaction**: Martial breakthrough, sect contention, martial arts alliance leader
- **Representative Works**: 《Long She Yan Yi》《Guo Shu Zong Shi》

### Combat Sports Flow
- **Feature**: Arena fighting, professional fighting, hot-blooded confrontation
- **Core Satisfaction**: Arena victory, technical progress, championship glory
- **Representative Works**: 《Quan Zhen Shan He》《Quan Zhi Gao Shou》

### Martial Cultivation Flow
- **Feature**: Martial arts combined with cultivation, enter dao through martial arts
- **Core Satisfaction**: Martial arts realm, physical body becomes saint, martial arts shatters void
- **Representative Works**: 《Zhe Tian》《Wan Mei Shi Jie》《Sheng Xu》

## 2. Worldview: High Martial Sociology

### Martial Status
- **Hidden period**: Martial artists hidden, ordinary people unaware
- **Public period**: Martial arts public, become profession
- **Mainstream period**: Martial arts become social core, martial artists hold lofty status

### Social Structure
| Class | Feature | Power |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Martial Saint | One nation's power | Transcendent status |
| Grand Master | Nation-ruling combat power | National teacher level |
| Master | One city's power | High level |
| Expert | Elite combat power | Middle level |
| Martial Artist | Basic combat power | Ordinary |
| Ordinary person | No martial arts | Bottom level |

### Power Structure
- **National martial affairs department**: Official martial arts organization
- **Martial arts families**: Families with long inheritance
- **Martial halls/dojos**: Martial arts teaching institutions
- **Underground forces**: Black market, killer organizations
- **Alien races/beasts**: Enemies of humanity

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Arena Crush
- **Scene**: Formal competition, all eyes watching
- **Satisfaction**: One move defeats enemy, shock everyone
- **Key**: Must have sufficient foreshadowing and opponent portrayal

### Martial Breakthrough
- **Scene**: Life-and-death moment/closed-door cultivation
- **Satisfaction**: Realm breaks through, combat power surges
- **Key**: Breakthrough must have accumulation, can't appear out of nowhere

### Conquering with Martial
- **Scene**: People looked down on MC
- **Satisfaction**: Fist is reason, defeat everyone
- **Key**: Can't be brainless face-slapping, must have restraint

### National War Glory
- **Scene**: Represent nation to battle
- **Satisfaction**: Glorify nation, national pride
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing of country and nation sentiment

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Entry (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: MC awakens martial talent
- **Map**: City/martial hall
- **Climax**: First show talent, defeat same-level strong enemy

### Volume 2: Rise (Chapters 81-180)
- **Core**: Join martial arts organization, participate in competitions
- **Map**: Provincial/national
- **Climax**: Win important competition championship

### Volume 3: Fame (Chapters 181-350)
- **Core**: Become known martial artist
- **Map**: National range
- **Climax**: Break through to Master, shock martial arts world

### Volume 4: Contend (Chapters 351-550)
- **Core**: Participate in international competition
- **Map**: International range
- **Climax**: National war victory, glorify nation

### Volume 5: Peak (Chapters 551-700)
- **Core**: Pursue martial peak
- **Map**: Global/other world
- **Climax**: Become martial peak

## 6. Battle Design

### Battle Rhythm
```
Exploration phase (20%):
- Both sides explore, show basic strength
- Audience/announcer introduce both backgrounds

Clash phase (40%):
- Formal exchange, technique clash
- Show martial arts characteristics

Climax phase (30%):
- Ultimate moves clash
- Unexpected reversal

Ending phase (10%):
- Winner and loser clear
- Subsequent impact
```

### Battle Description Key Points
- **Move names**: Must have martial arts feel
- **Force comparison**: Must have concrete description
- **Environment interaction**: Battle's impact on environment
- **Psychological description**: Both sides' psychological changes

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Combat power broken, numerical inflation
- ❌ Face-slapping too frequent, aesthetic fatigue
- ❌ Martial arts settings self-contradictory
- ❌ Supporting characters tool-like

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Combat power system self-consistent
- ✅ Face-slapping has restraint, has foreshadowing
- ✅ Martial arts settings detailed and consistent
- ✅ Supporting characters have independent personalities
