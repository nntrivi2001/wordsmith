# Suspense & Supernatural Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Atmospheric pressure + Bizarre rules + Logical self-consistency. Exploring unknown truths at the edge of fear and reason.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M06 / Pack M07
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Supernatural Exploration Flow
- **Feature**: Exploring supernatural locations, uncovering bizarre truth
- **Core Satisfaction**: Horror atmosphere, truth reveal, desperate escape
- **Representative Works**: 《Wo You Yi Zuo Kong Bu Wu》《Min Diao Ju Yi Wen Lu》《Dao Mu Bi Ji》

### Urban Legend Flow
- **Feature**: Supernatural events in modern cities
- **Core Satisfaction**: Everyday horror, rule discovery, outsmarting the supernatural
- **Representative Works**: 《Wo Zai Jing Shen Bing Yuan Xue Zhan Shen》《Gui Mi Zhi Zhu》

### Exorcism Flow
- **Feature**: MC has special abilities, specifically handles supernatural events
- **Core Satisfaction**: Ability growth, exorcism battles, saving others
- **Representative Works**: 《Mao Shan Hou Yi》《Wo Dang Dao Shi Na Xie Nian》

### Rule Mystery Flow
- **Feature**: Must follow rules to survive
- **Core Satisfaction**: Rule discovery, rule utilization, rule counter-kill
- **Representative Works**: 《Shen Yuan Gui Tu》《Gui Ze Guai Tan》

## 2. Worldview: Supernatural Sociology

### Supernatural Levels
| Level | Feature | Danger |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Ordinary supernatural | Harmless/slight harassment | Low |
| Evil spirit | Aggressive | Medium |
| Vengeful ghost | Extremely dangerous | High |
| Bizarre | Rule-level existence | Extremely high |
| Taboo | Unnameable | Fatal |

### Supernatural Sources
- **Grudge**: Strong negative emotions coalesce
- **Curse**: Man-made malice inflicted
- **Taboo**: Violating some rule
- **Otherworld**: From other dimensions
- **Natural**: Anomalies between heaven and earth

### Power Structure
- **Official agencies**: Special departments handling supernatural events
- **Folk organizations**: Taoist priests, exorcists, mediums
- **Supernatural beings**: Ghosts, bizarre entities, deities
- **Ordinary people**: Unaware majority

## 3. Horror Atmosphere Design

### Horror Types
| Type | Feature | Technique |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Psychological horror | Emphasis on implication | Leave blank, imagination |
| Visual horror | Direct description | Bloody, deformed |
| Atmospheric horror | Environment creation | Oppressive, eerie |
| Rule horror | Violation means death | Tense, anxious |

### Horror Rhythm
```
Setup phase (20%):
- Anomalies in daily life
- Ominous signs
- Atmosphere gradually oppressive

Escalation phase (40%):
- Supernatural events occur
- Horror gradually escalates
- Someone meets misfortune

Climax phase (30%):
- Face the core of horror
- Life and death line
- Truth revealed

Ending phase (10%):
- Horror lifted/continued
- Aftertaste/foreshadowing
```

---

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Rule Cracking
- **Scene**: Discovering bizarre rules
- **Satisfaction**: Using rule loopholes to escape/counter-kill
- **Key**: Rules must be self-consistent, cracking must be reasonable

### Truth Reveal
- **Scene**: Truth behind layers of fog
- **Satisfaction**: Suddenly enlightened, foreshadowing paid off
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, can't appear out of nowhere

### Desperate Escape
- **Scene**: Trapped in supernatural space
- **Satisfaction**: Nine deaths and one life, thrilling
- **Key**: Must have cost, can't be too easy

### Ability Awakening
- **Scene**: Awaken special ability at critical moment
- **Satisfaction**: Counter-kill supernatural, reverse situation
- **Key**: Ability must have limitations

## 5. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Entry (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: MC contacts supernatural world
- **Ability**: Initial awakening
- **Climax**: First supernatural event resolved

### Volume 2: Growth (Chapters 51-150)
- **Core**: Ability improves, contact more supernatural
- **Ability**: Steady improvement
- **Climax**: Major supernatural event resolved

### Volume 3: Deepening (Chapters 151-250)
- **Core**: Explore truth of supernatural world
- **Ability**: Qualitative change
- **Climax**: Partial truth revealed

### Volume 4: Confrontation (Chapters 251-350)
- **Core**: Confront high-level supernatural
- **Ability**: Near peak
- **Climax**: Defeat main enemy

### Volume 5: Final Stage (Chapters 351-400)
- **Core**: Final truth, ultimate confrontation
- **Ability**: Peak
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 6. Rule Design

### Rule Types
| Type | Feature | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Prohibition type | Cannot do something | Cannot look back |
| Compulsion type | Must do something | Must smile |
| Conditional type | Triggered by condition | Seeing causes death |
| Time type | Effective at specific time | 3 AM |

### Rule Design Principles
- **Self-consistency**: Rules don't contradict internally
- **Verifiable**: Rule violation has clear consequences
- **Has loopholes**: Can be exploited
- **Has origin**: Rule formation has reasons

---

## 7. Supernatural Ability Design

### Ability Types
| Type | Feature | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Yin-Yang eyes | See supernatural | Being seen by supernatural |
| Psychic | Communicate with spirits | Mental consumption |
| Exorcism | Expel supernatural | Physical consumption |
| Sealing | Seal supernatural | Material consumption |
| Precognition | Foresee danger | Headache/bleeding |

### Ability Limitations
- **Usage limit**: Limited per day
- **Side effects**: Weak after use
- **Condition limitation**: Need specific conditions
- **Level limitation**: Ineffective against high-level supernatural

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Horror piled without logic
- ❌ Rule loopholes casual
- ❌ MC's cheat breaks oppressive feeling
- ❌ Supernatural settings self-contradictory

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Horror has logic and foreshadowing
- ✅ Rules self-consistent and verifiable
- ✅ MC has cost and limitations
- ✅ Settings detailed and consistent
