# Games & Sports Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Competitive confrontation + Rule gaming + Team growth. Burn youth on the arena, prove everything with strength.

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Esports Competition Flow
- **Feature**: Professional esports, team competitions, road to championship
- **Core Satisfaction**: Skill crush, tactical counter-kill, championship glory
- **Representative Works**: 《Quan Zhi Gao Shou》《Ni Shi Wo De Rong Yao》《Qin Ai De Re Ai De》《Chuan Yue Huo Xian》

### Traditional Sports Flow
- **Feature**: Football/basketball/tennis and other traditional sports
- **Core Satisfaction**: Last-second wins, comeback competitions, championship dreams
- **Representative Works**: 《Guan Lan Gao Shou》《Zu Qiu Xiao Jiang》《Wang Qiu Wang Zi》《Wo Men De Shao Nian Shi Dai》

### Board Game Competition Flow
- **Feature**: Go/chess/mahjong and other intellectual sports
- **Core Satisfaction**: Intellectual crush, brilliant moves, genius battles
- **Representative Works**: 《Qi Hun》《Tian Yuan》《Wei Qi Shao Nian》

### Gaming Life Flow
- **Feature**: Game and reality intertwined, games change life
- **Core Satisfaction**: Game achievements, reality counterattack, virtual vs real
- **Representative Works**: 《Cong Ling Kai Shi》《Wang You Zhi Jin Zhan Fa Shi》《Jing Song Le Yuan》

## 3. Competition System Design

### Player Growth
```
Talent awakening → Basic training → Team trial → Substitute play → Starting position → Star player → Legend
```

### Ability Dimensions
| Dimension | Description | Training Method |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Operation | APM/accuracy | Repetitive practice |
| Awareness | Big picture/prediction | Review analysis |
| Mindset | Pressure/resilience | Competition experience |
| Coordination | Team tacit understanding | Break-in training |
| Tactics | Understanding/execution | Coach guidance |

### Competition Rhythm
```
BP phase (10%):
- Lineup gaming
- Tactical intent

Early game (25%):
- Lane/farm
- Small-scale conflicts

Mid game (35%):
- Resource contention
- Team fights breakout

Late game (25%):
- Decisive moments
- Winner determined

Post-game (5%):
- Review summary
- Emotional handling
```

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Desperate Comeback
- **Scene**: Behind in score, on brink of elimination
- **Satisfaction**: MC explodes, consecutive plays, reverse competition
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, show pressure

### Genius Battle
- **Scene**: Two genius players directly confront
- **Satisfaction**: Gods battle, full plays
- **Key**: Both must have highlights

### Tactical Crush
- **Scene**: Defeat strong enemy with unique tactics
- **Satisfaction**: Intellectual crush, opponent stunned
- **Key**: Tactics must be reasonable and credible

### Revenge Success
- **Scene**: Face opponent who previously defeated them
- **Satisfaction**: Vindication, prove growth
- **Key**: Must have emotional foreshadowing

## 5. Outline Structure (1.5 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Entry (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: MC shows talent, joins team
- **Status**: Substitute/youth team
- **Climax**: First game, stunning performance

### Volume 2: Growth (Chapters 61-150)
- **Core**: Become starter, participate in league
- **Status**: Starting player
- **Climax**: Achieve good results in league

### Volume 3: Rise (Chapters 151-280)

- **Status**: First-tier player
- **Climax**: Win league championship

### Volume 4: Peak (Chapters 281-400)
- **Core**: Challenge world championships
- **Status**: Top player
- **Climax**: Win world championship

### Volume 5: Legend (Chapters 401-500)
- **Core**: Become legend, retire with honor
- **Status**: Legend player
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

- **Mid**: Mid-league, steady rise
- **Late**: Top team, competing for championship

## 7. Competition Description Techniques

### Description Key Points
- **Operation details**: Specific keys/movement
- **Tactical interpretation**: Why do this
- **Mental activity**: Player's thoughts
- **Commentary perspective**: Third-party evaluation
- **Audience reaction**: Atmosphere boost

### Rhythm Control
- **Regular competition**: Brief description, highlight key moments
- **Important competition**: Detailed description, progressive layers
- **Finals**: Full-dimensional description, emotional explosion

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Only pile up competitions, no character development
- ❌ Tactical descriptions vague, unprofessional
- ❌ MC invincible, no challenges
- ❌ Supporting characters tool-like

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Combine competition with character growth
- ✅ Tactics professional and credible
- ✅ MC has failures and growth
- ✅ Teammates have independent personalities
