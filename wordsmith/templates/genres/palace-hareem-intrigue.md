# Palace/Hareem Intrigue Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Rule gaming + Mind games + Status advancement. Navigating carefully in the deep mansion, changing fate with wisdom.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F05
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Imperial Harem Competition Flow
- **Feature**: Imperial concubines fighting, competing for emperor's favor
- **Core Satisfaction**: Outsmart opponents, gain imperial favor, status rise
- **Representative Works**: 《Zhen Huan Zhuan》《Ru Yi Zhuan Quan》《Yan Xi Gong Lue》

### Mansion Internal Struggle Flow
- **Feature**: Great family internal struggles, legitimate vs illegitimate contention
- **Core Satisfaction**: Counterattack framings, control family power, successful revenge
- **Representative Works**: 《Zhi Mo Zhi Zhi Ying Shi Lue Fei Hong Shou》《Jin Xin Si Yu》

### Political Palace Intrigue Flow
- **Feature**: Court politics, power gaming
- **Core Satisfaction**: Political layout, bring down opponents, power in court
- **Representative Works**: 《Lang Ya Bang》《Qing Yan Nian》

### Rebirth Revenge Flow
- **Feature**: Rebirth back to the past, change tragic fate
- **Core Satisfaction**: Foresight, revenge face-slapping, reverse life
- **Representative Works**: 《Chong Sheng Zhi Di Nv Huo Sha》《Shu Nu Gong Lue》

## 2. Worldview: Palace/Mansion Sociology

### Imperial Harem Levels (Qing Dynasty Version)
| Level | Title | Number | Power |
|-------|-------|--------|-------|
| First | Empress | 1 | Lead the harem |
| Second | Imperial Noble Consort | 1 | Vice-empress |
| Third | Noble Consort | 2 | High-ranking consort |
| Fourth | Consort | 4 | One palace master |
| Fifth | Concubine | 6 | Has title |
| Sixth | Noble Lady | Unlimited | Low rank |
| Seventh | Attendant/Waiting Lady | Unlimited | Lowest |

### Mansion Levels
| Identity | Status | Power |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Old mistress | Highest | Final authority |
| Family matriarch | Manager | Control internal affairs |
| Legitimate son/daughter | Orthodox | Inheritance rights |
| Illegitimate son/daughter | Secondary | Limited rights |
| Concubine/Second wife | Low | Dependent on matriarch |
| Maid/servant | Lowest | No power |

### Resource System
- **Favor**: Most core resource, determines status
- **Money**: Bribe relationships, buy hearts
- **Connections**: Connections inside palace/mansion
- **Intelligence**: Key to knowing yourself and enemy
- **Children**: Biggest bargaining chip

## 3. Struggle Tactics Design

### Overt Tactics
| Tactic | Feature | Risk |
|--------|---------|------|
| Compete for favor | Direct competition | Make enemies |
| Report | Use strength against opponent | Backfire |
| Alliance | Band together | Betrayal |
| Show weakness | Hide abilities | Being bullied |

### Covert Tactics
| Tactic | Feature | Risk |
|--------|---------|------|
| Frame-up | Plant evidence | Exposed |
| Poison | Secret harm | Discovered |
| Bribery | Recruit opponent's person | Double agent |
| Rumor | Public opinion attack | Backfire |

### Defense Tactics
- **Test for poison**: Prevent being poisoned
- **Informant**: Monitor enemy movements
- **Backer**: Find protector
- **Leverage**: Hold opponent's weakness

---

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Counterattack Framing
- **Scene**: Being framed by someone, evidence solid
- **Reversal**: MC had long prepared, turns the tables
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, can't appear out of nowhere

### Intellectual Face-Slap
- **Scene**: Opponent publicly makes things difficult
- **Satisfaction**: MC responds cleverly, leaves opponent with no way out
- **Key**: Must match identity, can't be too far

### Status Rise
- **Scene**: Rise from low to high position
- **Satisfaction**: Those who looked down on MC now must bow
- **Key**: Rise must have foreshadowing, can't be too abrupt

### Revenge Success
- **Scene**: Launch revenge against those who harmed her
- **Satisfaction**: Let opponent taste the pain she once suffered
- **Key**: Revenge must have measure, can't be too cruel

## 5. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Enter the Game (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: MC enters palace/mansion, understand rules
- **Status**: Low position
- **Climax**: First crisis resolved, gain foothold

### Volume 2: Footing (Chapters 51-120)
- **Core**: Build relationship network, gain initial status
- **Status**: Medium-low position
- **Climax**: Defeat first opponent, slight promotion

### Volume 3: Rise (Chapters 121-200)
- **Core**: Involved in core struggles
- **Status**: Medium position
- **Climax**: Bring down main opponent, major promotion

### Volume 4: Peak (Chapters 201-280)
- **Core**: Compete for highest position
- **Status**: High position
- **Climax**: Become final winner

### Volume 5: Wrap Up (Chapters 281-350)
- **Core**: Handle remaining issues, ending
- **Status**: Highest position
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 6. Character Relationship Design

### Core Relationships
| Relationship | Function | Development |
|--------------|----------|-------------|
| Male lead | Romance line | From stranger to love |
| Best friend | Help | May betray |
| Opponent | Conflict | From enemy to friend/defeated |
| Elder | Backer/obstacle | Gain recognition |
| Servant | Intelligence/execution | Loyalty cultivation |

### Opponent Design
| Type | Feature | Ending |
|------|---------|--------|
| Stupid evil type | Arrogant and domineering | Face-slapped early |
| Scheming type | Surface kind | Exposed mid-game |
| Strong enemy type | Powerful | Defeated late game |
| Tragic type | Has difficulties | May reconcile |

## 7. Palace/Mansion Intrigue Rules

### Core Rules
- **Order matters**: Lower cannot offend higher
- **Legitimate vs illegitimate clear**: Legitimate birth higher status than illegitimate
- **Child honors mother**: Having children stabilizes status
- **Favor is king**: Those in favor have say

### Forbidden Lines
- **Kill master**: Killing master is death penalty
- **Adultery**: Secret affairs with men outside is death penalty
- **Treason**: Participation in treason affects nine families
- **Witchcraft**: Using sorcery is major taboo

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ All opponents are idiots
- ❌ MC has too many cheats
- ❌ Male lead blindly dotes on female lead
- ❌ Struggle tactics repeat

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Opponents have IQ
- ✅ MC wins through wisdom
- ✅ Romance line has twists
- ✅ Struggle tactics diverse
