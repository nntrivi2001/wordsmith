# War Espionage Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Identity game + Information warfare + High-pressure survival. Walking on the blade's edge behind enemy lines, protecting faith with wisdom and courage.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M10
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Underground Lurker Flow
- **Feature**: Long-term lurking in enemy camp, gathering intelligence
- **Core Satisfaction**: Identity maneuvering, winning in danger, key intelligence
- **Representative Works**: 《Qian Fu》《Feng Zheng》《Wei Zhuang Zhe》《Yao Ga》

### Anti-Japanese Behind Enemy Lines Flow
- **Feature**: Guerrilla warfare behind enemy lines, striking enemies
- **Core Satisfaction**: Tactical crush, winning against odds, national righteousness
- **Representative Works**: 《Liang Jian》《Xue Bao》《Wo De Tuan Zhang Wo De Tuan》

### Intelligence Warfare Flow
- **Feature**: Game between intelligence agencies
- **Core Satisfaction**: Intellectual duel, code breaking, counter-espionage
- **Representative Works**: 《An Suan》《Jie Mi》《Feng Sheng》

### Special Agent Action Flow
- **Feature**: Execute special missions
- **Core Satisfaction**: Thrilling, completing impossible missions
- **Representative Works**: 《Ma Que》《Yan Zhi》《Qi Pao》

## 3. Espionage System Design

### Intelligence Work
```
Intelligence collection:
- Interpersonal infiltration
- Technical theft
- Defection recruitment

Intelligence transmission:
- Encrypted telegram
- Dead drop
- Courier

Intelligence analysis:
- Code breaking
- Intelligence verification
- Strategic judgment
```

### Identity Management
| Element | Description | Risk Point |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Public identity | Cover identity | Being investigated |
| Social relations | Interpersonal network | Being implicated |
| Behavioral habits | Daily performance | Revealing flaws |
| Emotional relations | Love/family | Being exploited |

### Counter-reconnaissance Measures
```
Daily prevention:
- Check for surveillance
- Clear traces
- Establish signals

Emergency response:
- Emergency evacuation
- Destroy evidence
- Activate backup identity
```

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Winning in Danger
- **Scene**: Identity about to be exposed, desperate escape
- **Satisfaction**: Thrilling, turning danger into safety
- **Key**: Must have reasonable escape method

### Intellectual Battle with Enemy Agents
- **Scene**: Wits and courage battles with enemy agents
- **Satisfaction**: Intellectual crush, opponent falls
- **Key**: Both sides must have IQ

### Key Intelligence
- **Scene**: Obtaining intelligence that changes the war situation
- **Satisfaction**: Tremendous intelligence value, far-reaching impact
- **Example**: Obtaining enemy battle plans

### Successful Recruitment
- **Scene**: Successfully recruit enemy personnel
- **Satisfaction**: Turn enemy into friend, expand results
- **Key**: Recruitment process must be reasonable

## 5. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Infiltration (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: Establish identity, gain foothold
- **Status**: Peripheral personnel
- **Climax**: First important mission

### Volume 2: Rooting (Chapters 61-150)
- **Core**: Deepen into enemy camp, gain trust
- **Status**: Core personnel
- **Climax**: Obtain important intelligence

### Volume 3: Crisis (Chapters 151-250)
- **Core**: Identity under suspicion
- **Status**: Being investigated
- **Climax**: Resolve crisis

### Volume 4: Counterattack (Chapters 251-350)
- **Core**: Take initiative, strike enemies
- **Status**: Key piece
- **Climax**: Major action success

### Volume 5: Victory (Chapters 351-400)
- **Core**: Victory arrives, identity revealed
- **Status**: Hero
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 6. Character Design

### MC Types
| Type | Feature | Advantage |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Calm enduring type | Calm and composed | Doesn't reveal flaws |
| Clever flexible type | Adaptable | Resolve crises |
| Steadfast faith type | Strong will | Unshakeable |
| Double-life type | Superb acting | Perfect disguise |

### Core Supporting Characters
| Character | Function | Relationship with MC |
|-----------|----------|---------------------|
| Handler | Command | Trust/test |
| Comrade | Coordination | Life and death together |
| Enemy agent | Opponent | Battle of wits |

### Villain Design
| Type | Feature | Threat |
|------|---------|--------|
| Cunning agent | Experienced | Hard to deceive |
| Traitor | Knows inside information | Deadly threat |

## 7. Creating Era Atmosphere

### Historical Background
| Period | Feature | Core Contradiction |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| Early war | Full resistance | National survival |
| Mid war | Stalemate | Behind-enemy struggle |
| Late war | Victory in sight | Compete for victory fruits |

### Scene Design
```
Daily scenes:
- Ballroom/coffee house
- Office
- Streets and alleys

Tense scenes:
- Interrogation room
- Secret meeting
- Pursuit and escape

Climax scenes:
- Intelligence handover
- Identity reveal
- Life and death choice
```

### Detail Elements
- Era clothing (cheongsam, Zhongshan suit)
- Communication methods (telegraph, codes)
- Transportation (rickshaw, car)
- Social scenery (concession, occupied areas)

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Excessive melodrama, unrealistic
- ❌ Villain loses IQ
- ❌ MC's protagonist halo too strong
- ❌ Historical common sense errors

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Restrained but powerful emotions
- ✅ Villain has IQ
- ✅ MC has crises and costs
- ✅ Historical details accurate
