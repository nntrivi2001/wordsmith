# Esports Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Comeback from behind + Arena pressure + Team growth. Driven by "competition results" for intense rhythm and continuous following.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M12
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Professional League Flow
- **Feature**: Youth team → secondary league → top league progression path
- **Core Satisfaction**: Comeback after losing streak, key round reversal, MVP deification
- **Adaptation**: Long serial, team ensemble

### Genius Newcomer Flow
- **Feature**: Highly talented newcomer challenges old powerful teams
- **Core Satisfaction**: Stunning debut, matchup domination, public opinion reversal
- **Adaptation**: Male audience satisfying, fast-paced chapters

### Tactical Coach Flow
- **Feature**: Focus on BP, tactical gaming, data analysis
- **Core Satisfaction**: Win against weaker, strange formation breaks situation, on-the-spot command
- **Adaptation**: Intelligence battles, hardcore audience

### Retired Comeback Flow
- **Feature**: Return to arena after injury/banned/mental breakdown
- **Core Satisfaction**: Desperate return, rival rematch, championship redemption
- **Adaptation**: Strong emotional line, character arc

## 2. Worldview: Competition System

### League Structure
| Level | Description | Common Conflicts |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| Youth training camp | Newcomer training | Selection elimination, resource tilt |
| Secondary league | Advancement springboard | Promotion/relegation, sponsorship pressure |
| Top league | Highest stage | Encirclement by top teams, playoff life and death |
| International | Peak confrontation | Region rivalry, version understanding difference |

### Team Positions
- **Main C**: High risk high return, bear firepower output
- **Rhythm position**: Map control and team fight core, affect overall tempo
- **Shot caller**: Decision and on-the-spot response, decide key rounds
- **Support position**: Resource allocation and protection, stabilize team floor

## 3. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Reverse 2-3
- **Scene**: 0:2 behind, mentality on brink of collapse
- **Satisfaction**: Tactical adjustment succeeds, win three consecutive rounds
- **Key**: Each reversal logic must be clear, can't "force win"

### Rival Matchup
- **Scene**: MC directly faces former teammate/old rival
- **Satisfaction**: Matchup domination, classic play moments, post-game public opinion explosion
- **Key**: Historical grievances must be foreshadowed early

### Coach Breaks Situation
- **Scene**: Conventional methods countered, team trapped in dead end
- **Satisfaction**: On-the-spot formation change, strange trick wins
- **Key**: Tactical description "understandable but not wordy"

### Championship Moment
- **Scene**: Finals final round
- **Satisfaction**: Key team fight and personal limit plays
- **Key**: Victory cost must be real (injury, mental pressure, team sacrifice)

- **Climax**: First key victory

- **Climax**: Reverse 2-3 classic moment

### Volume 3: Break Circle (Chapters 181-320)

- **Climax**: Rival matchup reversal

### Volume 4: Reach Summit (Chapters 321-520)

- **Climax**: Final round championship

### Volume 5: New Dynasty (Chapters 521-700)
- **Core**: Defend championship, rebuild, train newcomers
- **Climax**: Inheritance and closure

## 5. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Competition process "only report results, don't write gaming"
- ❌ Characters only shout slogans, lack tactical execution details
- ❌ Team long winning streak without cost
- ❌ Post-game public opinion and commercial reality completely absent

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Each competition at least clearly writes 1 key decision point
- ✅ Victory or defeat jointly decided by "information, execution, mentality" three factors
- ✅ Victory accompanied by cost, defeat brings concrete consequences
- ✅ Pre-competition-competition-post form complete emotional chain
