# Live-Streaming Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Real-time interaction + Public opinion reversal + Commercial monetization. Using "audience feedback" to drive immediate satisfaction.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M08
- Universal Overlay: Pack U03

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### E-commerce Counterattack Flow
- **Feature**: From small streamer to top streamer, break through with product understanding and pitch
- **Core Satisfaction**: Single livestream explosive orders, bet reversal, brand proactively seeks cooperation
- **Adaptation**: Urban growth, realistic entrepreneurship

### Talent Competition Flow
- **Feature**: Singing/dancing/talking and other track competitions
- **Core Satisfaction**: Live performance deification, full platform hot search, slap face doubters
- **Adaptation**: Light rhythm, strong interaction

### Store Exploration Documentary Flow
- **Feature**: Offline store exploration + industry insider exposure
- **Core Satisfaction**: Real vs fake evaluation, rights protection counterattack, public opinion reversal
- **Adaptation**: Realistic theme, social issues

### Rich Fan Gift Flow
- **Feature**: Gift rankings, guard rankings, livestream room battles
- **Core Satisfaction**: Rankings sprint, PK reversal, hidden big brother identity revealed
- **Adaptation**: High satisfaction, emotional pull

---

## 2. Worldview: Platform Ecology

### Traffic Levels
| Level | Feature | Conflict Source |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| Cold start | Few recommendations, low exposure | Account anxiety |
| Growth period | Stable audience growth | Content homogenization |
| Outbreak period | Traffic surge | Black fans and trends |
| Top period | Deep commercialization | Contracts, public opinion, team management |

### Key Roles
- **Streamer**: Core output and persona carrier
- **Operation/editing**: Traffic amplifier
- **Brand**: Commercial cooperation and price negotiation
- **Audience**: Three types of interaction - supporters, passersby, black fans

---

## 3. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Livestream Crash and Counterattack
- **Scene**: Opponent sets trap for defamation, livestream room trend out of control
- **Satisfaction**: Release evidence on the spot to reverse, full network changes attitude
- **Key**: Evidence chain must foreshadow in advance

### Ranking Surge
- **Scene**: PK last 5 minutes behind
- **Satisfaction**: Tactical voting + key help, spectacular counterattack
- **Key**: Process must have rhythm peaks, can't steamroll in one go

### Brand Gaming
- **Scene**: Big brand suppresses price or shifts blame
- **Satisfaction**: Streamer counteracts with data and reputation, reversely increases bargaining power
- **Key**: Business logic must be realistic

### Account Rebirth
- **Scene**: Return after account banned/public opinion collapse
- **Satisfaction**: New account cold start breaks circle, top again
- **Key**: Return must have differentiated positioning

## 4. Outline Structure (1.5 Million Words Reference)

### Volume 1: Account Setup (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: Complete account positioning, get first wave of stable audience
- **Climax**: Single livestream small explosion

### Volume 2: Break Circle (Chapters 61-160)
- **Core**: Content upgrade + platform recommendation, enter competition track
- **Climax**: Rankings counterattack

### Volume 3: Attack and Defense (Chapters 161-300)
- **Core**: Deal with black fans, PR crisis and team conflicts
- **Climax**: Public opinion reversal battle

### Volume 4: Commercialization (Chapters 301-460)
- **Core**: Brand cooperation, supply chain, team expansion
- **Climax**: Commercial bet victory

### Volume 5: New Stage (Chapters 461-620)
- **Core**: From individual streamer to content company
- **Climax**: New track deification again

---

## 5. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Livestream ecology disconnected from reality, data all "heaven-sent traffic"
- ❌ Audience only as backdrop, don't participate in plot pushing
- ❌ Every chapter is arguing, no career line progress
- ❌ Public opinion reversal has no evidence and foreshadowing

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Each chapter reflects at least 1 "audience feedback" impact on decision
- ✅ Form closed loop: before livestream-during livestream-after livestream
- ✅ Before key reversals bury 2-3 recyclable evidence points
- ✅ Commercial conflicts and character emotional lines progress in parallel
