# Female Lead Mystery Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Logical reasoning + Human emotions + Relationship reversals. Uncovering truth in the fog of intimate relationships with meticulous observation.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F13
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Emotional Mystery Flow
- **Feature**: Mystery with emotional relationships as core
- **Core Satisfaction**: Relationship truth, trust collapse, emotional reversal
- **Representative Works**: 《Xiao Shi De Ta》《Bu Wan Mei Shou Hai Ren》《Hui Lai De Nu Er》

### Family Mystery Case Flow
- **Feature**: Family internal secrets and lies
- **Core Satisfaction**: Family secret, identity mystery, family affection reversal
- **Representative Works**: 《Yin Bi De Jiao Luo》《Xiao Min Jia》《Dou Tai Hao》

### Workplace Mystery Flow
- **Feature**: Conspiracy and truth under workplace background
- **Core Satisfaction**: Workplace conspiracy, truth reveal, counterattack revenge
- **Representative Works**: 《Nv Xin Li Shi》《Di Xian》《Mei Gui De Gu Shi》

### Revenge Deduction Flow
- **Feature**: Approaching with purpose, uncover past truth
- **Core Satisfaction**: Revenge success, truth revealed, justice served
- **Representative Works**: 《Hui Lang Ting》《Ba Jiao Ting Mi Wu》《Jiang Zhao Li Ming》

## 2. Worldview: Female Mystery Sociology

### Relationship Types
| Relationship | Feature | Mystery Point |
|--------------|---------|---------------|
| Husband/wife | Most intimate | Trust crisis |
| Parent-child | Blood bond | Identity secret |
| Best friend | Emotional dependence | Betrayal truth |
| Mother-in-law/daughter-in-law | Natural opposition | Family conspiracy |
| Coworker | Interest relationship | Workplace frame-up |

### Women's Predicaments
| Predicament | Expression | Mystery Treatment |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Marriage predicament | Husband cheating/domestic violence | Disappear/revenge |
| Workplace predicament | Sexual harassment/suppression | Collect evidence |
| Family predicament | Family of origin harm | Uncover truth |
| Identity predicament | Identity mystery | Find answer |

### Clue Types
| Type | Feature | Acquisition Method |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Physical evidence | Objectively exists | Search/discover |
| Witness testimony | Subjective statement | Dialogue/observation |
| Emotional clue | Relationship changes | Meticulous perception |
| Memory fragment | Past truth | Recall/trigger |

## 3. Mystery Design

### Puzzle Types
| Type | Core Question | Reveal Method |
|------|--------------|---------------|
| Identity mystery | Who is he/she | Layer by layer |
| Motive mystery | Why did they do this | Emotional reveal |
| Relationship mystery | What is the true relationship | Reversal reveal |
| Past mystery | What happened | Backtrack truth |

### Clue Burial
```
First layer (Appearance):
- Superficially harmonious relationships
- Seemingly normal daily life
- Slight sense of incongruity

Second layer (Doubt points):
- Unreasonable details
- Contradictory statements
- Hidden emotions

Third layer (Truth):
- True face of relationships
- Hidden past
- Real motive
```

### Reversal Design
| Reversal Type | Effect | Foreshadowing Requirement |
|---------------|--------|--------------------------|
| Identity reversal | Shock | Sufficient foreshadowing |
| Relationship reversal | Heartbreak | Emotional foreshadowing |
| Motive reversal | Understanding | Background explanation |
| Ending reversal | Unexpected | Logic self-consistency |

---

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Truth Reveal
- **Scene**: Truth revealed after layers of fog
- **Satisfaction**: Suddenly enlightened, foreshadowing paid off
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, logic self-consistency

### Relationship Reversal
- **Scene**: The most trusted person is actually the enemy
- **Satisfaction**: Shock, heartbreak, anger
- **Key**: Emotional foreshadowing must be sufficient

### Revenge Success
- **Scene**: Revenge against those who hurt her
- **Satisfaction**: Justice served, deeply satisfying
- **Key**: Revenge must have bottom line

### Self-Redemption
- **Scene**: Walking out of predicament, gaining new life
- **Satisfaction**: Growth and transformation, independent and strong
- **Key**: Must have growth process

## 5. Outline Structure (800K Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Suspicion (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: Discover anomaly, generate suspicion
- **Status**: Confused/uneasy
- **Climax**: First major discovery

### Volume 2: Investigation (Chapters 51-120)
- **Core**: Collect clues, approach truth
- **Status**: Determined/dangerous
- **Climax**: Discover key evidence

### Volume 3: Crisis (Chapters 121-200)
- **Core**: Truth about to be revealed, dangers everywhere
- **Status**: Tense/confrontation
- **Climax**: Identity/relationship reversal

### Volume 4: Truth (Chapters 201-280)
- **Core**: Truth revealed, justice served
- **Status**: Relieved/growth
- **Climax**: Final confrontation

### Volume 5: New Life (Chapters 281-350)
- **Core**: Walk out of shadow, start new life
- **Status**: Calm/hope
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 7. Female Mystery Characteristics

### Balance of Emotion and Deduction
```
Emotional line (40%):
- Relationship development
- Emotional conflict
- Inner growth

Deduction line (40%):
- Clue collection
- Logical deduction
- Truth reveal

Atmosphere line (20%):
- Mystery atmosphere
- Tension
- Emotional rendering
```

### Female Perspective Characteristics
- Meticulous emotional description
- Subtle changes in relationships
- Real presentation of women's predicaments
- Growth and self-redemption

### Rhythm Control
| Stage | Emotion | Mystery |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Opening | Daily life | Bury foreshadowing |
| Development | Shaken | Throw doubt points |
| Climax | Collapse | Reveal truth |
| Ending | Rebuild | Wrap up |

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Only talk about feelings, no deduction
- ❌ Unfair clues
- ❌ Reversals too deliberate
- ❌ Female lead too naive

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Emotion and deduction progress simultaneously
- ✅ Fair and traceable clues
- ✅ Reversals have foreshadowing
- ✅ Female lead has growth and wisdom
