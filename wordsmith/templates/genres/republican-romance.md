# Republic Era Romance Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Era turbulence + Hidden identity + Love and hate entanglement. Writing deeply memorable love legends in the turbulence of troubled times.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F10
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Wealthy Family Grudge Flow
- **Feature**: Love and hate in great families of Republican era
- **Core Satisfaction**: Family struggles, identity reversal, deep love and hate
- **Representative Works**: 《Jin Fei Shi Jia》《Jing Hua Yan Yun》《Ban Sheng Yuan》《Qing Cheng Zhi Lian》

### Troubled Times Beauty Flow
- **Feature**: Love under war background
- **Core Satisfaction**: Staying together through war, life and death, national sentiment
- **Representative Works**: 《Ren Jian Zheng Dao Shi Cang Sang》《Bei Ping Wu Zhan Shi》《Wei Zhuang Zhe》

### Famous Courtesan Glamour Flow
- **Feature**: Legendary life of Republican era socialites
- **Core Satisfaction**: Independent woman, era elegance, love choices
- **Representative Works**: 《Shang Hai Tan》《Qing Shen Shen Yu Meng Meng》《Xiang Wu Xiang Yu You Xiang Feng》

### Spy Shadow Deep Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Love under spy war background
- **Core Satisfaction**: Identity secrets, trust test, life and death dependence
- **Representative Works**: 《Ma Que》《Yan Zhi》《Qi Pao》《Wei Zhuang Zhe》

## 2. Worldview: Republican Era Sociology

### Social Classes
| Class | Feature | Life State |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Warlord/official | Power core | Luxurious/dangerous |
| Great families | Traditional forces | Particular/conservative |
| New capital | Business new rich | Open/profit-seeking |
| Intellectuals | Thought vanguard | Idealistic/poor |
| Common people | Social foundation | Difficult/simple |

### Era Characteristics
| Period | Feature | Core Contradiction |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| Beiyang period | Warlord warfare | Power struggle |
| Golden decade | Relatively stable | New-old conflict |
| War period | National peril | Choice of country or family |
| Pre-liberation | Turbulent | Fate choice |

### Women's Situations
| Type | Feature | Predicament |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Noble daughter | Strict upbringing | Marriage not autonomous |
| New woman | Pursue independence | Social prejudice |
| Social butterfly | Mix with powers | Not in control |
| Revolutionary woman | Devote to revolution | Life and death test |

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Identity Reversal
- **Scene**: Humble identity is actually noble daughter/revolutionary
- **Satisfaction**: Truth revealed, slap faces everyone
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing

### Staying Together Through War
- **Scene**: Not leaving each other through war
- **Satisfaction**: Life and death dependent, deeply moving
- **Key**: Must have real crisis

### Family Recognition
- **Scene**: Overcoming obstacles to gain family recognition
- **Satisfaction**: Lovers finally united
- **Key**: Must show female lead's highlights

### Revenge Success
- **Scene**: Revenge against those who harmed her
- **Satisfaction**: Justice served, deeply satisfying
- **Key**: Revenge must have bottom line

## 5. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Meeting (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: Fate intertwines, romance sprouts
- **Relationship**: Strangers → Acquaintances
- **Climax**: First heart flutter

### Volume 2: Getting to Know (Chapters 61-150)
- **Core**: Deepen understanding, feelings warm up
- **Relationship**: Acquaintances → Flirting
- **Climax**: Confirm feelings

### Volume 3: Test (Chapters 151-250)
- **Core**: Family/era obstacles
- **Relationship**: Flirting → Crisis
- **Climax**: Forced to separate

### Volume 4: Protection (Chapters 251-350)
- **Core**: Fight against everything for love
- **Relationship**: Crisis → Reunion
- **Climax**: Overcome obstacles

### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 351-400)
- **Core**: Lovers finally united
- **Relationship**: Reunion → Eternity
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 7. Creating Era Feel

### Language Style
- Use Republican era titles (Mrs., Miss, Mr.)
- Properly use classical language
- Avoid modern internet slang

### Life Details
| Aspect | Key Points | Pitfalls |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Clothing | Cheongsam/Western suit | Wrong style |
| Etiquette | East-West blend | Too modern |
| Titles | Era characteristics | Wrong titles |
| Transportation | Rickshaw/car | Transcendent props |

### Major Events
- May Fourth Movement
- Northern Expedition
- Anti-Japanese War
- Liberation War

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Weak era feel
- ❌ Conflicts too melo
- ❌ Female lead too naive
- ❌ Historical common sense errors

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Accurate era details
- ✅ Deep conflicts
- ✅ Female lead has growth
- ✅ Reasonable historical background
