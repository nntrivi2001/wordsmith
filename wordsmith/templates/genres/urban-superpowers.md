# Urban Superpower Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Familiar modern life + Superpower invasion. Finding the extraordinary in the ordinary.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M12 / Pack M05
- Universal Overlay: Pack U03

## 1. Spiritual Energy Awakening Three Stages (The Awakening Eras)

### Hidden Era (The Hidden Era)
- **Status**: Extremely few awakeners, strictly controlled by authorities. Ordinary people unaware of superpower existence.
- **Conflict**: MC awakens, tries to hide identity, dodge official investigation and villain pursuit.
- **Atmosphere**: Mystery, oppressive, urban legends.
- **Hard Rule**: Whenever "ordinary people might notice big disturbance" occurs (explosion/collapse/big fire/multiple injuries/road closure), must provide "real aftermath + concealment mechanism" explanation (complete in this chapter or next).

### Outbreak Era (The Outbreak Era)
- **Status**: Spiritual energy concentration rises, animals and plants mutate, awakeners surge. Secrets can't be hidden forever.
- **Conflict**: Social order turbulent, old and new forces reshuffle. Officials publicly acknowledge superpower existence, establish superpower academies.
- **Atmosphere**: Chaotic, opportunity, hot-blooded.

### New Order Era (The New Order)
- **Status**: Superpowers become normal, technology combined with cultivation. Humanity establishes base cities to fight beasts.
- **Conflict**: Race war (Humanity vs Monster/Other world invaders).
- **Atmosphere**: Epic, apocalyptic, salvation.

---

## 2. Faction Gaming (Factions)

### Official Agencies (The State)
- **Feature**: Order maintainers, strongest resources, most rules.
- **Relationship with MC**: Recruit? Cooperate? Or wanted?
- **Agency names**: Dragon Group, Special Management Bureau, 749 Bureau, Night Watchers.

### Folk Organizations (The Underground)
- **Wealthy clans**: Try to control awakeners with money, research longevity tech.
- **Sects**: Worship other-worldly evil gods, create chaos.
- **Guilds**: Self-help/mercenary organizations formed by awakeners.

---

## 3. Superpower System Design

### Awakening Levels
- **E-level**: Body enhancement, slightly stronger than ordinary people.
- **C-level**: Master elemental/special ability, can counter hot weapons.
- **A-level**: Nation-ruling combat power, one person can fight an army.
- **S-level**: Nuclear deterrence level, walking nuclear bomb.

### Ability Classification
- **Elemental type**: Fire, Thunder, Ice (high output).
- **Enhancement type**: Strength, Speed, Self-healing (strong survivability).
- **Psychic type**: Telekinesis, Hypnosis, Precognition (bizarre).
- **Special type**: Space, Time, Causality (BUG level).

---

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Playing Pig to Eat Tiger
- **Scene**: Thought MC was ordinary student/part-timer, rich second generation/bully comes to cause trouble.
- **Eruption**: MC crushes with one finger, reveals "S-level license" or shows terrifying superpower.

### Stone Gambling/Appraisal (Golden Pupils)
- **Scene**: Antique street, experts misjudge, MC finds hidden treasure.
- **Satisfaction**: Buy item with spiritual energy at extremely low price.

### Campus Beauty Bodyguard
- **Scene**: Ordered to protect young lady/campus beauty, cohabitation life.
- **Satisfaction**: Flirty interaction, deal with pursuers, finally gain both beauty and wealth.

---

## 5. Real Aftermath and Concealment Mechanism (Hidden Era Dedicated)

> **Purpose**: Enhance "world texture", avoid readers complaining "such big explosion nobody cares?".

### Must Deliver 1 "Aftermath Detail"
Select 1-2 from below to implement in plot (give specific wording/time/character reaction, not just one sentence glossed over):
- **Scene processing**: Police line, fire/ambulance, road closure, temporary power outage/seal
- **Trace erasure**: Surveillance footage retrieved, phones asked to delete video, passersby taken for statement
- **Public opinion version**: WeChat groups/short video platform fragmented info + official statement "reasonable explanation" (construction accident/gas explosion/high voltage fire)
- **Superpower world rules**: Special Management/Night Watchers appear, how "ordinary people aware" gets handled

### Writing Tips (Don't Drag Rhythm)
- Use **1 ordinary person's perspective fragment** (driver/security/property/neighbor group chat) + **1 official statement sentence**, enough to nail "hidden era".
