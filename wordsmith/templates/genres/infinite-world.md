# Infinite World Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Level clearing + Rule puzzles + Extreme survival. Grow in death games, break rules with wisdom and courage.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M19
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Horror Dungeon Flow
- **Feature**: Enter horror movie/urban legend worlds, complete tasks to leave
- **Core Satisfaction**: Rule cracking, desperate escape, outsmarting ghosts
- **Representative Works**: 《Wu Xian Kong Bu》《Shen Yuan Gui Tu》《Wo Zai Jing Po You Xi Li Feng Shen》

### Rule Mystery Flow
- **Feature**: Each dungeon has unique rules, violation means death
- **Core Satisfaction**: Discovering rules, using rules, rule counter-kill
- **Representative Works**: 《Gui Mi Zhi Zhu》《Quan Qiu Gao Wu》《Wo You Yi Zuo Kong Bu Wu》

### Team Competition Flow
- **Feature**: Multi-player team dungeon, teammates may be enemies
- **Core Satisfaction**: Team coordination, betrayal reversal, human nature game
- **Representative Works**: 《Da Tao Sha》《Mi Liu Zhi Guo De Ai Li Si》

### God Realm Space Flow
- **Feature**: Has clear god/system management, points exchange for abilities
- **Core Satisfaction**: Ability matching, resource management, cross-world adventure
- **Representative Works**: 《Wu Xian Kong Bu》《Lun Hui Le Yuan》

---

## 2. Worldview: Infinite Space Sociology

### Player Ecology
- **Newbie**: Extremely low survival rate, used as cannon fodder by veterans
- **Mid-level**: Has certain strength, form fixed teams
- **Top players**: Master core rules, can solo high-difficulty dungeons
- **Managers**: Mysterious existences, rule makers

### Resource System
- **Points**: Dungeon completion rewards, used to exchange abilities/items
- **Items**: Single-use consumables, lifesavers at critical moments
- **Skills**: Permanent abilities, need large amounts of points
- **Intelligence**: Dungeon guides, hard currency for trading

### Dungeon Difficulty Rating
- **D-level**: Newbie dungeon, 70% survival rate
- **C-level**: Normal dungeon, 50% survival rate
- **B-level**: Difficult dungeon, 30% survival rate
- **A-level**: Nightmare dungeon, 10% survival rate
- **S-level**: Hell dungeon, 1% survival rate

## 3. Dungeon Design Framework

### Three Dungeon Elements
```
1. Background setting: Basic rules of the dungeon world
2. Victory condition: Clear victory goal
3. Death condition: Punishment for rule violation
```

### Dungeon Structure (Single Dungeon)
```
Entry Phase (10%):
- Teleport into dungeon
- Get initial information/rule hints
- Confirm teammates/enemies

Exploration Phase (30%):
- Collect clues
- Test rule boundaries
- Someone violates rules and dies (demonstrate rule power)

Breakthrough Phase (40%):
- Discover rule loopholes
- Formulate clearing plan
- Accidents during execution

Settlement Phase (20%):
- Complete clearing conditions
- Get rewards
- Return to main space
```

### Classic Dungeon Types
| Type | Feature | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Survival | Survive until time ends | Zombie siege, island survival |
| Puzzle | Find truth/exit | Escape room, haunted house |
| Combat | Defeat BOSS/enemies | Monster hunting, faction war |
| Quest | Complete specific goals | Escort NPC, collect items |
| Plot | Change/complete plot | Movie world, historical dungeon |

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Rule Counter-Kill
- **Scene**: Enemy thinks they mastered the rules, wants to use them to kill MC
- **Reversal**: MC discovers deeper rules, counter-uses them
- **Example**: Enemy says "seeing the ghost means death", MC discovers "closing eyes means not seeing"

### Desperate Reversal
- **Scene**: Team wiped, MC faces BOSS alone
- **Burst**: MC uses accumulated trump cards/discovered loopholes to reverse
- **Key**: Trump cards must have foreshadowing, can't appear out of nowhere

### Intelligence Crush
- **Scene**: MC knows dungeon guide in advance (reborn/intelligence trading)
- **Satisfaction**: Others are still exploring, MC goes straight to the goal
- **Limitation**: Intelligence may be outdated/wrong, can't auto-clear

### Ability Awakening
- **Scene**: At life-and-death moment, MC awakens new ability
- **Satisfaction**: Ability perfectly fits current predicament
- **Limitation**: Awakening requires payment (points/side effects)

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Tutorial Village (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: First dungeon, establish rule recognition
- **Growth**: From newbie to qualified player
- **Climax**: Barely win first dungeon, get core ability

### Volume 2: Team Formation Period (Chapters 51-150)
- **Core**: Join/form fixed team
- **Growth**: Learn team coordination, ability system takes shape
- **Climax**: Team's first challenge of B-level dungeon

### Volume 3: Mid-Level Rise (Chapters 151-300)
- **Core**: Rise in player community
- **Growth**: Master multiple abilities, start researching rule essence
- **Climax**: Solo clear A-level dungeon

### Volume 4: Truth Exploration (Chapters 301-500)
- **Core**: Explore the truth of infinite space
- **Growth**: Contact manager-level secrets
- **Climax**: Discover the essence of infinite space

### Volume 5: Final Stage (Chapters 501-700)
- **Core**: Challenge/change infinite space rules
- **Growth**: Become rule maker
- **Climax**: Break infinite space, gain freedom

## 6. Teammate Design

### Classic Teammate Configuration
| Role | Position | Function | Fate |
|------|----------|----------|------|
| Veteran | Mentor | Teach MC rules | Mid-term sacrifice |
| Newbie | Contrast | Show growth | May darken |
| Strategist | Support | Analyze rules | Provide counsel at key moments |
| Fighter | DPS | Direct combat | Exit after highlight |
| Traitor | Reversal | Create conflict | Defeated by MC |

### Teammate Death Rhythm
- **Early**: Cannon fodder teammates die, establish tension
- **Mid**: Important teammates sacrifice, emotional impact
- **Late**: Core teammate crisis, but can be saved

## 7. Ability System Design

### Ability Classification
| Type | Feature | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| Combat | Direct damage | Fire, lightning, enhancement |
| Support | Buff/debuff | Healing, speed up, weaken |
| Exploration | Information acquisition | X-ray, precognition, sensing |
| Special | Rule interference | Time stop, space, causality |

### Ability Acquisition Methods
- **Dungeon Rewards**: Random/choose after clearing
- **Point Exchange**: Buy in main space store
- **Special Opportunities**: Hidden rewards discovered in dungeons
- **Ability Fusion**: Multiple abilities combine for advancement

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Dungeon rules self-contradictory
- ❌ MC ability stacks infinitely to godhood
- ❌ Teammates are tools, die without feeling
- ❌ Dungeons homogenized, skin changes only

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Each dungeon rule is self-consistent
- ✅ Abilities have clear limits and costs
- ✅ Teammates have independent personality and storylines
- ✅ Dungeon types diverse, difficulty progressive
