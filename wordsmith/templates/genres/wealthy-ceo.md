# CEO Romance Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Power gap + Contract relationship + Emotional tugging. Modern interpretation of Cinderella and the prince, choosing between money and true heart.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F07
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Contract Marriage Flow
- **Feature**: Sign contract for some reason, fake drama becomes real
- **Core Satisfaction**: Identity reversal, genuine feelings revealed, contract expiry choice
- **Representative Works**: 《Zong Cai De Ti Shen Qian Qi》《Hao Men Qi Yue: Zong Cai De Mi Mi Qing Ren》

### Marriage Then Love Flow
- **Feature**: Marry first then develop feelings
- **Core Satisfaction**: Love grows over time, cold face warm heart, exclusive love
- **Representative Works**: 《Pei Shao De Qian Yi Xin Niang》《Zong Cai De Tian Jia Meng Qi》

### Wealthy Family Intrigue Flow
- **Feature**: Caught in wealthy family internal struggles
- **Core Satisfaction**: Outsmart opponents, gain footing, counterattack to top
- **Representative Works**: 《Hao Men Sheng Chong》《Ming Men Nuan Hun》

### Revenge Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Approach with purpose, but fell for real
- **Core Satisfaction**: Identity revealed, heart-torturing reversal, broken mirror reunion
- **Representative Works**: 《Zong Cai De Fu Chou Xin Niang》《Hao Men En Yuan》

## 2. Worldview: Wealthy Family Sociology

### Wealthy Family Levels
| Level | Feature | Asset Scale |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Top tycoon | Control industry lifelines | Hundred billion level |
| First-tier wealthy family | Industry leader | Ten billion level |
| Second-tier wealthy family | Regional giant | Billion level |
| New rich | Emerging rich | Hundred million level |
| Ordinary wealthy | Slight assets | Ten million level |

### Wealthy Family Rules
- **Proper match**: Marriage is beneficial arrangement
- **Legitimate vs illegitimate clear**: Illegitimate children low status
- **Inheritance struggle**: Family property is core conflict
- **Face project**: Image more important than truth

### Power Structure
- **Family elders**: Control major power, need to please
- **Legitimate children**: Orthodox heirs
- **Illegitimate/secret children**: Marginal, may darken
- **Marriage targets**: Political bargaining chips
- **Family business**: Carrier of power

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Identity Reversal
- **Scene**: Female lead looked down on actually has surprising identity
- **Satisfaction**: True identity revealed, slap faces everyone
- **Example**: Ridiculed poor relative is secretly wealthy young lady

### Exclusive Protection and Face-Slapping
- **Scene**: Someone bullies female lead
- **Satisfaction**: Male lead decisively protects, publicly face-slaps
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, can't be too abrupt

### Contract Reversal
- **Scene**: Contract expires, should separate
- **Satisfaction**: Male lead unwilling to let go, sincere confession
- **Key**: Emotional development must have foreshadowing

### Wealthy Family Recognition
- **Scene**: Family elders make things difficult
- **Satisfaction**: Female lead conquers elders with strength/genuine feelings
- **Key**: Must show female lead's highlights

## 5. Outline Structure (800K Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Meeting (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: Male and female leads meet, establish contract relationship
- **Relationship**: Strangers → Contract relationship
- **Climax**: Sign contract, start cohabitation

### Volume 2: Flirting (Chapters 51-120)
- **Core**: Daily life together, romance sprouting
- **Relationship**: Contract → Flirting
- **Climax**: First heart flutter/jealousy

### Volume 3: Test (Chapters 121-200)
- **Core**: Feelings encounter test
- **Relationship**: Flirting → Crisis
- **Climax**: Misunderstanding/separation

### Volume 4: Confirmation (Chapters 201-280)
- **Core**: Clear misunderstanding, confirm feelings
- **Relationship**: Crisis → Lovers
- **Climax**: Officially together

### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 281-350)
- **Core**: Gain recognition, achieve success
- **Relationship**: Lovers → Married couple
- **Climax**: Grand wedding

## 6. Contract Design

### Contract Types
| Type | Reason | Duration |
|------|-------|----------|
| Fake marriage | Deal with family | 1-3 years |
| Surrogacy | Need heir | Pregnancy period |
| Lover | Business need | Uncertain |
| Assistant | Work relationship | Contract period |

### Common Contract Terms
- Cannot fall in love with the other party
- Cannot interfere in private life
- Leave with nothing after expiry
- Confidentiality clause

## 7. Conflict Design

### Internal Conflicts
| Conflict | Source | Resolution |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Status gap | Not a proper match | Prove self |
| Contract binding | Cannot develop real feelings | Break contract |
| Self-respect | Don't want to be given things | Independent and strong |

### External Conflicts
| Conflict | Source | Resolution |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Family opposition | Elders | Gain recognition |
| Romantic rival | Ex/pursuer | Firmly choose |
| Business crisis | Company | Face together |
| Identity secret | Past | Truth revealed |

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Forced romance, romance for romance's sake
- ❌ CEO too template, no flaws
- ❌ Female lead too naive
- ❌ Misunderstandings too low-level

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Pain has measure, sweet has sugar
- ✅ CEO has humanized side
- ✅ Female lead has own views and bottom line
- ✅ Misunderstandings reasonable
