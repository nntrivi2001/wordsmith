# Historical Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Grand era background + Power struggle + Scale upgrade. Writing personal legend and era changes in the currents of history.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M08
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Hegemony and Political intrigue Flow
- **Feature**: Chaotic world hegemony, competing for the throne
- **Core Satisfaction**: Strategic crush, recruiting talents, unifying the world
- **Representative Works**: 《Fu Han》《San Guo Zhi Zui Feng Liu》《Han Xiang》《Qin Cou》

### Official Career Advancement Flow
- **Feature**: Officialdom ups and downs, climbing step by step
- **Core Satisfaction**: Political gaming, promotion and face-slapping, power in court
- **Representative Works**: 《Guan Ju Yi Pin》《Zai Chi Tian Xia》《Da Ming Wang Hou》《Shang Pin Han Shi》

### Tech Farming Flow
- **Feature**: Using modern knowledge to change history
- **Core Satisfaction**: Tech crush, administrative construction, rich nation and strong military
- **Representative Works**: 《Xin Song》《Tang Zhuan》《Zhui Xu》《Hui Dao Ming Chao Dang Wang Ye》

### Military Campaign Flow
- **Feature**: Battlefield campaigns, path of famous generals
- **Core Satisfaction**: Tactical crush, winning against odds, achievements at Wolf Wuling Xu
- **Representative Works**: 《Da Tang Shuang Long Zhuan》《Qie Ming》《Ming Chao Na Xie Shi Er》

## 2. Worldview: Historical Sociology

### Era Characteristics
| Era | Feature | Core Contradiction |
|-----|---------|-------------------|
| Qin-Han | Early unified empire | Central vs local |
| Three Kingdoms | Warlord division | Hegemony and unification |
| Sui-Tang | Golden age, openness | Aristocracy vs imperial examination |
| Song | Civil governance, weak military | Internal and external troubles |
| Ming | Concentrated imperial power | Factional internal depletion |
| Qing | Isolation | Reform for survival |

### Social Classes
| Class | Feature | Advancement Path |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| Royal family | Supreme power | Inheritance/usurpation |
| Aristocracy | Clan power | Marriage/meritorious service |
| Scholars | Literati | Imperial examination/recommendation |
| Merchants | Wealthy but no power | Buy official position/attach |
| Peasants | Social foundation | Military merit/reading |

### Power System
| Institution | Function | Power |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Zhongshu Province | Decision | Core |
| Menxia Province | Review | Check and balance |
| Shangshu Province | Execution | Real power |
| Yushitai | Supervision | Impeachment |

## 3. Officialdom System Design

### Official Rank Advancement
```
Imperial examination path:
Scholar → Xiucai → Juren → Jinshi → Hanlin → Six Ministries → Cabinet

Military merit path:
Soldier → Squad leader → Baihu → Qianhu → General → Grand General

Patron path:
Nominate → Scatter official → Actual position → Important position
```

### Political Resources
| Resource | Description | Acquisition Method |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Connections | Same year/township/teacher | Imperial examination/social |
| Reputation | Clean name/achievements | Action/propaganda |
| Faction | Party affiliation | Side-taking/management |
| Financial | Economic foundation | Salary/management |
| Military power | Armed guarantee | Military merit/appointment |

### Court Gaming
```
Information warfare (30%):
- Intelligence gathering
- Public opinion guidance
- Message blocking

Personnel warfare (40%):
- Recruit allies
- Strike opponents
- Place trusted personnel

Policy warfare (30%):
- Propose policies
- Obstruct opponents
- Compete for credit
```

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Imperial Examination Counterattack
- **Scene**: Humble scholar passes top examination
- **Satisfaction**: Face-slap aristocrats, one day of fame
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, show effort

### Battlefield Deity
- **Scene**: Key battle winning against odds
- **Satisfaction**: Tactical crush, fame throughout the world
- **Example**: Using clever strategy to defeat strong enemy

### Court Reversal
- **Scene**: Counterattack after impeachment/frame-up
- **Satisfaction**: Evidence solid, opponent falls
- **Key**: Must have layout, can't be too coincidental

### Recruiting Talents
- **Scene**: Historical famous persons rally to MC
- **Satisfaction**: Talent gathering, everyone supports
- **Key**: Must have reasonable attraction point

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Entering the Game (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: MC enters the historical stage
- **Status**: Bottom/peripheral
- **Climax**: First showing talent, gaining foothold

### Volume 2: Rise (Chapters 81-200)
- **Core**: Accumulate strength, expand influence
- **Status**: Mid-level official/local force
- **Climax**: Obtain important position/territory

### Volume 3: Contending (Chapters 201-400)
- **Core**: Direct confrontation with main opponents
- **Status**: Major official/warlord
- **Climax**: Defeat main opponent

### Volume 4: Hegemony (Chapters 401-600)
- **Core**: Compete for world domination
- **Status**: Top-tier force
- **Climax**: Decisive battle/political change

### Volume 5: Establishing (Chapters 601-700)
- **Core**: Unify/reform, establish new order
- **Status**: Supreme power
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 7. Creating Historical Feel

### Language Style
- Use classical language but not obscure
- Official titles must be accurate
- Avoid modern internet slang

### Detail Research
| Aspect | Key Points | Pitfalls |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Clothing | Match the era | Crown wrong |
| Food | Reasonable ingredients | Transcendent food |
| Etiquette | Order of respect | Etiquette collapse |
| Titles | Accurate official titles | Wrong titles |

### Utilizing Major Events
- Use historical events to push plot
- MC participates but doesn't dominate
- Maintain historical general trend unchanged (unless alternate history)

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Too many historical errors
- ❌ Modern thinking too heavy
- ❌ Leveling up too fast and smooth
- ❌ Supporting characters tool-ized

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Basic historical facts accurate
- ✅ Thinking matches the era
- ✅ Has setbacks and costs
- ✅ Supporting characters have flesh and blood
