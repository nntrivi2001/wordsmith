# Western Fantasy Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Divine-demons system + Knight adventure + Contract cost. Writing mortal epic in the gap between gods and demons.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M22
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Knight Adventure Flow
- **Feature**: Classic D&D-style adventure, team formation to kill monsters and level up
- **Core Satisfaction**: Team coordination, equipment collection, BOSS battles
- **Representative Works**: 《Wang Ling Fa Shi》《Wu Shi Shi Jie》《Fa Shi Ao Yi》

### Magic Academy Flow
- **Feature**: Enter magic school to study, campus + adventure
- **Core Satisfaction**: Talent awakening, academy competition, master inheritance
- **Representative Works**: 《Wu Shi Shi Jie》《Ao Shu Shen Zuo》《Xue Ba De Hei Ke Ji Xi Tong》

### Theocratic Church Flow
- **Feature**: Gods truly exist, church controls the world
- **Core Satisfaction**: Faith gaming, divine authority confrontation, truth revealed
- **Representative Works**: 《Gui Mi Zhi Zhu》《Sheng Xu》《Shen Mi Fu Su》

### Lord Management Flow
- **Feature**: Obtain territory, develop faction, compete for the continent
- **Core Satisfaction**: Territory building, talent recruitment, war command
- **Representative Works**: 《Ling Zhu Shi Dai》《Cong Ling Kai Shi》《Ling Zhu De Rong Yao》

### Dark Epic Flow
- **Feature**: Cruel worldview, gray morality, fate struggle
- **Core Satisfaction**: Desperate survival, moral choices, epic battles
- **Representative Works**: 《Bing Yu Huo Zhi Ge》《Lie Mo Ren》《Hei An Zhi Hun》

## 2. Worldview: Western Fantasy Sociology

### Racial System
| Race | Feature | Positioning |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Human | Adaptable, fast breeding | Main race |
| Elf | Long-lived, magic affinity | Noble but arrogant |
| Dwarf | Craftsmanship talent, tough | Forge master |
| Orc | Strong power, tribal system | Barbarian/enemy |
| Dragon | Top combat power, rare | BOSS/ally |
| Demon | Contract deals, corruption | Temptor |

### Class Structure
```
Gods/God's messengers
    ↓
Pope/Archbishop
    ↓
King/Grand Duke
    ↓
Nobles/Knights
    ↓
Merchants/Craftsmen
    ↓
Peasants/Slaves
```

### Power Structure
- **Church**: Divine authority spokesperson, controls faith and some secular power
- **Kingdom**: Secular rulers, both cooperating and confronting church
- **Magic tower**: Magic research institution, relatively independent
- **Adventurer guild**: Mercenary organization, mission broker
- **Dark forces**: Heretical cults/Demons/Undead, main enemies

## 3. Magic System Design

### Magic Levels (Standard Version)
| Level | Title | Ability | Social Status |
|-------|-------|--------|---------------|
| 1-ring | Apprentice | Basic spells | Ordinary |
| 3-ring | Full mage | Battle spells | Respected |
| 5-ring | High mage | Domain spells | Noble treatment |
| 7-ring | Archmage | Forbidden spells | National teacher level |
| 9-ring | Legendary mage | Change rules | One nation's power |

### Magic Schools
| School | Feature | Cost |
|--------|---------|------|
| Elemental | Fire/ice/thunder/wind | Mental consumption |
| Necromancy | Control undead | Life force/sanity |
| Summoning | Summon creatures | Contract cost |
| Enchantment | Enhance items | Material consumption |
| Divine magic | Bestowed by gods | Piety |
| Blood magic | Blood as medium | Life/blood |

### Magic Costs (Important)
- **Mental power**: Casting consumption, recoverable
- **Life force**: Powerful spell cost, hard to recover
- **Sanity**: Contacting forbidden knowledge, may go mad
- **Contract binding**: Deal with higher beings, has cost

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Bloodline Awakening
- **Scene**: MC discovers special bloodline (dragon/demon/divine descendant)
- **Satisfaction**: Talent crush, bloodline ability unique
- **Key**: Bloodline must have cost or limitation

### Inheritance Obtaining
- **Scene**: Discover ruins of ancient archmage/knight
- **Satisfaction**: Obtain powerful inheritance, strength leap
- **Key**: Inheritance must unlock gradually, can't reach peak instantly

### Contract Summoning
- **Scene**: Sign contract with powerful being
- **Satisfaction**: Obtain powerful helper/ability
- **Key**: Contract must have cost, can't get free benefits

### Identity Reversal
- **Scene**: Person looked down on is actually surprisingly powerful
- **Satisfaction**: True identity revealed, slap faces everyone
- **Example**: Wandering knight is prince of lost kingdom

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Origin (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: MC awakens/enrolls, establish basic abilities
- **Map**: Academy/small town
- **Climax**: First show talent, defeat same-level strong enemy

### Volume 2: Training (Chapters 81-180)
- **Core**: Go out for adventure, form team
- **Map**: Within kingdom
- **Climax**: Complete major mission, gain recognition

### Volume 3: Rise (Chapters 181-350)
- **Core**: Involved in major faction gaming
- **Map**: Multi-kingdom range
- **Climax**: Become core of a faction

### Volume 4: Contend (Chapters 351-550)
- **Core**: Participate in continental-level conflicts
- **Map**: Continental range
- **Climax**: Defeat main enemy, reveal truth

### Volume 5: Divine War (Chapters 551-700)
- **Core**: Confront gods/supreme beings
- **Map**: Multiple planes
- **Climax**: Become god/change world rules

## 6. Adventure Team Configuration

### Classic Five-Man Team
| Role | Class | Function | Personality |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| MC | Mage/Knight | Core damage | Growth type |
| Warrior | Knight/Fighter | Front tank | Loyal and reliable |
| Priest | Cleric | Healing support | Gentle/serious |
| Rogue | Assassin/Ranger | Scout/trap | Clever and cunning |
| Mage | Element/Summon | Back row DPS | Scholarly |

### Team Development
- **Early**: Temporary team formation, breaking period
- **Mid**: Fixed team, tacit coordination
- **Late**: Each grows, separating and reuniting

## 7. Gods and Faith

### Divine Design
| Deity | Domain | Worshippers | Attitude |
|-------|-------|-------------|---------|
| God of Light | Order/justice | Church | Order above all |
| God of War | Battle/glory | Knights | Honor the strong |
| God of Knowledge | Magic/wisdom | Mages | Neutral |
| God of Nature | Life/forest | Elves/Druids | Protect nature |
| God of Death | Undead/reincarnation | Necromancers | Fair |
| God of Chaos | Destruction/change | Heretical cults | Hostile |

### Divine Magic Acquisition
- **Devout prayer**: The deeper the faith, the stronger the magic
- **Divine contract**: Sign contract with deity
- **Divine chosen**: Chosen by deity, obtain divine power

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Worldbuilding terms piled up, readers can't understand
- ❌ Race settings copied, no novelty
- ❌ Magic without cost, MC invincible
- ❌ Supporting characters tool-like, no independent personality

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Worldbuilding unfolds gradually, introduce while traveling
- ✅ Race settings have unique features
- ✅ Magic has clear costs and limitations
- ✅ Supporting characters have their own storylines
