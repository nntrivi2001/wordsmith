# Period Drama / Historical Romance Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Ancient charm + Power struggles + Deep love with difficult fate. Expanding love, hate, and power gaming under alternate or real historical backgrounds.

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Palace Drama Flow
- **Feature**: Harem competition, advance steadily, mind games
- **Core Satisfaction**: Outsmart rivals, gain imperial favor, rise step by step
- **Typical Mode**: Low-ranking palace maid → Favorite consort/Empress

### Mansion Drama Flow
- **Feature**: Great family internal struggles, mother-in-law and daughter-in-law, household management
- **Core Satisfaction**: Defeat evil mother-in-law, gain household management, couple united
- **Typical Mode**: New bride → Family matriarch

### Political Intrigue Flow
- **Feature**: Court gaming, country and family, intellectual battles
- **Core Satisfaction**: Strategize, turn the tide, leave name in history
- **Typical Mode**: Caught in political intrigue → Influence court situation

### Sweet Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Sweetness primary, power struggles secondary, sugar overload
- **Core Satisfaction**: Male lead exclusively dotes, others envious, sweet daily life
- **Typical Mode**: Meet → Know → Fall in love → HE

## 2. Classic Plot Framework

### Palace Drama Framework
```
Enter palace → Gain footing → Compete for favor → Crisis → Peak/Exit

Detailed stages:
1. Enter palace (selection/impperial decree/family arrangement)
2. First enter harem (bullied/seek protector)
3. Gain favor (talent/personality/fate)
4. Make enemies (Empress/favorite's suppression)
5. Rise step by step (promotion/children)
6. Major crisis (framed/lost favor/family implicated)
7. Desperate counterattack (reveal truth/reverse)
8. Final ending (become Empress/leave palace/imperial harmony)
```

### Mansion Drama Framework
```
Marry in → Gain footing → Manage household → Crisis → Lead household

Detailed stages:
1. Marry in (marry up/marry down/rush wedding)
2. Mother-in-law confrontations (set rules/be made to suffer)
3. Sisters-in-law struggles (compete for resources/favor)
4. Husband relationship (cold/warm up/misunderstanding)
5. Household management rights struggle
6. Family crisis (investigated/fallen)
7. Turn the tide
8. Become family matriarch
```

### Political Intrigue Framework
```
Caught in → Take sides → Game → Decisive battle → New situation

Detailed stages:
1. Caught in political vortex
2. Choose faction/forced to take sides
3. Participate in planning
4. Multi-party gaming
5. Key intelligence/turning point
6. Final decisive battle
7. New pattern
```

## 3. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: First Enter Deep Palace/Mansion (Chapters 1-80, 20%)
- **Opening**: Palace entry/ marriage incentive
- **Characters appear**: Main forces, key supporting characters
- **Core task**: Gain footing, familiarize environment
- **Small climax**: First crisis resolved

### Volume 2: Advance Step by Step (Chapters 81-180, 25%)
- **Faction layout**: Recruit allies, deal with enemies
- **Emotional development**: Relationship with male lead warms up
- **Core conflict**: First round confrontation with main opponent
- **Climax**: Gain staged victory

### Volume 3: Changing Circumstances (Chapters 181-300, 30%)
- **Pattern expands**: Higher level gaming
- **Crisis deepens**: Framed/lost favor/family implicated
- **Dark moment**: Fall to lowest point
- **Turning point**: Key help appears

### Volume 4: Desperate Counterattack (Chapters 301-380, 20%)
- **Counterattack layout**: Collect evidence, unite allies
- **Truth reveal**: Main villain revealed
- **Final confrontation**: Direct confrontation
- **Settlement**: Enemies punished

### Volume 5: Satisfying Ending (Chapters 381-420, 15%)
- **Aftermath handling**: New pattern established
- **Emotional fulfillment**: HE with male lead
- **Side stories/daily life**: Happy life

## 4. Ancient Dialogue Standards

### Title Address System
```
Royalty:
- Emperor: Your Majesty, Your Highness, Imperial Majesty
- Empress: Empress, Mother Empress
- Imperial concubines: [X] Consort, Consort
- Imperial sons: [X] Highness, Prince

Officials:
- Officials: Grandee, [X] Duke
- Official's wife: Madam, [X] Clan
- Young miss: [X] Girl, Miss [X]

Commoners:
- Men: Young sir, [X] Lang
Women: Girl, [X] Niang
```

### Common Ancient Vocabulary
```
Addresses:
- I: Your subject, this body, this palace, your servant girl, this humble woman
- You: You/You, sir, respectfully
- He: That person, this person

Actions:
- Look: Glance, gaze, see
- Say: Speak, say, called
- Walk: Go, depart, head
- Come: Arrive, arrive at, come to

Descriptions:
- Beautiful: Topple cities, gorgeous, nation-ending beauty
- Smart: Wise, clever, ingenious
- Angry: Furious, enraged, thunderous fury
```

### Dialogue Examples
```markdown
【Pay respects】
"Your subject pays respects to Your Majesty, may Your Majesty live ten thousand years."
She bows gracefully, posture respectful but not servile.

【Confrontation】
"What the sister says, this sister dares not accept."
She covers her lips and smiles lightly, eyes revealing cold intent.

【Love talk】
"This world, I can discard. You, I cannot lose."
He pulls her into his arms, voice low and firm.
```

## 5. Classic Scene Bank

### Meeting Scenes
```
1. Life-saving grace: She saves him (doesn't know identity)
2. Accidental offense: Unintentionally offends noble
3. Glimpse: Unforgettable glance at some occasion
4. Childhood companions: Childhood playmates reunite
5. Substitute marriage/impersonation: Marry in place of someone
```

### Favor Competition Scenes
```
1. Stunning talent: One song/dance/poem shocks all
2. Wisely resolve difficult situation: Show wisdom at crisis moment
3. Credit for saving: Saved important person at key moment
4. Retreat to advance: Show weakness for sympathy
5. Use enemy's move against them: Counterattack with opponent's scheme
```

### Framing and Reversal
```
Framing methods:
- Plant evidence: Place forbidden items in room
- False accusation: Forge evidence/bribe witnesses
- Poison: Frame others for poisoning
- Sow discord: Drive wedges between ally/male lead

Reversal methods:
- Evidence: Key physical and witness evidence
- Ally: Testify at crucial moment
- Scheme: Lure snake out of hole, make opponent show flaw
- Noble backer: Protection from higher power
```

## 7. Historical Background Quick Reference

### Common Alternate Era Templates
```
Golden age template:
- Strong national power, flourishing culture
- Suitable for: Palace drama, sweet romance
- Reference: Tang Dynasty golden age, Qing Kang-Qian

Troubled times template:
- Frequent warfare, power changes
- Suitable for: Political intrigue, country and family
- Reference: Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms, Southern and Northern Dynasties

Decline template:
- Dynasty declining, internal and external troubles
- Suitable for: Tragedy, rebirth
- Reference: Late Ming, Late Qing
```
