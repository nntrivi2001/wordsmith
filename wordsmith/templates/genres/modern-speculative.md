# Modern Romance Brainwave Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Real relationships + Rules/anomalies + Emotional reversals. When supernatural rules invade modern love, how do feelings and rules coexist?

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F03
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Rule Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Romance constrained by some rule
- **Core Satisfaction**: Rule utilization, rule breaking, true heart overcomes rules
- **Representative Works**: 《Cong Jie Hun Kai Shi Lai Ai》《Qin Ai De Xiao Hai Zi》《Wo De Qian Ban Sheng》

### Contract Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Feelings starting from contract/transaction
- **Core Satisfaction**: Fake drama becomes real, contract reversal, genuine feelings revealed
- **Representative Works**: 《He Yi Sheng Xiao Mo》《Shan Shan Lai Le》《Wei Xiao Xiao Hen Qing Shen》

### Rebirth Revenge Flow
- **Feature**: Reborn to change fate, gain true love
- **Core Satisfaction**: Avoid traps seize opportunities, revenge success, true love accompanies
- **Representative Works**: 《Chong Sheng Zhi Ming Liu Ju Xing》《Shu Nu Gong Lue》《Chong Sheng Zhi Di Nv Huo Sha》

### Supernatural Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Modern romance with special abilities
- **Core Satisfaction**: Ability assists, identity secret, ability and love
- **Representative Works**: 《Du Xin》《Shi Jian Gui Hua Ju》《Guan Yu Wo Zhuan Sheng Bian Cheng Shi Lai Mu Zhe Jian Shi》

## 3. Rule and Emotion Design

### Rule Design Principles
```
Related to romance:
- Rules affect romance development
- Rules create romantic conflicts
- Rules test true feelings

Has loopholes:
- True heart can break through
- Specific conditions can release
- Cost can be borne

Has clear cost:
- Violation has punishment
- Breaking through has cost
- Choice has trade-off
```

### Emotion and Rule Conflicts
| Conflict Type | Performance | Resolution |
|---------------|-------------|------------|
| Rule prohibits | Cannot be together | Break rule |
| Rule forces | Must separate | Find loophole |
| Rule test | Choice question | Prove true heart |
| Rule cost | Being together has cost | Willing to bear |

### Cost System
| Cost Type | Feature | Romantic Meaning |
|-----------|---------|------------------|
| Memory cost | Forget the other | Heart-torturing |
| Ability cost | Lose ability | Sacrifice |
| Identity cost | Change identity | Choice |
| Time cost | Lose time | Treasure |

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Rule Breaking
- **Scene**: Break rule restrictions with genuine feelings
- **Satisfaction**: Love conquers all
- **Key**: Must have sufficient emotional foreshadowing

### Identity Revealed
- **Scene**: Hidden identity/ability revealed
- **Satisfaction**: Reversal surprise, slap faces everyone
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing

### Fate Rewriting
- **Scene**: Change original tragic fate
- **Satisfaction**: Avoid tragedy, gain happiness
- **Key**: Must have comparison

### True Heart Proof
- **Scene**: Prove true feelings through test
- **Satisfaction**: Moved, sweet, fulfilling
- **Key**: Test must have meaning

## 5. Outline Structure (800K Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Meeting (Chapters 1-50)
- **Core**: Meet because of rules/abilities
- **Relationship**: Strangers → Acquaintances
- **Climax**: Discover rules/abilities

### Volume 2: Getting to Know (Chapters 51-120)
- **Core**: Get along under rules
- **Relationship**: Acquaintances → Flirting
- **Climax**: Confirm feelings

### Volume 3: Test (Chapters 121-200)
- **Core**: Rule and feeling conflicts
- **Relationship**: Flirting → Crisis
- **Climax**: Face choice

### Volume 4: Breakthrough (Chapters 201-280)
- **Core**: Break rules, choose true heart
- **Relationship**: Crisis → Confirm
- **Climax**: Rule breakthrough

### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 281-350)
- **Core**: Lovers finally united
- **Relationship**: Confirm → Eternity
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 7. Balance of Reality and Brainwave

### Reality Foundation
```
Maintain reality feel:
- Modern city background
- Real interpersonal relationships
- Reasonable social rules

Brainwave elements:
- Supernatural rules
- Special abilities
- Fate arrangement
```

### Emotional Authenticity
| Aspect | Key Points | Pitfalls |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Emotion development | Gradual progress | Too fast, too abrupt |
| Character reaction | Match personality | Serve plot |
| Conflict resolution | Reasonable and credible | Too coincidental |
| Ending | Have foreshadowing | Force HE |

### Integration of Rules and Daily Life
- Rules must integrate into daily life
- Abilities must have usage limits
- Supernatural elements must have explanation

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Rules too fantastical
- ❌ Romance line loses authenticity
- ❌ Rules change arbitrarily
- ❌ Cost not fulfilled

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Rules integrated with reality
- ✅ Emotion development natural
- ✅ Rules self-consistent
- ✅ Cost clearly fulfilled
