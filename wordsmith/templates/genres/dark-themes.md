# Dark Themes Template

## Core Settings

### Essence of Dark Themes
```
Order collapse + New order establishment + Ultimate power + Moral gray area
```

### Core Attractions
- Ultimate satisfaction of power fantasy
- Forbidden pleasure of breaking conventions
- Survival law of the jungle
- Alternative perspective narrative experience

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M16
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## Dark MC Design

### MC Type Selection
| Type | Feature | Core Drive |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Tyrant type | Absolute rule, iron-blooded methods | Order and control |
| Conqueror type | Expansion ambition, never satisfied | Possession and conquest |
| Destroyer type | Destroy everything, rebuild world | Destruction and rebirth |
| Parasite type | Secret infiltration, covert manipulation | Survival and evolution |

### Personality Trait Template
```
Rational and cold + Absolute control + Ambitious + Lonely sufferer
```

### MC Origin Template
```
Abandoned by the world → Awaken dark power → Revenge/conquer → Establish new order
```

### MC Complexity
- Has own behavior logic
- Not purely evil
- Has understandable motives
- Occasionally shows humanity

## Plot Rhythm

### Opening (First 10 chapters)
- Dark power awakens
- First devour/conquest
- Establish basic force
- Establish behavior logic

### Development Period (Chapters 10-100)
- Force expansion
- Ability evolution
- Conquer/devour stronger opponents
- Establish new order

### Climax Period (Chapter 100+)
- Become top predator
- Conquer large areas
- Face ultimate enemy
- Establish dark empire

---

## Moral Boundaries

### Bottom Line Setting
- Clear MC's behavior logic
- Has own principles (even if dark)
- Not mindless killing
- Has clear goals and reasons

### Humanity Display
- Occasional soft moments
- Obsession with specific person/matter
- Inner loneliness and struggle
- Not completely devoid of humanity

---

## Precautions

- **Scale control**: Cannot be too bloody and violent (avoid being banned)
- **Logic self-consistency**: Dark doesn't mean stupid, MC must have IQ
- **Reader resonance**: Even dark MC must have understandable motives
- **Rhythm control**: Cannot be oppressively grim throughout, must have satisfaction release
- **Moral bottom line**: Avoid overly twisted three views, maintain basic moral bottom line
- **Characterization**: Dark MC must also have charm, cannot be purely evil
