# Period Drama Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Historical background + Life struggle + Emotional resonance. Writing the epic struggle of ordinary people in the currents of a specific era.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F12
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### 1960s-70s Era Stream
- **Feature**: Planned economy, rustication movement, material scarcity
- **Core Satisfaction**: Space hoarding, knowledge crush, improving life
- **Representative Works**: 《Chuan Yue Qi Ling Zuo Jun Fu》《Qi Ling Nian Dai Xiao Ri Zi》《Chong Sheng Liu Ling Hao Shi Guang》

### 80s-90s Era Stream
- **Feature**: Reform and opening up, starting businesses, era of change
- **Core Satisfaction**: Seizing opportunities, entrepreneurship wealth, era dividends
- **Representative Works**: 《Chong Sheng Ba Ling Zhi Jiao Qi Ru Xue》《Ba Ling Nian Dai Nu Shou Fu》

### Republican Era Stream
- **Feature**: Turbulent times, family rise and fall, love and hate
- **Core Satisfaction**: Survival in chaos, family rise, love perseverance
- **Representative Works**: 《Min Guo Da Jie Xu Shi》《Luan Shi Jia Ren》

### Early PRC Era Stream
- **Feature**: Rebuilding from scratch, construction boom, idealism
- **Core Satisfaction**: Technical contribution, construction achievements, era glory
- **Representative Works**: 《Chuan Yue Wu Ling Nian Dai Dang Jun Fu》

## 2. Worldview: Period Sociology

### 1960s-70s Era Characteristics
| Element | Feature | Impact |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Economy | Planned economy, ticket system | Material shortage |
| Politics | Frequent movements | Need to be cautious |
| Society | Collectivism | Individual subordinate to collective |
| Marriage | Organizational introduction | Emotion secondary |
| Work | Assignment system | Iron rice bowl |

### 80s-90s Era Characteristics
| Element | Feature | Impact |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Economy | Reform and opening | Opportunities emerge |
| Politics | Relatively relaxed | Can do business |
| Society | Concept change | Individual struggle |
| Marriage | Free love | Emotion primary |
| Work | Business tide | Break iron rice bowl |

### Resource System
- **Tickets**: Food tickets, cloth tickets, meat tickets (60s-70s)
- **Connections**: Relationships are the most important resource
- **Information**: Policy info, business opportunities
- **Skills**: Craftsmanship, knowledge, culture

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Space Hoarding
- **Scene**: MC has space, hoards supplies in advance
- **Satisfaction**: Others go hungry, MC eats well
- **Key**: Must hide well, can't be too conspicuous

### Knowledge Crush
- **Scene**: Using modern knowledge to solve problems
- **Satisfaction**: What others can't do, MC easily accomplishes
- **Example**: Using modern medical knowledge to save people

### Seizing Opportunities
- **Scene**: Using foresight to seize era opportunities
- **Satisfaction**: Early layout, earning first bucket of gold
- **Example**: Buying houses, stocks, starting businesses in advance

### Face-Slapping Antics
- **Scene**: Extreme relatives/neighbors bully MC
- **Satisfaction**: After MC prospers, the other party regrets deeply
- **Key**: Must have foreshadowing, can't be too deliberate

## 5. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Footing (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: Transmigrate/reborn, adapt to environment
- **Status**: Bottom
- **Climax**: Gain footing, improve life

### Volume 2: Development (Chapters 61-150)
- **Core**: Seize opportunities, start accumulating
- **Status**: Lower-middle
- **Climax**: Earn first bucket of gold

### Volume 3: Rise (Chapters 151-250)
- **Core**: Career development, status improvement
- **Status**: Middle
- **Climax**: Become local wealthy household

### Volume 4: Peak (Chapters 251-350)
- **Core**: Career peak, family fulfillment
- **Status**: Upper
- **Climax**: Reach life peak

### Volume 5: Completion (Chapters 351-400)
- **Core**: Children and grandchildren, enjoy later years
- **Status**: Top
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 7. Creating Period Feel

### Language Characteristics
- 60s-70s: "Comrade", "Revolution", "Serve the People"
- 80s-90s: "Go into business", "Ten thousand yuan household", "Iron rice bowl"

### Life Details
| Era | Clothing | Food | Housing | Transport |
|-----|----------|------|---------|----------|
| 60s-70s | Blue gray green | Coarse grain main | Tube building | Bicycle |
| 80s-90s | Bell-bottoms | Starting to diversify | Unit room | Motorcycle |

### Major Events
- 60s-70s: Rustication movement, College entrance exam resumed
- 80s-90s: Reform and opening up, Southern tour speeches, Hong Kong return

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Weak period feel, like modern text
- ❌ Politically sensitive content
- ❌ Cheat too obvious
- ❌ Plot drags

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Focus on period details
- ✅ Avoid sensitive topics
- ✅ Cheat must be hidden
- ✅ Pacing must be tight
