# Sci-Fi Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Tech settings + Ethical conflicts + Grand imagination. Exploring humanity's future and boundaries in the starry seas.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M18
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Cyberpunk Flow
- **Feature**: High tech low life, body modification, megacorporation rule
- **Core Satisfaction**: Hacker invasion, body upgrade, rebel against system
- **Representative Works**: 《Sai Bo Ying Xiong Zhuan》《2084》《Ji Xie Fei Sheng》

### Space Opera Flow
- **Feature**: Interstellar navigation, alien civilizations, cosmic warfare
- **Core Satisfaction**: Spaceship battles, alien exploration, civilization collision
- **Representative Works**: 《San Ti》《Yin He Di Guo》《Xing Ji Zhi Quan Neng Jin Hua》

### Hard Sci-Fi Flow
- **Feature**: Rigorous scientific principles, realistic tech details
- **Core Satisfaction**: Science puzzles, tech breakthroughs, logical deduction
- **Representative Works**: 《San Ti》《Huo Xing Jiu Yuan》《Qiu Zhuang Shao Dian》

### Apocalypse Sci-Fi Flow
- **Feature**: Tech disaster, humanity survival, civilization reconstruction
- **Core Satisfaction**: Desperate survival, tech salvation, human nature choices
- **Representative Works**: 《Liu Lang Di Qiu》《Mo Ri Le Yuan》

### Mecha/War Flow
- **Feature**: Giant mecha, interstellar war, hot-blooded battles
- **Core Satisfaction**: Mecha piloting, tactical command, ace pilot
- **Representative Works**: 《Shi Shi Chuan Shuo》《Ji Dong Feng Bao》《Chao Shen Ji Xie Shi》

## 2. Worldview: Sci-Fi Sociology

### Tech Levels (Kardashev Index Modified)
| Level | Energy Use | Civilization Feature |
|-------|-----------|---------------------|
| Type 0.7 | Partial planet | Contemporary Earth |
| Type 1 | Entire planet | Global unification |
| Type 2 | Stellar system | Dyson sphere |
| Type 3 | Galactic | Galactic empire |

### Social Forms
| Type | Feature | Conflict Source |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Corporate rule | Megacorporations hold power | Class oppression |
| Federation | Multi-planet alliance | Political gaming |
| Empire | Centralized | Imperial succession |
| AI rule | Artificial intelligence management | Human-machine contradiction |
| Anarchist | Border stars | Pirate infested |

### Power Structure
- **Federation/Empire**: Main regime, order maintainer
- **Corporate consortium**: Economic giants, secret manipulation
- **Resistance organizations**: Underground forces, pursue freedom
- **Alien civilizations**: Friendly/hostile/mysterious
- **AI forces**: Awakening artificial intelligence

## 3. Tech System Design

### Core Tech Tree
| Domain | Primary | Intermediate | Advanced |
|--------|---------|--------------|----------|
| Energy | Nuclear fusion | Antimatter | Vacuum energy |
| Propulsion | Ion engine | Warp drive | Wormhole jump |
| Weapons | Laser/EM | Particle beam | Stellar cannon |
| Defense | Energy shield | Force field | Space folding |
| Communication | Quantum comm | Faster-than-light | Consciousness link |
| Biology | Gene modification | Body modification | Consciousness upload |

### Body Modification Levels
- **Civilian grade**: Repair disabilities, enhance senses
- **Military grade**: Combat enhancement, built-in weapons
- **Experimental grade**: Break human limits, side effects
- **Taboo grade**: Complete mechanization, lose humanity

### Tech Costs (Important)
- **Energy consumption**: High tech needs lots of energy
- **Side effects**: Body rejection, gene collapse
- **Ethical issues**: Cloning, consciousness upload moral dilemmas
- **Tech out of control**: AI rebellion, weapon malfunction

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Tech Crush
- **Scene**: MC masters advanced technology
- **Satisfaction**: Use high-tech to crush low-tech enemies
- **Key**: Tech source must be reasonable (transmigrate/inheritance/genius)

### Mecha Awakening
- **Scene**: MC has special connection with mecha
- **Satisfaction**: Human-machine integration, combat power surges
- **Key**: Mecha must have uniqueness (AI/biological/ancient relic)

### Spaceship Command
- **Scene**: MC becomes captain, command battles
- **Satisfaction**: Tactical crush, win against odds
- **Key**: Show command talent, not brainless crush

### Civilization Contact
- **Scene**: First contact with alien civilization
- **Satisfaction**: Cultural shock, tech exchange, crisis resolved
- **Key**: Alien civilization must have uniqueness

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Awakening (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: MC obtains special ability/tech
- **Map**: Single planet/space station
- **Climax**: First show of strength, draw attention

### Volume 2: Rise (Chapters 81-180)
- **Core**: Join organization, establish position
- **Map**: Star system range
- **Climax**: Complete major mission, gain recognition

### Volume 3: Contend (Chapters 181-350)
- **Core**: Involved in faction struggles
- **Map**: Multi-star system
- **Climax**: Become core of a faction

### Volume 4: War (Chapters 351-550)
- **Core**: Participate in interstellar war
- **Map**: Galactic range
- **Climax**: Turn tide of war, reveal conspiracy

### Volume 5: Final (Chapters 551-700)
- **Core**: Face ultimate threat
- **Map**: Cosmic scale
- **Climax**: Save civilization/change cosmic pattern

## 7. Alien Civilization Design

### Civilization Types
| Type | Feature | Relationship with Humans |
|------|---------|------------------------|
| Humanoid | Similar appearance | Easy to communicate |
| Silicon-based | Completely different | Hard to understand |
| Cluster consciousness | Hive mind | Major conflict |
| Energy life | Non-material | Mysterious |
| Mechanical civilization | AI evolved | Can be enemy or ally |

### First Contact Protocol
- **Observation period**: Non-intervention, just observe
- **Testing period**: Limited contact, assess threat
- **Formal contact**: Establish diplomatic relations
- **Conflict handling**: War or peace

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Tech settings self-contradictory
- ❌ Terms piled up, readers can't understand
- ❌ Tech almighty, no cost
- ❌ Aliens too similar to earthlings

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Tech settings self-consistent
- ✅ Terms explained gradually
- ✅ Tech has clear limitations
- ✅ Aliens have uniqueness
