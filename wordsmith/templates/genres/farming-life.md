# Farming & Kingdom Building Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Resource accumulation + Faction building + Steady growth. "Build walls, store grain, delay claiming kingship" - conquer the world with management wisdom.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F12
- Universal Overlay: Pack U03

## Etymology Note

"Farming" originated from SLG strategy games, referring to players developing factions with "build walls, store grain, delay claiming kingship" as their motto. Farming novels are divided into two major streams:
- **Early farming novels**: Faction management, internal administration construction, technology development, using economic/tech advantages to overthrow opponents
- **Later farming novels**: Daily life, trivial matters, small person struggles

---

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Faction Management Stream (Early Farming)
- **Feature**: Establish base, develop agriculture/economy/military/politics, crush opponents with administrative advantages
- **Core Satisfaction**: Faction expansion, tech crush, conquer the world
- **Representative Works**: 《Zhui Xu》《Tang Zhuan》《Xin Song》《Zai Chi Tian Xia》《Guan Ju Yi Pin》

### Territory Building Stream
- **Feature**: Obtain territory/feud, build from scratch
- **Core Satisfaction**: Territory upgrade, population growth, economic prosperity
- **Representative Works**: 《Ling Zhu Shi Dai》《Cong Ling Kai Shi》《Wo Zai Yi Jie You Zuo Cheng》

### Base Development Stream
- **Feature**: Apocalypse/space/other world safe base construction
- **Core Satisfaction**: Base expansion, resource accumulation, team growth
- **Representative Works**: 《Mo Shi Zhi Wo You Kong Jian》《Xing Ji Nong Chang Zhu》

### Daily Life Stream (Later Farming)
- **Feature**: Ancient/near-modern background, ordinary people's plain life
- **Core Satisfaction**: Get rich, improve life, family harmony
- **Representative Works**: 《Nong Jia Xiao Fu Nu》《Jin Xiu Nong Nv Zhong Tian Mang》《Qing Chuan Ri Chang》

## 3. Faction Management System (Early Farming Core)

### Technology Tree Design
```
Agricultural technology:
Primitive farming → Iron tools → Irrigation → Crop rotation → Seed breeding

Industrial technology:
Manual workshop → Water machinery → Standardized production → Assembly line → Industrial revolution

Military technology:
Cold weapons → Bow improvement → Gunpowder weapons → Firearms artillery → Modern weapons

Commercial technology:
Barter → Currency circulation → Bank notes → Stock system → Financial system
```

### Talent System
| Type | Function | Acquisition Method |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| Strategist | Strategic planning | Recruit/surrender |
| Military general | Military command | Recruit/cultivate |
| Administration | Economic management | Imperial examination/recommendation |
| Craftsman | Technology R&D | Recruit/cultivate |
| Merchant | Trade management | Cooperate/recruit |

### System Building
- **Political system**: Centralization/feudalism/parliamentary system
- **Economic system**: Planned economy/market economy/mixed system
- **Military system**: Military tenant system / recruitment / draft system
- **Selection system**: Imperial examination/recommendation/assessment

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Tech Crush (Faction Management)
- **Scene**: Use advanced technology against backward forces
- **Satisfaction**: Dimensional strike, win with few against many
- **Example**: Use firearms against cold weapon armies

### Administrative Advantage (Faction Management)
- **Scene**: Enemies still fighting internally while MC has developed and grown
- **Satisfaction**: Accumulate slowly, one battle decides destiny
- **Key**: Must show development process

### Talent Gathering (Faction Management)
- **Scene**: All kinds of talents rally to MC
- **Satisfaction**: Talent gathering, everyone supports
- **Key**: Talents must have distinctive features

### Get Rich (Daily Life)
- **Scene**: From poor to rich
- **Satisfaction**: More money, better life
- **Key**: Must show accumulation process

### Face-Slapping Antics (Daily Life)
- **Scene**: Relatives/neighbors look down on MC
- **Satisfaction**: After MC prospers, the other party regrets deeply
- **Key**: Can't be too deliberate

## 5. Outline Structure

### Faction Management Type (2 Million Words Standard)

#### Volume 1: Footing (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: Obtain base, gain foothold
- **Faction**: One county/village
- **Climax**: Repel first wave of threats

#### Volume 2: Development (Chapters 81-200)
- **Core**: Internal administration building, accumulate strength
- **Faction**: One prefecture/region
- **Climax**: Tech/system initially effective

#### Volume 3: Expansion (Chapters 201-400)
- **Core**: Annex surroundings, expand influence
- **Faction**: Several states/region
- **Climax**: Become a regional hegemon

#### Volume 4: Hegemony (Chapters 401-600)
- **Core**: Compete for world domination
- **Faction**: Half the territory
- **Climax**: Decisive battle victory

#### Volume 5: Unification (Chapters 601-700)
- **Core**: Unify the world, establish new order
- **Faction**: World
- **Climax**: Enthrone/satisfying ending

### Daily Life Type (1 Million Words Standard)

#### Volume 1: Start (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: Separate household/transmigrate, obtain basic resources
- **Assets**: Few acres of land/shack
- **Climax**: First harvest, improve life

#### Volume 2: Development (Chapters 61-150)
- **Core**: Expand planting, start processing
- **Assets**: Dozen acres, small workshop
- **Climax**: Open sales channels, income doubles

#### Volume 3: Growth (Chapters 151-250)
- **Core**: Establish industry, hire workers
- **Assets**: Large farm, multiple workshops
- **Climax**: Become local wealthy household

#### Volume 4: Peak (Chapters 251-350)
- **Core**: Business empire, status upgrade
- **Assets**: Chain industry
- **Climax**: Obtain official recognition

#### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 351-400)
- **Core**: Family prosperity
- **Assets**: Aristocratic family
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 6. Farming System Design

### Agricultural System
| Type | Cycle | Profit | Risk |
|------|-------|--------|------|
| Grain | Long | Low | Low |
| Vegetables | Short | Medium | Medium |
| Economic crops | Medium | High | Medium |
| Medicinal herbs | Long | High | High |

### Processing System
- **Primary processing**: Sun-dry, salt, grind
- **Secondary processing**: Brewing, sugar-making, oil-pressing
- **Advanced processing**: Medicinal herb processing, specialty foods

### Economic Cycle
```
Production → Processing → Sales → Capital accumulation → Expanded reproduction
                ↓
           Technology upgrade → Efficiency improvement
```

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Waterlogged accounts, no conflicts
- ❌ Development too smooth, no setbacks
- ❌ Technology/systems don't match era
- ❌ Data unreasonable

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Has conflicts and setbacks
- ✅ Development has obstacles and costs
- ✅ Technology/systems gradual
- ✅ Data reasonable
