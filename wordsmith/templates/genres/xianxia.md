# Xianxia/Cultivation Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Defying fate + Immortal longevity + Ruthless laws. Cultivation is a battle against heaven and people alike.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M01 / Pack M02
- Universal Overlay: Pack U03

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Mortal Path
- **Feature**: MC has average talent, relies on scheming, caution, and cheat items to rise up.
- **Core Satisfaction**: IQ crush, accumulation, playing dumb to catch the tiger.
- **Representative Works**: 《Ren Xian Xian Xie Zhuan》

### Invincible Path
- **Feature**: Peak from the start (or has an invincible background/master).
- **Core Satisfaction**: Steamroll everything, conquer all who resist, break the rules.
- **Representative Works**: 《Di Ba》

### Clan Path
- **Feature**: MC leads their clan to rise, farming/management.
- **Core Satisfaction**: Collective ascension, resource snowball, power struggle.
- **Key**: Unity (reject internal strife, unite externally).

### Cautious Path
- **Feature**: Never get involved in karma, never stand under dangerous walls, max out defense.
- **Core Satisfaction**: Enemies all dead while I'm still alive, outlasting opponents is also victory.
- **Twist**: When forced to strike, shows overwhelming power (because preparation was too thorough).

---

## 2. Worldview: Cultivation Sociology

### Dark Forest Law
- **Limited Resources**: Spiritual energy, spirit medicines, ore veins are all scarce.
- **Kill and Plunder**: In the wilds when meeting unfamiliar cultivators, first reaction is "will he kill me", second is "can I kill him".
- **Possessing Treasure Without Power**: Having precious items without strength is a crime.

### Class Barriers
- **Lone Cultivators**: They are at the bottom of the cultivation world, fighting for a few spirit stones.
- **Sect Disciples**: Have organization, backing, look down on lone cultivators.
- **Noble Children**: Monopolize resources, bloodline inheritance.

---

## 3. Four Steps for Obtaining Opportunities

Don't let the MC find treasure directly. Must experience:

1. **Rumor**: Heard in taverns, auction leads, ancient text records.
2. **Exploration**: Enter secret realms/ruins, encounter traps, formations, demon beasts.
3. **Conflict**: Treasure emerges, multiple forces clash. MC muddles through or crushes with force.
4. **Reward**: Get treasure, immediately convert to strength (e.g., breakthrough on the spot).

---

## 4. Realm and Combat System

### Realm Division (Standard Version)
1. **Qi Refining**: Draw qi into body, spells begin to appear. (Lifespan 100)
2. **Foundation Building**: Build the foundation, sword flight possible. (Lifespan 200)
3. **Golden Core**: Swallow one golden core, my life is mine not heaven's. (Lifespan 500)
4. **Nascent Soul**: Break core into infant, if body destroyed can possess another. (Lifespan 1000)
5. **Transcension**: Comprehend laws, divine sense takes form. (Lifespan 2000)
6. **Void** → **Merging** → **Great Ascension** → **Tribulation**.

### Combat Power Inflation Control
- **Big Realm Suppression**: Foundation Building kills Qi Refining like killing chickens, cannot cross (unless has heaven-defying cheat).
- **Small Realm Gap**: Early stage killing later stage is hard, but equipment/talismans/formations can compensate.
- **Human Wave Tactics**: Low-level cultivators can't kill high-level no matter how many (AOE skills exist).

---

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Sect Storm (Chapters 1-100)
- **Tutorial**: Outer sect disciple competition, before Foundation Building.
- **Core Conflict**: Resource struggle, villain framings.
- **Climax**: First in sect competition, successful Foundation Building.

### Volume 2: Bloody Trial (Chapters 101-250)
- **Map**: Secret realm/ruins.
- **Core Conflict**: Multi-sect war, demon path invasion.
- **Growth**: Form Golden Core, obtain life weapon.

### Volume 3: Overseas/Central Region (Chapters 251-500)
- **Map Change**: Encounter pursuit and escape, or actively travel.
- **Core Conflict**: Lone cultivator's harsh survival, meet new companions.
- **Climax**: Form Nascent Soul, establish own power.

### Volume 4: Realm War (Chapters 501-1000)
- **Scale**: Human-demon two clan war, or righteous-demon war.
- **Status**: Become a major force, influence war situation.
