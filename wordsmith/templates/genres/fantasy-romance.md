# Fantasy Romance Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Otherworldly rules + Emotional sacrifice + Fateful reversal. Writing love that crosses races and fate in a fantastical world.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack F11
- Universal Overlay: Pack U01

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Xianxia Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Love and hate in the cultivation world
- **Core Satisfaction**: Cultivation couple together, trials overcome, three lives three worlds
- **Representative Works**: 《San Sheng San Shi Shi Li Tao Hua》《Hua Qian Gu》《Xiang Mi Chen Chen Jin Ru Ru》《Liu Li》

### Fantasy Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Fantastic love on an alternate continent
- **Core Satisfaction**: Contract binding, racial taboos, destined person
- **Representative Works**: 《Dou Luo Da Lu》《Dou Po Chong Tian Long》《Wu Dong Qian Kun》 (romance line)

### Western Fantasy Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Romance with Western fantasy background
- **Core Satisfaction**: Racial forbidden love, magical binding, kingdom marriage alliance
- **Representative Works**: 《Mu Guang Zhi Cheng》《Xue Gui Ren Shi》《Zhi Huan Wang》 (romance line)

### Mythical Romance Flow
- **Feature**: Love stories of deities and demons
- **Core Satisfaction**: Divine couple, human-divine forbidden love, reincarnation together
- **Representative Works**: 《Chen Xi Yuan》《Dong Gong》《Chang Xiang Si》《Yu Feng Xing》

## 2. Worldview: Fantasy Romance Sociology

### Racial System
| Race | Feature | Relationship with Humans |
|------|---------|------------------------|
| Divine race | Supreme existence | Cannot be desecrated |
| Immortal race | Long-lived | Aloof |
| Demon race | Practice and transform | Discriminated |
| Monster race | Naturally opposed | Hostile |
| Human race | Short lifespan | Foundation |

### Taboo Types
| Taboo | Reason | Cost |
|-------|--------|------|
| Racial forbidden love | Bloodline incompatible | Divine punishment |
| Master-disciple forbidden love | Ethical prohibition | Expelled from sect |
| Enemy families forbidden love | Opposing stance | Betray clan |
| Human-divine forbidden love | Lifespan difference | Fall into reincarnation |

### Binding Types
| Binding | Feature | Effect |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Contract | Soul binding | Life and death together |
| Blood pact | Blood connection | Sense each other |
| Destined | Heavenly ordained | Cannot escape |
| Reincarnation | Lifetime after lifetime | Bound to meet |

## 3. Power and Emotion System

### Power Levels
```
Mortal → Cultivator → True person → Immortal → Divine → Supreme God
```

### Relationship Between Emotion and Power
| Emotional State | Effect on Power | Plot Function |
|-----------------|----------------|---------------|
| Heart demon | Hinders breakthrough | Create conflict |
| Dual cultivation | Mutual gain | Feelings warm up |
| Love tribulation | Must experience | Test feelings |
| Sacrifice for love | Power explosion | Climax moment |

### Fate Design
```
Previous life cause:
- Bond of previous life
- Unfulfilled agreement
- Debt owed

This life result:
- Destined meeting
- Repeated choices
- Break fate
```

---

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Destined Meeting
- **Scene**: Fated meeting
- **Satisfaction**: Previous and this life, fate heaven-ordained
- **Key**: Must have previous life foreshadowing

### Forbidden Love
- **Scene**: Love crossing race/status
- **Satisfaction**: Fight against everything for love
- **Key**: Must have real obstacles

### Sacrifice for Protection
- **Scene**: Sacrifice everything for the other
- **Satisfaction**: Moved, heart-torturing, sweet
- **Key**: Sacrifice must have value

### Breaking Cocoon and Rebirth
- **Scene**: Reunion after trials
- **Satisfaction**: Bitterness turns to sweetness, HE ending
- **Key**: Must have enough pain

## 5. Outline Structure (1 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Meeting (Chapters 1-60)
- **Core**: Destined meeting, romance sprouting
- **Relationship**: Strangers → Acquaintances
- **Climax**: First heart flutter

### Volume 2: Getting to Know (Chapters 61-150)
- **Core**: Deepen understanding, feelings warm up
- **Relationship**: Acquaintances → Flirting
- **Climax**: Confirm feelings

### Volume 3: Trials (Chapters 151-250)
- **Core**: Taboos/obstacles appear
- **Relationship**: Flirting → Separation
- **Climax**: Forced to separate

### Volume 4: Protection (Chapters 251-350)
- **Core**: Fight against everything for love
- **Relationship**: Separation → Reunion
- **Climax**: Break taboos

### Volume 5: Fulfillment (Chapters 351-400)
- **Core**: Lovers finally united
- **Relationship**: Reunion → Eternity
- **Climax**: Satisfying ending

## 6. Character Design

### Female Lead Types
| Type | Feature | Interaction with Male Lead |
|------|---------|--------------------------|
| Innocent type | Simple and kind | Protected |
| Resilient type | Steadfast will | Fight side by side |
| Hidden identity type | Has secret | Identity reversal |
| Reincarnation type | Has past life memory | Search for truth |

### Male Lead Types
| Type | Feature | Attitude toward Female Lead |
|------|---------|---------------------------|
| Cold divine lord | Cold outside warm inside | Only gentle with her |
| Dark belly demon lord | Deep schemes | Secret protection |
| Gentle immortal lord | Gentle as jade | Silent dedication |
| Domineering emperor | Strong possession | Exclusive |

### Classic Supporting Characters
| Character | Function | Ending |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| Devoted backup | Create triangle | Support/darken |
| Evil female rival | Create conflict | Punished |
| Master/elder | Obstacle/support | Recognition |
| Loyal subordinate | Wingman | Also finds happiness |

## 7. Fantasy Element Design

### Worldview Elements
| Element | Function | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Divine artifact | Emotional token | Token of love |
| Forbidden zone | Key scene | Meet/separate |
| Heavenly tribulation | Test | Face together |
| Reincarnation | Fate | Three lives three worlds |

### Emotional Props
| Prop | Function | Plot Effect |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Forget-me-not water | Forget feelings | Heart-torture |
| Love knot | Sense each other | Sweet |
| Blood pact talisman | Life and death together | Binding |
| Reincarnation orb | Preserve memory | Reunion |

### Scene Design
```
Sweet scenes:
- Dual cultivation/healing
- Divine consciousness exchange
- Face heavenly tribulation together

Heart-torture scenes:
- Forced to separate
- Amnesia/misunderstanding
- Sacrifice for protection

Climax scenes:
- Break taboos
- Defy heaven
- Eternal together
```

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ Unclear rules
- ❌ Emotional progress stiff
- ❌ Pain meaningless
- ❌ Supporting characters tool-like

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ Clear self-consistent rules
- ✅ Emotional have foreshadowing
- ✅ Pain has value and reward
- ✅ Supporting characters have flesh and blood
