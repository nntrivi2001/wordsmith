# Realistic Fiction Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Realistic feel + Social resonance + Human depth. Root in realistic life, evoke strong reader immersion and emotional resonance.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M15
- Universal Overlay: Pack U02

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Workplace Struggle Flow
- **Feature**: Workplace leveling up, career and emotion in parallel
- **Core Satisfaction**: Counterattack to top, slap face difficult people, both career and love win
- **Typical Mode**: Small employee → Industry elite

### Marriage Life Flow
- **Feature**: Marriage family realistic portrayal, emotional entanglements
- **Core Satisfaction**: Scum man/woman gets retribution, true love comes, happy ending
- **Typical Mode**: Marriage crisis → Self awakening → New life

### Counterattack Growth Flow
- **Feature**: Bottom person struggle history, fate struggle
- **Core Satisfaction**: Bottom-up, prove self, achieve dream
- **Typical Mode**: Rock bottom → Rise → Peak

### Family Ethics Flow
- **Feature**: Family conflicts, blood ties, human nature test
- **Core Satisfaction**: Conflict resolved, family affection returns, healing heart
- **Typical Mode**: Conflict → Understanding → Reconciliation

## 2. Realistic Feel Anchoring Techniques

### Detail Realism
```
Scene details:
- Subway morning peak crowds
- Rental apartment smallness and clutter
- Late night overtime exhaustion
- Month-end anxiety repaying Huabei

Professional details:
- Industry jargon/terminology
- Work flow
- Workplace hidden rules
- Overtime culture
```

### Emotional Realism
```
Avoid:
❌ "He fell in love with her beautiful appearance"
❌ "She was attracted by his wealth"

Should:
✅ "Working until dawn, he silently placed that cup of hot coffee on the desk"
✅ "After arguing, discovered he had quietly paid this month's rent"
```

### Dialogue Realism
```
❌ Written style:
"I believe there are feasibility issues with this plan."

✅ Colloquial:
"This plan... feels a bit unreliable."

❌ Too literary:
"My heart is as if falling into the abyss."

✅ Realistic expression:
"At that moment, I felt empty in my heart, couldn't say a word."
```

## 3. Social Issue Handling

### Common Issues
| Issue | Entry Angle | Precautions |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| Workplace gender discrimination | Female lead encounters → Fights → Changes | Avoid preachiness, let story speak |
| Family of origin | Parents PUA → Awaken → Establish boundaries | Don't demonize parents, show complexity |
| Marriage predicament | Child-rearing neglect/mother-in-law conflicts | Don't sell anxiety, give outlet |
| Workplace involution | 996/layoffs/age discrimination | Have resonance also have hope |
| Appearance anxiety | Appearance evaluation → Self acceptance | Avoid "counterattack then slap face" single mode |

### Handling Principles
```
1. Present problems, don't amplify anxiety
2. Show predicament, also give outlet
3. Criticize phenomenon, don't attack group
4. Evoke resonance, deliver power
```

## 4. Outline Structure (600K Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Predicament Presentation (Chapters 1-50, 20%)
- **Opening hook**: Display MC's current predicament
- **Characters appear**: MC, opponents, allies, love interest
- **Core contradiction**: Workplace/marriage/family predicament
- **Small climax**: First attempt to break through (fail)

### Volume 2: Struggle Awakening (Chapters 51-130, 30%)
- **Conflict intensifies**: Predicament deepens, pressure increases
- **Cognition change**: MC begins awakening/changing
- **Ability accumulation**: Learning, growth, preparation
- **Turning point**: Key event pushes change

### Volume 3: Breakout Fight (Chapters 131-220, 30%)
- **Action unfolds**: Proactively take action, change status quo
- **Meet resistance**: Opposition forces obstruct
- **Help appears**: Key help arrives
- **Climax**: Decisive victory

### Volume 4: New Life (Chapters 221-280, 20%)
- **Harvest results**: Career/relationship fulfillment
- **Relationship reconciliation**: Reconcile with the past
- **New challenge**: Higher-level pursuit
- **Ending**: Open or satisfying ending

## 5. Character Depth Building

### Three-Dimensional Character Method
```
First dimension: External features
- Appearance, profession, social identity
- Behavioral habits, way of speaking

Second dimension: Inner personality
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Fears and desires
- Values and principles

Third dimension: Growth arc
- Starting state
- Change catalyst
- Ending state
```

### Gray Character Design
```
Antagonist not pure black:
- Mother-in-law makes things difficult, but does it for her son
- Boss suppresses, but is also oppressed
- Scum cheats, but the marriage itself had problems

Protagonist not pure white:
- Has selfish side
- Makes mistakes
- Needs to grow
```

## 6. Classic Scene Bank

### Workplace Scenes
```
Crisis:
- New person steals credit
- Supervisor makes things difficult
- Encounter sexual harassment
- Optimized at 35

Counterattack:
- Key project saves the day
- Switch jobs and crush former employer
- Entrepreneurship succeeds
- Expose workplace hidden rules
```

### Marriage Scenes
```
Crisis:
- Discover cheating evidence
- Mother-in-law war
- Child-rearing neglect
- Economic control

Awakening:
- Decide to divorce
- Economic independence
- Redefine self
- Meet true love
```

### Family Scenes
```
Conflict:
- Push marriage/push childbirth
- Favor siblings
- Parents PUA
- Economic disputes

Reconciliation:
- Understand parents' limitations
- Establish healthy boundaries
- Selective forgiveness
- Create new relationship model
```

## 8. Dialogue Examples

### Workplace Dialogue
```markdown
【Being Difficult】
"Rewrite this report." Supervisor throws file on the table.
"Where are the problems?" She holds back anger.
"I say there are problems there are, newbie don't ask so many."

【Counterattack】
"Manager Wang, this proposal was made by me, there are meeting minutes as record."
She speaks neither servile nor arrogant, the audience erupted in noise.
```

### Marriage Dialogue
```markdown
【Arguing】
"You only know work work work, do you manage this family?"
"Do you think mortgage and car loans fall from the sky?"
Two people silent, air solid as ice.

【Reconciliation】
"Sorry, shouldn't have yelled at you."
"I was wrong too, we... talk properly."
```
