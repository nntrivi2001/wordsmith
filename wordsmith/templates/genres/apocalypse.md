# Post-Apocalyptic Genre Template

> **Core Appeal**: Resource collapse + Survival gaming + Human nature choices. Rebuilding order on the ruins of civilization, finding hope in despair.

---

## Creative Constraints (Optional)
- Recommended Pack: Pack M20
- Universal Overlay: Pack U03

## 1. Core Sub-Genres

### Zombie Apocalypse Flow
- **Feature**: Virus outbreak, dead rise, humanity barely surviving
- **Core Satisfaction**: Kill zombies and level up, establish safe zone, save survivors
- **Representative Works**: 《Mo Shi Zhi Hei An Zhao Hui Shi》《Mo Shi Chao Ji Xi Tong》《Wo You Yi Zuo Mo Ri Cheng》

### Mutation Apocalypse Flow
- **Feature**: Spiritual energy awakening/radiation mutation, plants and animals evolve, humanity awakens abilities
- **Core Satisfaction**: Ability awakening, hunting mutant beasts, collecting evolution materials
- **Representative Works**: 《Mo Shi Zhi Shen Yuan Zhao Hui Shi》《Quan Qiu Jin Hua》《Wo De Mo Shi Ji Di Che》

### Natural Disaster Flow
- **Feature**: Natural disasters (ice age/drought/earthquake), extreme resource scarcity
- **Core Satisfaction**: Resource hoarding, shelter management, exploring ruins
- **Representative Works**: 《Mo Ri Zhang Lang》《Mo Shi Zhi Wo Shi Zhu Zai》

### Order Rebuilding Flow
- **Feature**: Post-disaster reconstruction, faction competition, establishing new civilization
- **Core Satisfaction**: Faction expansion, talent recruitment, system building
- **Representative Works**: 《Chong Sheng Zhi Mo Shi Xin Zhi Xu》《Mo Shi Zhi Hei An Yan Sheng》

---

## 2. Worldview: Apocalypse Sociology

### Resource Law
- **Food**: Hardest currency, people can kill for a bite to eat
- **Water**: Clean water more precious than gold
- **Medicine**: A bottle of antibiotics can exchange for a life
- **Fuel**: Control fuel controls mobility

### Human Nature Spectrum
- **Saint**: Holds bottom line, protects the weak (rare)
- **Ordinary person**: Follows the crowd, does good when possible
- **Gray zone**: Can do bad things for survival
- **Evil person**: Causes chaos in turmoil, law of the jungle

### Faction Types
| Type | Feature | Relationship with MC |
|------|---------|---------------------|
| Military remnants | Organized and disciplined | Can cooperate/can oppose |
| Survivor settlements | huddling for warmth | Can join/can establish |
| Raiders | Live by robbery | Main enemy |
| Cult organization | Apocalypse faith | Mid-to-late BOSS |
| Research institution | Find antidote/truth | Mission source |

## 3. Ability/Evolution System

### Awakening Levels
- **Level 1**: Body enhancement, 2-3 times stronger than ordinary people
- **Level 2**: Elemental emergence, can control single element
- **Level 3**: Elemental mastery, AOE attacks
- **Level 4**: Domain formation, small-range rule distortion
- **Level 5**: Legendary level, one person can destroy a city of zombies

### Ability Classification
| Type | Feature | Rarity |
|------|---------|--------|
| Power type | Brute force crush | Common |
| Speed type | Mobile and flexible | Common |
| Elemental type | Fire/ice/thunder/wind | Relatively rare |
| Psychic type | Control/perception | Rare |
| Space type | Storage/teleport | Extremely rare |
| Healing type | Restore injuries | Extremely rare |

### Evolution Materials
- **Crystal core**: Obtained from mutant creatures, level corresponds
- **Evolution potion**: Produced by research institutions, risk and reward coexist
- **Special food**: Mutant plant fruits, probability of awakening

## 4. Classic Satisfaction Formulas

### Material Hoarding
- **Scene**: Before/during early apocalypse, MC frantically hoards goods
- **Satisfaction**: Others go hungry, MC eats well
- **Key**: Must have reasonable hoarding reason (reborn/foresight/space)

### Base Building
- **Scene**: Find a piece of land, build from scratch
- **Satisfaction**: From ruins to fortress, from alone to team
- **Key**: Show building process, have sense of accomplishment

### Rescuing and Recruiting
- **Scene**: Save stranded survivors
- **Satisfaction**: Gain loyal followers, team grows
- **Key**: Rescued person must be useful (skills/resources/connections)

### Faction Crush
- **Scene**: Small faction provokes, doesn't know MC's strength
- **Satisfaction**: One person destroys a gang, deters all directions
- **Key**: Must have enough foreshadowing, can't brainless crush

## 5. Outline Structure (2 Million Words Standard)

### Volume 1: Survival (Chapters 1-80)
- **Core**: Apocalypse arrives, MC awakens, difficult survival
- **Map**: City ruins
- **Climax**: First kill of high-level mutant, obtain key ability

### Volume 2: Foothold (Chapters 81-180)
- **Core**: Establish/join base, gain foothold
- **Map**: Base surroundings
- **Climax**: Repel large-scale zombie tide/hostile force

### Volume 3: Expansion (Chapters 181-350)
- **Core**: Faction expansion, annex surroundings
- **Map**: Regional range
- **Climax**: Become regional hegemon

### Volume 4: Contending (Chapters 351-550)
- **Core**: Game with other major factions
- **Map**: Cross-regional
- **Climax**: Unify large region/discover apocalypse truth

### Volume 5: Final Stage (Chapters 551-700)
- **Core**: Solve apocalypse root/establish new order
- **Map**: National/global
- **Climax**: Humanity regains hope

## 6. Team Configuration

### Core Team
| Role | Position | Ability | Function |
|------|----------|---------|----------|
| MC | Leader | Comprehensive/special | Decision core |
| Warrior | DPS | Power/element | Front-line combat |
| Assassin | Scout | Speed/concealment | Intelligence gathering |
| Medic | Support | Healing | Team sustainability |
| Logistics | Management | Non-combat | Base operation |

### Team Development
- **Early**: 3-5 person squad, flexible and mobile
- **Mid**: 20-50 person base, clear division of labor
- **Late**: Hundreds of person faction, hierarchical management

## 7. Base Building System

### Base Elements
| Element | Function | Upgrade Direction |
|---------|----------|------------------|
| Defense works | Resist invasion | Wall → electric fence → energy shield |
| Living area | House population | Tent → board house → building |
| Agricultural area | Food output | Vegetable garden → greenhouse → hydroponics |
| Industrial area | Equipment manufacturing | Manual → mechanical → automated |
| Research institute | Tech upgrade | Lab → research center |

### Resource Cycle
```
Outgoing exploration → Obtain resources → Base building → Strength improvement → Explore further
```

---

## 8. Common Pitfalls

### Must Avoid
- ❌ MC is too merciful, saves people without bottom line
- ❌ Resource acquisition too easy, lose apocalypse feel
- ❌ Only write fighting monsters, not human nature
- ❌ Late stage invincible, lose tension

### Recommended Approach
- ✅ MC has bottom line but not too merciful
- ✅ Resources always tight, need trade-offs
- ✅ Human nature conflict is core highlight
- ✅ Always have stronger threats
